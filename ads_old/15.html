What is Personal Pickup?


Simply place your order on the Apple Online Store, then pick it up at your favorite Apple Retail Store. When you arrive, we'll help you set it up, so you'll be up and running before you leave the store.


  • Choose the "Pick up" option when you check out, then select an Apple Retail Store near you.
  • Products "Available today" can usually be picked up within an hour of placing your order.
  • You'll receive a notification email when your order is ready to be picked up.
  • You can pick up the order yourself, or have someone else pick it up for you.
