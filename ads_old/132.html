Influence excellence as
an educator.

At University of Phoenix, we're committed to helping you act on your passion to teach, or take your skills as an educator to the next level.

Our education curricula offer:

  • Programs aligned to national and state standards
  • Online and on-campus formats (where available)
  • Experienced P-12 teacher and administrator faculty
  • Real-world application through field experiences and virtual simulations

Earn an accredited
education degree.

College of Education programs are nationally accredited by the Teacher Education Accreditation Council (TEAC), and we also offer state-approved programs.

Education degrees include:

  • Bachelor of Science in Education/Elementary Teacher Education
  • Bachelor of Science in Liberal Studies (for California only)
  • Master of Arts in Education/Elementary Teacher Education
  • Master of Arts in Education/Secondary Teacher Education
  • Master of Arts in Education/Administration and Supervision
