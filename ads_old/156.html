JustHost.com bills itself as the last host you will ever need. Even a cursory glance at their site mkes it easy to see why this statement is true. It is not often that you find a web hosting company which is truly dedicated to customer service and includes tons of features.

Hosting Plans:

Even though JustHost offers a shared hosting plan, you still have an unlimited amount of disk space and bandwidth. Actually, many of the most important features are unlimited. This includes the number of domains, MySQL databases, email accounts and more. Another great thing about the service is that they use the incredibly easy cPanel software as your web interface. This has become the industry standard and you are probably familiar with it already (if not, it is as easy as using a word processor). Customers also receive a no-questions asked, anytime money back guarantee.

Since the standard plan includes an unlimited amount of space, traffic and domains, there is no need to complicate matters by adding additional packages. Most customers also really enjoy many of the extra features. The customizable templates and the drag and drop site editor are great tools for easily getting your site ready. Full integration with a number of e-commerce shopping carts makes it quick and easy tp start collecting sales and income. If you want to add a blog or a forum onto your site, no problem! There are all kinds of plug-ins and scripts included which can make this happen.

Pricing Concerns:

One of the best ways to tell if you are dealing with a top hosting company is by examining their pricing plans. For example, Just Host does not offer anything confusing. You do not need to choose which plan is best (usually by guessing) or how much data you think you will need. Additionally, you will not be charged extra fees for anything. There are no per transaction fees and no set-up fees, unlike many other web host providers. What you see here is really exactly what you get! Great features, tools and customer service for one simple, low price.

Customer Support:

If you ever have a query or concern, you will be able to find assistance. Just Host takes their commitment to customer satisfaction seriously. Customers are offered a number of ways to reach help. These include a responsive email support team (accessed through an online form), telephone support and even a live chat. All of the representatives are helpful, friendly and very knowledgeable.

If this were not enough, there are even more resources available for help. There is a helpful FAQ section right on the site and also some excellent tutorials.

Reliability:

As a first class web host, you will be happy to see a 99.9% up-time guarantee. This makes sure that your customers can access your site and place their orders. Each site also includes an SSL certificate, essential today for handling online credit transactions. It is also something which will help your customers to feel safe and more confident about placing their orders. Your site will be backed-up regularly and there are several different types of back-up systems to ensure your site will never go down. The servers are top of the line, dual quad processors, housed in a secured, state of the art facility.

Ease of Use:

Everything about using JustHost is easy. The site builder is a very familiar interface and accessing all of your information and data is also simple. The software even walks you right through the process of building the site and then there are tutorials to answer any further questions you might have.

Bottom Line:

If you are looking for a feature-rich and low cost web host solution, look no further than JustHost. Everything is straightforward and you will be impressed at how well it all works.
