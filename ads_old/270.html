In-Store Express Refills

If you've filled a prescription and picked it up at your neighborhood Walgreens pharmacy, then in-store express refills
may be right for you.

Just enter the prescription number found on the label, then choose the time, date and location and you're finished.
We can notify you via email or send a text message when your prescription is ready for pickup.

And for even faster service, create a Walgreens.com pharmacy account and use a host of tools designed to
keep you moving.

You can use in-store express refills if the prescription number is from your last refill picked-up in-store.

Enter Prescription Number(s)

Don't know your prescription numbers? Refill from Account

(e.g. 1234567-12345)
  See example



Enter your email address if you would like to be notified when your prescription is ready for pickup. If we already have one on file for you, an email will be sent and you can skip this step.


We don't rent or sell your information.
