The voice of Australian small business

Thanks for dropping by. We’d love you to join our research panel, where you’ll get to share your opinions and earn a little cash for your time.

With over two million small businesses operating in Australia, we are the largest business sector and corporate Australia wants to know our views. Hence the arrival of Micro Business Research.

Organisations and brands realise the value of customer feedback, and by tapping in to communities like Flying Solo (who along with Cint.com are the people behind this website), they can access the views of micro business owners and so get a proper grasp on our needs and priorities.

Joining Micro Business Research enables you to share your opinion online and be heard by businesses who want to serve you better. You’ll also benefit from small cash incentives.
