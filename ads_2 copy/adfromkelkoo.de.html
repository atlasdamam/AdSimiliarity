Welcome to Airwell Oil & Gas Pty Ltd

Airwell Oil & Gas posses a revolutionary technology for the de-liquefying of gas wells, this method also excels when utilised for coal bed methane de-watering or enhanced oil recovery from shallow stripper wells.

Airwell Oil & Gas fully developed and patented this pumping technology, these products are available either by purchase from Airwell Oil & Gas or available through their contract pumping service.

This unique technology makes it possible for Airwell to offer features to the Oil & Gas industry that are currently not available from other service companies using existing pumping technology.


Other associated Sub-Operating Services are also available.

Features of the Airwell Oil & Gas Pumping Equipment:

  • Optimizing production from high to low flow wells.

  • Multiple wells pumped from a centralized power supply.

  • Remote monitoring of pumping systems available.

  • The standard pump unit can be run in on Airwell’s ¾” API production pipe or on coiled tubing. Both options provide speed and convenience of installation with the coiled tubing option allowing the pumping unit to be installed and retrieved on a live well.

  • Low environmental impact both visually and in operation.

  • Very resilient to silt and grit.

  • Resilient to freezing.

  • Low maintenance requirements with negligible levels of down well maintenance.
