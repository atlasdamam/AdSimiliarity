Get treated like an insider at the airport and beyond
with the MileagePlus Explorer Card*:

  • Check your first bag for free

    Save up to $100 per roundtrip. Primary cardmembers and one companion on the same reservation can each check their first bag free on United-operated flights when purchasing tickets with their Explorer Card.

    Primary cardmember is the cardmember that originally applied for the card and is financially responsible for charges.

  • Enjoy priority boarding privileges

    Primary cardmembers and their companions on the same reservation will board United-operated flights after Premier members and prior to general boarding.

  • No foreign transaction fees†

    Purchases made with your Explorer Card outside the U.S. will not be subject to foreign transaction fees.

  • Visit the United Club with two one-time-use passes every year

    Take some time to relax before your flight with two one-time-use passes each year to the United Club. Enjoy complimentary beverages, snacks, and amenities like private meeting rooms and workspaces, free Wi-Fi and more.

  • Earn double miles on United ticket purchases

    Earn two MileagePlus miles for each $1 spent on tickets purchased from United and 1 mile per $1 on all other purchases.

  • Your miles don’t expire

    Your miles don’t expire as long as you are a Cardmember, with no limit to the number of miles you can earn.

  • Use miles for any seat, any time, on any United flight

    Primary cardmembers can use their miles to book any available seat, any time on any United-operated flight when they redeem miles at the MileagePlus Standard award level. No limitations, no restrictions and no blackout dates on available seats if you provide your MileagePlus number before starting the award booking process.

  • Receive room upgrades and special amenities

    Enjoy room upgrades, complimentary daily breakfast for two, early check-in, late checkout and other amenities at more than 700 luxury hotels and resorts worldwide.
