Regional Silver ADDY® for Bloggie's video site!

Caviar over lobster. That's how this win feels to us. After snagging 7 local awards in this year's Swinging Addys, Media 8 garnered a Silver ADDY® for its work for Sony's Bloggie website. This video based website was developed as part of a 360° launch campaign that included print ads, in-store demo videos, PoP material, posters, BTL, display media and a social media promotion (all developed and produced by Media 8).

The ADDY® Awards honor advertising's best and brightest for their creative excellence. With over 60,000 national annual entries, it is the single largest advertising competition in the world. We are proud to share this win with our long-standing client, Sony America Latina.

The all-inclusive digital agency.

What do Ocean's Eleven, the iPod, Wikipedia and Media 8 have in common?

They all saw the light of day in 2001. Since then, we have grown from a small 3-people-working-in-shorts shop to a full service digital agency with offices in Miami, México, Buenos Aires and Córdoba.

While others only lead creatively, we strive in every aspect of the marketing cycle. Our mix of research, strategy, creativity and technology allows us to create engaging user experiences that make your brand irresistible to users no matter where they interact with it.
