JoJoo Puzzle

Jo and Joo offer you to play puzzle. Together with them, you will learn to collect puzzles ranging from 1 to 25 pieces. Collect pictures quickly and get more stars and points for that.

The child in this fun game will learn to recognize objects by shape and color. This game allows the child to develop through play: creative thinking and motor skills of hands, attention and reaction.
