Event Information

Formula 1 racing is considered one of the most exciting motorsport events in the world. Combine the world’s top drivers with some of the most technologically-advanced machines reaching speeds up to 220 mph, and you get an event that’s nothing short of exhilarating. But the United States Grand Prix is more than a race, it’s one of the world’s top motorsports events featuring live entertainment from nationally-recognized artists at the spectacular outdoor entertainment venue.

Described as “one of the best, if not the best Grand Prix” of last season by race winner Lewis Hamilton, Circuit of The Americas hosted F1’s successful return to the United States last November. The 2012 FORMULA 1 UNITED STATES GRAND PRIX attracted 117,429 fans on race day and 265,499 over the three-day event. Don’t miss out on what’s sure to be an even better event in 2013!
