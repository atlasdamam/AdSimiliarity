    Owner Reviews

    CarAdvices expert Reviews are great, but sometimes you just want to hear it straight from the horses mouth. In our Owner Reviews section, we ask genuine new car owners to rate their car and share their experience with you.

    Youll get an honest appraisal of what its like to own the car on a daily basis. Car owners provide a star rating according to their opinion of their cars performance, so you can get a sense of what to expect before you buy.

    Owner reviews are unbiased, genuine reviews of new cars that you can really trust, because theyre written by the very people who experience the car, day in, day out.

    And if you go ahead and buy a new car? Then please, do us a favour - come back and rate your new car for other potential new car buyers.

    2012 JEEP GRAND CHEROKEE LAREDO REVIEW

    2013 NISSAN NAVARA ST-X 550 REVIEW
     
    The ST-X 550 Blackline: looked at one liked it now own one. Plastic interior is a bit so-so. Generaly a nice vehicle, however I dont understand why in this day and age auto manufacturers produce mid price range vehicles with drum brakes. I would think 4xdisc would inprove its braking power.

    2011 HYUNDAI i30 cw SX 1.6 CRDi REVIEW
     
    Having driven wife's 2010 diesel 5 spd manual hatch I waited for 2011 wagon upgrade with 6 spd manual and bluetooth. Excellent family car, we fit 2 adult kids and a large dog and tow a cage trailer. Great economy, very well appointed for the base model and super reliable. Comfy on long trip and surprisingly good to drive. Gutsy for a 1.

    2006 BMW X5 3.0d REVIEW
     
    I waited most of my life to be able to have this driving experience. Comfort goes hand in hand with manoeuvrability, performance and style. As a woman I feel great safety and confidence with handling and driving this car, especially in the dirt.

    2009 BMW 1 25i REVIEW
     
    I have recently sold my 125i, and to date it has been the best car I have owned. When I bought it new in 2009 I was looking for an affordable, small rear-wheel drive car. I didn't want a convertible, which ruled out the MX-5, so I was left with the option of either the 125i or a Mazda RX-8.

    2011 VOLVO S60 T4 REVIEW
     
    Ok. I have rated this car pretty high. But you need to understand I looked for a car to fit my purpose. This one came top. Price: For the base model (T4) which I bought, the value is mind boggling. About the only thing I really wanted that didn't come standard was the navigation.

    2012 HYUNDAI iX35 ELITE (AWD) REVIEW
     
    I own a 2012 model, after waiting 4 months for delivery because of my colour choice. The two things I find annoying are the front pillars are to wide, I find myself trying to duck and weave to look around it at intersections.     2012 HONDA ACCORD VTi LIMITED EDITION REVIEW
     
    Extremely uncomfortable front seats - like concrete blocs. Also, design issue the back part dips too low and there is no sufficient lumbar support. There are many drivers complaints on the internet about this problem with Honda Accord. The seat caused me so much back pain I had to sell the car back to the dealer at great financial loss.

    2002 HONDA ACCORD VTi-L REVIEW
     
    I traded my 2007 Honda Jazz down to one of these as it was simply too small for a growing family, plus my wife only has an automatic license. For a used buy, a sixth generation Accord (98-02) represents fantastic value. The VTi-L in my opinion is the pick.

    2012 HOLDEN CRUZE SRi V REVIEW

    2011 HYUNDAI ACCENT ACTIVE REVIEW
     
    I have a Hyundai Accent Diesel. The ESP off light has come on - the garage is unable to fix it. I never know if the safety feature is one or off. This is a problem to me when I travel on the highway. Otherwise I am happy with the car.

    2012 SUBARU XV 2.0i-S REVIEW
     
    Had my white pearl XV since 1/12 and have not been disappointed apart from expected standard features eg auto dim mirror, rain sensing wipers. Love the CVT and not short on power for me unless down low. Beautiful ride and excellent economy (came down from a Forester XT Premium). Has a lot of B's and W's but lacks some that I'd use.

    2012 SKODA YETI 103 TDI REVIEW
     
    Character, fun to drive, superbly built, engagingly clever design, supremely practical. After 40 years of magic carpet Citroen ride - the charactered, charm, fun & practicality tempted me into Yeti land. Very pleased with my purchase after 500 kms in my first week.

    2007 MITSUBISHI OUTLANDER LS REVIEW
     
    The drive in both city and country is very comfortable. Storage is spacious for camping gear and sundries. Find it a bit thirsty on country trips. The fuel gauge is very unreliable which is very unsettling on country trips. Suttons say this is a Mitsubishi fault and can't be fixed. I tend to think it is a computer fault and can be fixed.

    2013 NISSAN X-TRAIL ST REVIEW
     
    It may not be the best looking compact SUV out there. But it is it has lots of room, beautiful to drive and is value for money. On the highway it averages 8.6 litres for 100km, in town driving it is averaging about 9.5 litres every 100 km. The engine is very quiet even when travelling on the highway at 100km.
