WHAT IS COOLSCULPTING®?

CoolSculpting is a non-invasive procedure that literally freezes your fat away. It is FDA approved and is done with no incisions, which means no scars. It’s one of the newest and best ways to get rid of those pesky pounds that have been bothering you for years.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Developed by Harvard scientists, CoolSculpting® non-surgically targets the fat cells in your body that give you those stubborn lumps and bumps.
