    Nedbank MoneyTrader (short term)

    Some investments lock you in at an interest rate for the long term. Others give you more flexible access, but with lower returns. Nedbank MoneyTrader offers the best of both worlds – allowing investors to save smartly.

    The benefits of Nedbank MoneyTrader at a glance

    • A great rate linked to money market conditions.
    • Access your funds on a minimum of 24 hours’ notice, with your capital fully guaranteed.
    • No fees and commissions.*
    • Available for a limited time only.
    • Exclusively for individuals and small businesses.

    How Nedbank MoneyTrader works

    • You’ll need a minimum initial deposit of R50 000. There is no maximum limit.
    • Additional deposits and withdrawals are subject to a minimum amount of R5 000.
    • Interest rates can fluctuate on a daily basis, as they are linked to money market conditions.
    • The interest rate applies regardless of your balance. Interest is calculated daily, and may be paid out or capitalised monthly – provided a minimum balance of R50 000 is maintained.
    • MoneyTrader can be linked to an affinity of your choice, at no additional cost to you.

    * Terms and conditions apply.

    The Nedbank EasyAccess Deposit offers the guaranteed returns of a fixed deposit combined with free access to up to 50% of your funds on a minimum of 24 hours’ notice.

    Features

    • Your capital is secure.
    • No fees are charged.
    • A great interest rate is guaranteed for an investment term of 3, 6, 12 or 18 months.
    • 50% of the original capital can be withdrawn on a minimum of 24 hours’ notice.

    • Your rate is not affected if a portion of your funds is withdrawn.
