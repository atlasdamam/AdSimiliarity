Dermatology Urgent Care & Tuesday Walk-In Clinic

Do you have an abscess, open wound, inflamed cyst, itchy burning skin or other skin problem that you need looked at today?

Why wait when you can be seen today?

At IVSI, we believe that dermatologic conditions are as important as other health related issues; therefore, patients receive immediate professional attention. We are proud to be the only dermatology urgent care in the Park Ridge area.

In many cases, if you are wheeling to wait a bit, certain procedures and most of laser treatments can be done on the spot!
