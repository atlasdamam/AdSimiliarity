Interact with MotionSense

Roll over each hotspot to trigger the flow of water and learn more about MotionSense™

Wave Sensor Pass your hand across the top of the faucet to activate water flow. Wave again to turn it off.

Ready Sensor Simply move your hands below the spout to start the flow of water. Pull away and the water stops.

Handle Use the handle any time, or with hands-free mode when you want to adjust the water temperature and flow.
