Graduate School of Management

Welcome to Marquette University Graduate School of Management.

For 100 years the College of Business Administration has educated socially responsible business and community leaders. The College began offering graduate business education with our Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree in 1951; since that time the number of graduate programs has grown to include an Executive MBA, Masters programs in Accounting, Applied Economics, and Human Resources, a certificate program in Entrepreneurship as well as joint MBA programs with Law, Political Science and Nursing. The Graduate School of Management, established in 2006, administers all of our graduate business programs and is home to over 4,500 alumni worldwide. 

All of our graduate programs are taught by dedicated faculty members ready to challenge, support, and mentor you through the curriculum.  The programs have the flexibility to allow students to work full-time while completing the degree on a part-time basis. This allows our students to take what they learned in the classroom immediately back to work.  You are also associated with peers that form a great networking group throughout your time in the program and beyond. What if you want to complete the degree at a more accelerated pace? Our programs allow students to go full-time through any part of or the entire program; you decide on your pace but you are still part of the network of fellow students and working professionals. What are you waiting for?  Separate your resume from the pile with a graduate degree from the Graduate School of Management at Marquette University.
