Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Master’s Degree – Online

Learn how to launch your tech-based business idea.

If you’ve got a concept for a product or service within the tech or creative industries, learn the skills and strategies to develop, finance, and market your venture. Whether you’re interested in launching a brand new business or want to generate ideas for a large company, Full Sail University’s online Innovation & Entrepreneurship master’s degree program can show you how to channel your innovative spirit. You’ll explore the ideation process to recognize and build out your ideas, and gain an understanding of financial, legal, and distribution issues. Ultimately, you’ll develop a complete business plan and learn how to pitch to potential investors.
  • Ideation & the Creative Process
  • Research & Development
  • Digital Marketing Strategies
  • Business Planning in an Evolving Market
  • Financing Entrepreneurial Ventures
Specialized courses include:
  • Entrepreneurial Finance
  • High-Tech Entrepreneurship
  • Business Model Innovation
  • Product Design and Development
  • Market Strategies
  • Market Research
