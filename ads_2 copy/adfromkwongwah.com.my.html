Our state-of-the-art, 80,000-square-foot lab is designed for newborn stem cell banking. We continuously invest in technology and processes so, when families bank with CBR, they know their stem cells are stored in a leading-edge facility.

Ultimate use of newborn stem cells will be determined by the treating physician who will consider if they are applicable for the condition and should come from the patient or a suitable donor (like an HLA-matched sibling).
