Welcome

Thank you for clicking on our MetService link.

As New Zealand's leading rural insurer we understand the importance of weather to not only those on the land but to all New Zealanders in our everyday lives. It's this understanding of heartland Kiwis that sets us apart from your average insurer.

We invite you to look around our site and encourage you to contact us for a chat; you'll see for yourself how refreshingly straight up we are.

FMG also provides rural, business, lifestyle, household and personal insurances, including life and income protection, to over 55,000 clients across 30 offices nationwide. Click on the links below to learn more.
