    Keep in Touch with BlackBerry® Hub

    Seamlessly access your messages, email and social media updates in one place, and respond instantly without leaving your inbox.

    BlackBerry 10 Keyboard Adapts to You

    The virtual keyboard adjusts to your personal typing style. It learns how you type and where your fingers hit the keys, calibrating accordingly so you dont miss a key. Next-word suggestions are based on past messages and are easily flicked into place on this smartphone.

    Discover and share the best of the web with the speedy BlackBerry® Browser. Web pages load quickly, with support for elements such as Flash and HTML5. You can tap a share button to send a link through email or post it to your favorite social networks.
