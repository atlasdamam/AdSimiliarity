Get Informed About Key Dates

April 15, 2013
Save your tax information. It will be used to determine whether you are eligible for funds to help pay for health insurance.

August 1, 2013
Gather personal information like current insurance and expected insurance needs along with household income.

September 1, 2013
Only one month to go before open enrollment to purchase health insurance on a health insurance exchange.

October 1, 2013
Open enrollment through the health insurance exchanges begins and continues through March 31, 2014.

December 15, 2013
Final day to purchase health care insurance coverage on a health insurance exchange that will be effective on January 1, 2014.

March 31, 2014
Final day for open enrollment through the health insurance exchanges.
