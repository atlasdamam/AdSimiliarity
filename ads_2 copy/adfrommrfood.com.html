Refrigerators
Share on facebook Share on twitter Share on email

KitchenAid® refrigerators have everything you need to keep your food and beverages fresh—from freestanding refrigerators with a state-of-the-art food preservation system to built-in models with the widest usable refrigeration space available.

Unique Features: Preserva™ Food Care System

  • Sequential Dual Evaporators

    This independent cooling system prevents the freezer’s air from mixing with the air in the refrigerator, creating the perfect environment to keep food fresh.

  • FreshFlow™ Produce Preserver

    Delays over-ripening by absorbing ethylene gas, which can help keep foods like lettuce fresh up to four days longer.*

  • FreshFlow™ Air Filter

    15 times more powerful than baking soda, this feature absorbs aromas from the refrigerator, preventing unwanted odor transfer from one food to another.
