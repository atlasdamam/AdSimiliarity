JN Money Services Limited (JNMS), a fully-owned subsidiary of the Jamaica National Building Society (JNBS), was incorporated in December 2005 to manage the remittance operations of the Society, and to provide a suite of products and services, including money transfers and bill payments.

Under its distinct JN Money Transfer brand, the company has a strong network of over 350 branches and Agents spanning North America, the Caribbean, the United Kingdom and Ghana in Africa, where JN Money Services operates under the Ghana Money Transfer brand

The remittance company, renowned for its fast and quality service, recently strengthened its presence in the Caribbean, acquiring the QuikCash Money Transfer brand in 2010 to expand its services in the Cayman Islands.
