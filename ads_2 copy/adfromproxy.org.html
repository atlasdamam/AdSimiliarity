    To add your own network-compatible phones to your Family Mobile account, just follow these three simple steps:
    Step 1 Make sure your phone is compatible with the T-Mobile network.
    Step 2 Buy a $25 Starter Kit for each phone you want to add (this is just a one-time activation charge).
    Step 3 Activate your phones in-store, online or by phone.
    Check Your Phone's Compatibility
    For a phone to be compatible, it must be:
    1. A GSM phone (refer to table of common providers, below)
    2. Unlocked (most are locked to a specific carrier)
    Your current service provider can help you answer both questions, and — if your phone is currently locked — unlock it for you.
    Activate Your Phone on Family Mobile
    After you order and receive your Starter Kit(s), you can add your phone(s) to your Family Mobile account by:
    1. Visiting a Walmart store, where an associate can assist you
    2. Calling the T-Mobile Activations Line at 1-877-308-9621
      (6 a.m. - 9 p.m. PT)
    3. Visiting www.myfamilymobile.com
    Be sure to have these available: your Family Mobile Starter Kit, your driver's license or other government-issued ID, and your unlocked, network-compatible phone(s).
