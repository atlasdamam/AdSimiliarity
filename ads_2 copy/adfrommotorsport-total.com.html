Data privacy statement

The protection of your privacy while using our websites is extremely important to us. Therefore, in the following we will inform you regarding the collection of anonymous and personal data.

Anonymous collection of data

Generally speaking, you can visit the Volkswagen AG websites without telling us who you are. We only are provided information regarding the Website you viewed before coming to our site and the pages you visit while you are visiting our site. This information is used for statistical purposes only. As an individual user, you remain anonymous

Collection and processing of personal data

Personal data will only be collected if you provide us with this information, for example in processing a contract, an opinion survey or upon registration for personalised services.
Within the scope of Volkswagens personalised services, your registration data under the prerequisite that you have approved this will be used for purposes of advertising and market research as well as the tailored design of our electronic services. Furthermore, in case you provide your consent, the information you access during your visit to the Volkswagen homepages/portals will be combined into a user profile which is used in order to provide you with advertising individually tailored to your match profile.
This data is transmitted in encrypted form in order to guard against misuse by third parties. Your data will be processed within the Federal Republic of Germany. Use and transmission of personal data

Without your consent, the personal data collected within the scope of the Volkswagen AG websites are only used for contract administration and the processing of your query. Beyond this, use of your data for the purposes of advertising and market research as well as for the tailored design of the electronic services of Volkswagen AG will only be processed if you have provided your previous consent. The use of your data for personalised advertising also only occurs if you have provided your consent.
Since the provision of your consent regarding the use of your data for the above mentioned purposes can also include the transfer of your data to Volkswagen Partners (dealers and workshops) selected by you, your data may also be forwarded to these parties. Incidentally, a forwarding of the data to other third parties does not occur.

DESIGN

Let others know who theyre dealing with:

The unique front design including the GTI-specific red line and the grill with the legendary honeycomb design offer an impressive look. The widened side skirts, the striking rear spoiler and the dual chrome tailpipes will let the others know: here is someone to be taken seriously.

 

ALLROY RIMS AUSTIN

Brilliance on the road: The optional alloy rims Austin give your new Golf GTI a dazzling appearance with their sporty design and high quality workmanship. Additional security against theft teaches people an important lesson: you cant steal style.

 

BI-XENON HEADLIGHTS

At a glance: The new Golf GTI with its powerful bi-xenon headlights has its watchful gaze on the road. The headlights do not only provide an optimal illumination of the road, they also provide an absolutely sporty look.

 

MULTIFUNCTIONAL STEERING WHEEL

Power that fits your hands: The multi-functional steering wheel lets you handle the extraordinary performance of your new Golf GTI. Design elements like the GTI-clasp and the flattened undersite emphasize the sporty impression.

 

SEAT COVER CLARK

A true original: The high quality sport seats come with the iconic GTI seat cover Clark. This original Volkswagen design is part of the Golf GTI heritage and thus is exclusively reserved for this model.

 

GTI GEAR KNOB

Gears of strength: The unique GTI gear knob resembling a golfball is a major design characteristic of the GTI series. This tradition is passed down from the first and oldest generation to the newest.

 

SPORT SUSPENSION

Performance in its purest form: The sport suspension offers excellent handling of your Golf GTI by lowering the suspension by 15 mm, adding better control on curves and providing you with pure driving pleasure with a sporty tuning.

 

MOTORISATION

The beast beneath the bonnet: The Golf GTI stands for impressive performance and high speed. This power comes from the 220 hp TSI engine, which takes it to a maximum speed to 246 km/h and accelerates from 0-100 km/h in a breathtaking 6.5 seconds.

 

GTI PERFORMANCE

Limits are there to be broken: The upgraded Golf GTI 'Performance' carries the power to the extreme with extra 10 hp and 250 km/h maximum speed. The bigger disc brakes and the front differential lock make sure that you have the best control over the maximum power.

 

PROGRESSIVE STEERING

Progress: The innovative progressive steering technology ensures a even more direct and intense ride on winding roads and at higher speed. At a lower speed it reduces steering effort and makes parking more comfortable.

 

'DISCOVER PRO' NAVIGATION SYSTEM

Know your status: The 'Discover Pro' navigation system comes with a coloured easy-to-use TFT-touchscreen and a variety of useful components. The integrated Car-Net online services keep you updated about what is going on in your surrounding area.

 

DYNAUDIO 'EXCITE' SOUND SYSTEM

Powerful sound: The new Golf GTI not only sounds impressive with its dual tailpipes. The sound of the speakers will also give you goose bumps. The 400W DYNAUDIO 'Excite' soundsystem will caress your ears as you discover a new dimension in music.

 

SAFETY

With great power comes great responsibility: The new Golf GTI is equipped with the latest security systems, including ESC, automatic distance control, electronic parking brake, multi-collision brake and many more. Because even the bravest need a secure foundation.
