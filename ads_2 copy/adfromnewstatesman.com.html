What we do

Statoil is an international oil and gas company, building on more than 35 years of experience from oil and gas production on the Norwegian continental shelf. We operate in 36 countries all over the world, with more than 20 000 employees. We are headquartered in Norway, and are listed on the New York and Oslo stock exchanges.

The way we do it

We are committed to accommodating the world's energy needs in a responsible manner, applying technology and creating innovative business solutions. Our approach is founded on a values-based performance culture, a belief in cooperation and a striving for continuous operational improvement.
