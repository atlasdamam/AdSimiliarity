Bachelor's Degree - Online

Create the applications that keep
us connected.

As web-based gadgets land in the palms and
pockets of consumers of all ages, the demand for
developers who specialize in mobile technology has
taken off. From mobile banking and business utilities,
to applications that bolster our productivity and the
games and diversions that allow us to happily do the
opposite, gifted programmers are needed to create
specialized software from start to finish.

In Full Sail's online Mobile Development bachelor's
degree program, you’ll explore usability and design
principles, software development and management,
and application production for cell phones, iPads,
websites, handheld games, and other devices. You'll
create multiple projects that are in sync with
innovations in portable technology – and learn how
to take your apps to market.

Specialized courses include:

  • Android Application Development
  • iOS Application Development
  • Integrated Product Development
  • Mobile Business and Marketing
  • Mobile Game Design
  • Mobile Interface and Usability
