Advance your career with a degree

Capella is an accredited*, online university where working professionals gain the skills and knowledge to make a difference—in their lives, work, and communities. We serve more than 34,000 students from all 50 states and offer more than 145 bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral level specializations in business management, counseling, health care, higher education, human resources, information technology, K-12 education, nursing, psychology, public administration, and public safety.
