Expert Freshening Tips

Welcome to our Expert Freshening Tips, with help from our Home Lifestyle Expert, Evette Rios. Learn more about how to freshen up your home using our Febreze™ Carpet Deodorizing Powders.

Pull Off a Fresh and Clean Home in 20 Minutes or Less

The arrival of warmer weather means the beginning to spring and summer entertaining. From Mother’s Day and Father’s Day to graduation parties and bridal showers, people are hosting an endless string of events in their homes. With these quick and easy cleaning tips, every host with the most can have the house guest-ready and fresh in no time. Read More

If you’ve ever found yourself expecting last-minute guests, then you know it can be tough to clean the house in a hurry. Even if there isn’t time to do a thorough cleaning, you can still freshen fast and make the house guest-friendly with these tips. Watch Video

Five Simple Tasks for a Clean Home

Most people want a clean home, but not everyone has the time to regularly scrub their homes from top to bottom. Fortunately, homeowners can tackle a short daily cleaning checklist that will keep their homes in order and make them feel welcoming. Read More

WATCH: Eliminate Unwanted Pet Odors and Hair

Although you love your pets, you may not always love the little messes they leave behind. Here are some quick tips you can use to keep all family members, whether two-legged or four, living in harmony. Watch Video

Easy Party Prep and Cleaning Tips

While everyone loves a good party, especially during the spring and summer months, the party can seem like more of a chore for hosts who have the dreaded task of cleaning once the fun is done. But with a few simple party prep and cleaning tips, party hosts can have more fun knowing they’ll be dealing with less mess after the final guest has gone home. Read More
