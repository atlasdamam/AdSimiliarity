Welcome to the managed cloud

Simply put, the managed cloud is a layer of support and services for your cloud computing infrastructure. You get the same Fanatical Support we’re famous for, plus access to our trained cloud team who help you plan, deploy, and run your cloud.




Benefit from our cloud expertise

We have more Red Hat®-certified engineers than any other hosting company, and we’re Microsoft’s 2012 Hosting Partner of the Year. We’ve offered cloud services since 2006, and we co-founded OpenStack with NASA in 2010.



Get a complete platform

Your site or app has specific compute, database, networking, and storage needs. We offer a broad range of open cloud products you can draw from to customize your solution—and access to the expertise to help you manage it.



Optimize your cloud

Our staff can help you optimize your Linux or Windows web, application, and database servers. We can also help configure FTP, SMTP, software firewall rules, and even website caching. All while providing Fanatical Support®.
