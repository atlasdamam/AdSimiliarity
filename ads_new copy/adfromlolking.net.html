    XFINITY® Internet from Comcast: High-Speed Internet Service

    XFINITY Internet gives you the fastest in-home Wi-Fi for all rooms, all devices, all the time. Chat, surf, stream HD movies and TV shows and game online – all at the fastest speeds.

    The Fastest Internet

    XFINITY Internet gives you reliably fast speeds with the most coverage in your home so everyone can get online and connect all of their devices at the same time.

    • Fast speeds even with the whole family online.

    • Share music and upload photos in the blink of an eye.

    • Play games online with less lag.

    Constant Guard®

    Constant Guard is the most comprehensive online protection of any major Internet provider. It’s a $360 value included with XFINITY Internet service for no additional cost.

    • Password protection – Secure your passwords with one-click access to online banking, shopping and email.

    • Norton® Security Suite — Help protect against viruses, spam, phishing and more with the top-rated protection suite.

    • Secure Backup & Share — Back up valuable files like photos, music and more.

    • IDENTITY GUARD® — Help safeguard yourself from identity theft with Lost Wallet Protection and more.

    Introducing Constant Guard mobile app – Now you can help protect your iPad® and iPhone® on the go with one-tap login for online banking and shopping.

    Learn more about online security with XFINITY

    XFINITY Connect

    XFINITY Connect is your online hub for all your communications – email, voicemail, contacts, calendar and more. Plus, you can access from anywhere – from any computer connected to the Internet or from your mobile device with the XFINITY Connect app.

    • Check your XFINITY email. You can even combine external email accounts in one inbox.

    • Stay up to date with Twitter and Facebook feeds.

    • Use the calendar to keep track of important dates and get reminders about upcoming events and appointments.

    • If you’re a XFINITY Voice Unlimited subscriber, you can send and receive text messages and listen to or read your voicemail messages – all with XFINITY Connect.
