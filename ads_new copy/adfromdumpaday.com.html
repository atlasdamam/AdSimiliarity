Why Stay at a Disney Resort Hotel?

Located in the middle of the magic, Disney Resort hotels offer an array of special benefits to make your vacation as memorable as can be.

Book Your Vacation Package

Benefits of Staying at a Disney Resort Hotel

Enjoy valuable Disney Resort hotel benefits designed to provide you and your family with the most time-saving, convenient and entertaining vacation possible.

Complimentary Airport Service – Avoid the hassle of baggage claim with Disney’s Magical Express transportation, which conveniently transports you and your bags to and from Orlando International Airport.

Complimentary Resort Transportation and Parking – Motor coaches, ferryboats and our world-famous monorail make it easy and convenient for you to get around and experience all there is to see and do throughout Walt Disney World Resort, so you don’t have to miss a thing. And if you’re driving, complimentary parking is also available.

Extended Theme Park Hours – Each day, experience the magic at a more easygoing pace thanks to Extra Magic Hours, where you’ll enjoy additional quality time with family and friends in one of the parks—above and beyond the standard operating schedule.

Complimentary Wi-Fi – Stay connected with convenient, wireless high-speed internet access in Guest rooms, select restaurants and most public spaces.

Disney Dining Plans – Choose a Magic Your Way package that includes a dining plan and you could save up to 15% while enjoying a variety of dining experiences throughout Walt Disney World Resort.

Resort Hotel Entertainment – Elaborately themed pools with slides, Disney movie screenings under the stars plus our widely popular Character Dining experiences are just some of the fun-filled activities that may be available at select Resort hotels.

Legendary Disney Service – Want to know what it's like to have a Fairy Godmother? Someone who really cares about transforming your vacation dreams into happily-ever-after reality? Disney Cast Members are available 24 hours a day to make your vacation as captivating and carefree as possible. It's all part of the phenomenal brand of Disney service you’ll find no matter where in the "World" you turn.

Discover Unique Disney Accommodations

Disney Story Rooms – Act like a pirate, live like royalty or feel like a character in a beloved Disney animated film when you stay in magically themed accommodations at select Walt Disney World Resort hotels.

Family Suites – Immerse yourself in the magic of expansive accommodations for parties up to 6—with just the right amount of space for everyone in your family. Family suites include three separate sleeping areas, two bathrooms and a kitchenette.

Rooms for 5 or More – Enjoy expansive accommodations for groups of 5 or more—with room for everybody in your family. And if you’re planning a family reunion or a special occasion with friends, larger rooms may also be available.

Guest with Disabilities – Disney Resort hotels provide numerous accommodations for Guests with disabilities, including those with mobility disabilities, visual disabilities and hearing disabilities. We offer wheelchair-accessible bathrooms, rooms designed for the hearing impaired, rooms that accommodate service animals and much more.

Certain benefits mentioned above are also available to Guests staying at the Walt Disney World Swan and Dolphin Hotels and select Downtown Disney Resort area hotels.
