About Clinical Studies

A clinical trial is a study. The aim of the Tourmaline AL1 Trial is to investigate the overall hematologic response rate, vital organ (heart and kidney) effects and the safety of an investigational therapy in relapsed or refractory AL Amyloidosis. As with all clinical trials, study participants can stop participating at any time, for any reason. Find out more about clinical trials at the National Institute of Health Resources site.

What is AL Amyloidosis?

Amyloidosis is a disease that occurs when substances called amyloid proteins build up in your organs. Amyloid is an abnormal protein usually produced by cells in your bone marrow that can be deposited in any tissue or organ.
