At ASUS, we've been at the forefront of computer technology for over a decade and recognized early-on that the digital audio revolution would change the way we store and playback our music.

 

We knew that in order for die-hard audio enthusiasts to give up their standalone CD players and accept the PC as a source, many of the fundamental digital audio principles learnt from the transport and standalone DAC era would need to be employed within a small footprint. We felt our expertise in computer motherboards was the ideal foundation to meet this challenge, we knew where there problems lied and what we'd need to do to mitigate them, so we put together an in-house team dedicated to computer based audio products.

 

In 2007 we launched our first Xonar product. Employing careful layout and shielding techniques we were able to produce a soundcard that provided high quality audio from within a PC, gaining the recognition of audio enthusiasts. Subsequent models featured award winning innovations that allowed users more flexibility and we refined our designs further to provide audio fidelity that surpassed many of the external solutions.

 

As software progressed, the PC became known as the ideal audio source, capable of storing an entire audio library on a single disc and outputting a bit perfect data-stream to an external DAC. We've expanded our product portfolio to take full advantage of the PC as a source. The latest addition to the Xonar family is the Essence One series of USB based DACs. Utilizing our engineering expertise, we are able to produce a multi-functional product, incorporating innovative ideas that put the listener first.

In order to develop audio products that meet the needs of consumers, we need a team that's in touch with them. The ASUS Xonar team is comprised of musicians, audiophiles and skilled engineers. Members of our team were active in online audio communities long before they joined us here at ASUS. We are active in an official capacity in those communities today, providing customer support, taking polls and listening to requests.


As a team we collaborate, bounce around ideas, and then prototype them, tuning everything from hardware to software and aesthetics. The diverse nature of our team helps us develop products that respect the diversity of user preference - our soundcards and DACs feature user-tunable options that you won't find elsewhere. At the same time, we're mindful of making our products simple to use. Our belief is that PC based audio devices need not be complicated to setup or maintain.


Backed by years of experience in the computer industry, you can rest assured that the Xonar team is committed in bringing you the best audio products for your PC.

 

Integrated audio on motherboards has to struggle with limited space, complicated board layouts and cost considerations - constraining the performance of headphones and speakers. In contrast, the improved audio provided by Xonar audio products allows you to discover previously unnoticed details in your favorite songs.

The Chime of the Tiger is known as one of the first audio masterpieces in human history; an icon crystallized with the precious knowledge of materials and music. The Chime of the Tiger totem has inspired the Xonar team's passion for audio, in honor of the accomplishments of those who came before us. The team has made its own mark on history, contributing new audio innovation, thus maintaining humanity's endless pursuit of the essence of sound.


Merged with this spirit, the persistent commitment of the Xonar team delivers state-of-art Xonar Essence Products for audiophiles, offering an innovative approach to audio that is complemented by industry-leading standards.
