Colorado 7
Play

Think Colorado Truck.

The Colorado is designed to be distinctive at first glance, surround you with comfort and loads of style, deliver unbeaten 3.5 tonne towing capacity across the 2.8L range, and keep you and your cargo safe. As comfortable taking on the worksite as taking on the weekend, the Colorado has whatever it takes to get the job done.

Work or Play?

Built for power and performance, youll find theres no quit in the Colorado. It gets the job done  simple as that. And when its time to play, the stylish interior makes getting there easy.

Find out more about exterior features

Comfort and Style

Inside, the Colorado takes care of you as much as it takes care of business. With the information you need and the entertainment you want all within easy reach. Plus theres room to spare with plenty of comfort and loads of style.

Find out more about interior features

3.5 Tonne Towing#

Hitch it up and let class leading 3.5 tonne towing capacity# go to work. Take more to the job, and more on the weekend  leave nothing behind. Its all in a days work for the Colorado.

# across the 2.8L range.

Safety

You, your mates, your family  that's the most important cargo of all. So it's comforting to know that the Colorado is equipped with front airbags for the driver and front passenger, as well as full length side curtain airbags. Add to this Electronic Stability Control (ESC) on every model  it's just another way the Colorado has got your back.
