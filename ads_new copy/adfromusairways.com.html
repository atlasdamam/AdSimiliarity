Important Information about the US Airways Dividend Miles MasterCard Card Program: Not everyone will qualify for the US Airways Dividend Miles MasterCard. Offers may vary. Please review the information below and the Terms and Conditions for the reward benefits (such as bonus miles and how miles are earned), interest rates and account fees and terms for this particular offer before applying.


1 Offer subject to credit approval. Not everyone will qualify for the Premier World MasterCard or World MasterCard. In order to be approved for a Premier World MasterCard or World MasterCard and all of the benefits featured here, you must meet the credit and income criteria previously established for this offer. This offer is available to new Cardmembers only. For information about rates, fees, the Annual Fee, other costs, and the reward program rules (including miles accrual rate, bonus miles awards, etc.) and benefits associated with the use of this credit card program, please see the Terms and Conditions.


2 Important Balance Transfer Information: The 0% introductory APR on balance transfers is applicable for the first 15 months after the account is opened. At the end of the introductory period, the APR for all balance transfer balances will be either 15.99% or 24.99%, which will be determined at account opening based on your credit worthiness. This APR will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate. If you make a balance transfer you will be charged interest on purchases if you do not pay off your entire balance, which includes any transferred balance, by the payment due date each month. This introductory APR does not apply to Purchase or Cash Advance transactions. For information about the rates, fees, the annual fee, and other costs associated with the use of this credit card account, please see the Terms and Conditions.


3 Bonus Miles: Applicants who are approved for the Premier World MasterCard will receive 30,000 bonus miles after the first use of the account for a Purchase or Balance Transfer (that are not later returned or rescinded) and have paid the Annual Fee on the account (and such fee is not rescinded). Applicants who are approved for the World MasterCard will receive 15,000 bonus miles after the first use of your account for a Purchase or Balance Transfer (that are not later returned or rescinded) and have paid the Annual Fee on the account (and such fee is not rescinded). Bonus miles will be sent to US Airways at the close of your billing statement after qualifying transaction(s) have been posted. Balance Transfer Checks do not qualify for bonus miles.


Balance Transfer Bonus Miles: Earn one (1) mile per one dollar ($1) in balances transferred to your US Airways Dividend Miles MasterCard requested in the first 90 days after account opening, up to a maximum of ten thousand (10,000) miles. Please note: If you make a balance transfer you will be charged interest on purchases if you do not pay off your entire balance, which includes any transferred balance, by the payment due date each month. There is a fee for Balance Transfers. Balance Transfer Checks and Convenience Checks do not qualify for balance transfer bonus miles. The Balance Transfer is subject to the Balance Transfer Fee set forth in the Terms and Conditions.


Earning Miles: Premier World MasterCard Cardmembers will receive two (2) miles per one dollar ($1) on US Airways purchases and one (1) mile per one dollar ($1) spent on all other purchases. World and MasterCard Cardmembers will receive one (1) mile per one dollar ($1) on US Airways purchases and one (1) mile per one dollar ($1) spent on all other purchases. See Terms and Conditions for complete details.


First Class check-in and Zone 2 boarding: Premier World Cardmembers are invited to check-in at the US Airways Preferred or First Class counter when checking-in for their flight. The Primary Cardholder of the Premier World MasterCard or World MasterCard is invited to board the aircraft early as part of Zone 2 provided their Dividend Miles number is part of their reservation. First Class check-in and Zone 2 boarding are only available on US Airways operated and marketed flights.


Companion Certificate: Companion certificates issued to Cardmembers with the $89 Annual Fee MasterCard are redeemable for two companion certificates (starting at $99 each, plus taxes and fees) provided the annual fee has been paid. Companion certificates issued to Cardmembers with the $49 fee MasterCard, are redeemable for one companion certificate (starting at $149 each, plus taxes and fees). Companion certificates are valid for travel in the Continental U.S. or Canada with the purchase of a US Airways ticket (minimum fare purchase of $250). Additional restrictions, taxes and fees apply. The Companion Certificate is valid for travel within 12 months and tickets must be purchased within 9 months from the certificate issue date.


4 20,000 Mile Award Travel: The Primary Cardmember will receive a five thousand (5,000) mile discount off of each eligible Award Ticket. For example, if twenty-five thousand (25,000) Dividend Miles are required to redeem an award ticket, then as a US Airways Dividend Miles MasterCard Cardmember who met the criteria set forth in the Terms and Conditions, the Cardmember's Dividend miles account would only be deducted by twenty thousand (20,000) miles instead of twenty-five thousand (25,000) miles. For more information visit www.usairways.com.
