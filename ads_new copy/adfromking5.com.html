Planning to build a custom home?

Our free eBook will tell you what you need to know to build and finance your custom dream home.

Building a custom home is one of the biggest and most exciting decisions you can make. Now is a great time to build the house of your dreams. If you've never been involved in a custom construction project, however, the process can seem daunting and complicated. We're here to help!

At Washington Federal, we've been helping people in our community realize their dreams since 1917. It's what we do. We've written this eBook to give you the tools you need to feel confident as you make decisions about your new custom home.

In our eBook, you'll learn how to:

  • Choose a piece of land for your new home.
  • Set priorities for features and design elements.
  • Create a budget that meets your needs.
  • Hire the experts who will help walk you through the process.
  • Finance your custom construction project.
  • Be prepared with the necessary paperwork and permits.

Ready to talk? So are we.

When it comes to building a custom home, it's a good idea to talk to your banker early in the process.  Our team of branch managers stand ready to provide you with a no-obligation consultation.  Even if you don't plan to break ground for a year, we're happy to go over how financing works and what you should be doing now in preparation for your big build. 

Find the branch manager nearest you to get started today.

Start a conversation about your next mortgage with one of our neighborhood branch managers. Call 800-324-9375 or drop us an email.
