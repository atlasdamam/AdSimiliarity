Variable Rate and Fee Information:
For a Home Equity Line of Credit, the annual percentage rate (APR) is a variable rate based on the highest Prime Rate (Prime) published in the "Money Rates" section of The Wall Street Journal® (a trademark of Dow Jones, LP) on the first business day of the calendar month and a margin from 0.75% to 3.70% depending on individual credit qualifications, credit commitment amount and other criteria. On April 1, 2013, Prime was 3.25%. Your resulting APR would be from 4.00% to 6.95%.

If you have BMO Harris SelectSM or Portfolio CheckingSM with Auto Pay, your margin would be from 0.50% to 3.45% depending on individual credit qualifications, credit commitment amount and other criteria and your resulting APR would be from 3.99% to 6.70%. The maximum APR is 18.00%. The minimum APR is 3.99%. Auto Pay means periodic scheduled payments automatically deducted from your BMO Harris Select or BMO Harris Portfolio Checking account, as applicable, to pay the BMO Harris Home Equity Line of Credit.

Rates shown are for lines of credit with a combined loan-to-value (CLTV) of 80% or less in AZ, IL, IN, KS, MO, MN and WI, a CLTV of 70% or less in FL and a CLTV of 60% or less in NV. Qualification for 3.99% APR pricing is dependent on having a credit commitment of $100,000 or more, individual credit qualifications and other criteria.

A $50 annual fee applies after the first year. If you cancel the line of credit within 36 months of closing, a cancelation fee of $350 will be charged.

Closing Costs Information: BMO Harris will pay the following closing costs: appraisal charges, flood determination, title insurance and document recording fees. The customer may be required to pay state and local taxes and to satisfy prior liens. In FL, customer is responsible for Documentary Stamp Tax of $3.50 per $1,000 and Intangible Tax of $2.00 per $1,000. In KS, customer is responsible for Mortgage Recording Tax of $2.60 per $1,000. In MN, customer is responsible for Mortgage Recording Tax of $2.30 per $1,000.

Insurance Information: The customer must obtain property insurance and may be required to obtain flood insurance.

General Information: Property must be an owner-occupied 1-4 family residence in AZ, FL, IL, IN, KS, MO, MN, NV or WI. The line of credit must be secured by a first or second lien on your property. This offer does not apply to the purchase of any property. This offer cannot be combined with any other offer. Opening a checking account is also subject to bank approval.
