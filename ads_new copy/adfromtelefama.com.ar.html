Notre Dame de Namur University

For 160 years, Notre Dame de Namur University (NDNU) has helped students achieve their career goals through education. We now combine our longtime commitment to personal attention and professional guidance with the convenience and flexibility of online learning.
  • Professors who bring real-world professional experience to the online classroom, and who possess the finest academic credentials

  • Convenience and flexibility of an online learning environment

  • 24/7 technical support, so you can study at any hour of the day or night

  • A diverse and inclusive learning community

  • Career-oriented education in a student-centered environment of encouragement and support

The NDNU Difference

Notre Dame de Namur University is consistently named as a top university in the West by US News & World Report®. in its “Best Colleges” rankings.

NDNU is accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges, one of six regional accrediting bodies certified by the U.S. Department of Education. With this nationally recognized accreditation, NDNU online graduates can be confident their degrees will be respected both in the academic community and the professional marketplace.
