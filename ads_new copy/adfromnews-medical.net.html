Featured Products

Quidel® Molecular diagnostic kits are FDA-cleared, CE-IVD–labelled, real-time PCR assays for the qualitative detection and identification of these infectious agents:

Influenza A + B
(FDA-Cleared/CE-IVD)

C. difficile
(FDA-Cleared/ CE-IVD)
 
Respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) and human metapneumovirus (hMPV)
(FDA-Cleared/CE-IVD)

HSV 1+ 2/VZV
(CE-IVD)

 

      
 

Apply for free evaluation kit

    

The kits offer accurate, rapid results as well as streamlined workflows, and are validated for use on the QuantStudio™ Dx Real-Time PCR instrument, and the 7500 Fast Dx Real-time PCR Systems.

Featured Instruments


  • 7500 Fast Real-time PCR System

  • QuantStudio™ Dx Real-time PCR Instrument

Benefits of Quidel® Diagnostic Kits

Features Benefits
One-step reagent preparation Just add rehydration solution to the lyophilized master mix and pipette it into the assay plate
Simplified storage requirements The kits are designed for refrigerator storage (2–8°C) and have up to a 2-year shelf life from date of manufacture
Room-temperature setup The assay is setup at room temperature; no ice or cooling blocks are required
Rapid results Assay results are typically available in less than 75 minutes after extraction of the sample (60 minutes for the Direct C. difficile Assay)
Common internal control All Quidel® kits use a common internal PCR control and common cycling parameters, which allows extracted samples to be assayed with any of the Quidel® kits and also allows different assays to be run on the same plate
Regulatory—the kits conform to GMP, CE-IVD, and FDA requirements (Not all Quidel® diagnostic kits are available in the US: see individual products for details.)
Flexible format Kits include vials of 12 x 8 master mix, rehydration solution, and liquid process controls
