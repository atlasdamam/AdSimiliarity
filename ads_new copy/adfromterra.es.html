Product Details
Fiery. Powerful. Sexy. A bold new modern-day interpretation of Obsession for men, Dark Obsession for men is powerfully masculine and intensely sexy. Top notes of brazilian green mandarin, guarana and touches of absinthe awaken the senses. The heart notes of french clary sage, fir balsam and white vetiver create a bridge to the sensual and provocative base with notes of madagascar vanilla bean, labdanum and suede for an overall bold and uncompromising fragrance.
