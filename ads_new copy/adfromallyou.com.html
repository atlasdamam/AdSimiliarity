Caponata Crostini

Monk Fruit In The Raw | This & That | Really Get Cooking | Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 8 servings

In Sicily, this sweet and sour dish featuring eggplant, raisins, and capers is served as part of an antipasto. It is perfect presented on crisp whole-wheat crostini as an hors d’oeuvre or as a side dish accompanying grilled fish, poultry, or pork. Also serve it with brown rice and cannellini beans for a meatless meal.
