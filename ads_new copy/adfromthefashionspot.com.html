Jennifer Davidson

Jennifer Davidson is the Managing Editor of theFashionSpot and Co-founder of StyleBakery.com. Prior to joining tFS, Jennifer produced fashion segments for television and was a fashion stylist for television and celebrity events. Counting Diane von Furstenberg and Ali MacGraw circa 1970 as her style icons, Jennifer has a profound weakness for printed dresses and vintage Chanel jewelry.
