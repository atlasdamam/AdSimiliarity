Salon Smoothness Has Never Been So Easy

 

Our innovative EasyWax™ Electrical Roll-On Kit is a self-heating wax system that has everything you need for quick and easy salon-quality results at home. Simply heat up, roll on, and wax off hair for incredible smoothness that lasts up to 28 days!

  • Salon-quality without the salon prices
  • Keeps wax at the perfect temperature
  • Ensures a clean and even application

Each Pack Contains
  • A self-heating device and its cap
  • A 50ml roll-on wax refill, specially designed for use on legs & arms
  • 12 non-woven strips
  • 4 Perfect Finish Wipes
  • A base to hold the device and electrical cable
  • Time

Directions of Use

You’re so close to salon smoothness, you can almost feel it. Actually, after these simple steps, you will be able to feel it! It’s really easy and there won’t be any mess to worry about.

  • 1

    Insert the Veet® wax refill into the device.

  • 2

    Plug the electrical cable into the device and in the socket. Place the plugged device on its base and leave it connected for 30min. Once charged, take out the device and find a comfy place to begin.


  • 3

    Apply the wax in the direction of hair growth (from knee to ankle for legs). Check that the slanted side of the roller head is in contact with the skin during application to allow the roller to glide over the skin.

  • 4

    Immediately place the strip over the layer of wax, ensuing that there’s a least 1cm at the bottom that’s wax free. Rub the strip repeatedly over the wax in the direction of hair growth.

  • 5

    Hold your skin taut to avoid discomfort. Holding the bottom of the strip, very quickly and in one swift motion, pull the strip back on itself against the direction your hair grown (from ankle to knee for legs). Remember, the FASTER the better.

  • 6

    When you have finished waxing, remove any wax residues form your skin with a perfect finish wipe (or the back of a used strip). Keep in mind that this wax can’t be rinsed off with water.

  • 7

    Unplug the device. After use the refill will be hot, so allow it to cool down before removing it from the device. And there you have it, salon smoothness for the next 28 days!

Directions

  • Remove the pink cap from electrical warmer and insert the Veet® wax refill into the device, then replace the cap.
  • Plug the electrical cable into the device and in the socket. A red power light will appear on the electrical warmer. Place the plugged device on its base and leave it connected for 30min. Once charged, take out the device and find a comfy place to begin.
  • After 30 minutes, check the temperature of the wax by rolling it over a piece of paper. If it rolls evenly and easily, it’s at the right temperature and you are ready to wax. If it’s not ready, let the electrical warmer continue to warm the wax for an additional 10 to 20 minutes, checking periodically to see if the wax is flowing.  To make sure the refill stays in, give the electrical warmer a gentle shake upside down to help the wax flow to the roller head. You may leave the heater plugged in while you wax since it regulates its own temperature. If you’d prefer, you can unplug it and the wax should stay at the right temperature for 15 minutes. Always remember to unplug the heater after use.
  • Before you begin, TEST THE WAX FOR WARMTH AND REACTION to your skin by applying and removing the wax to a small part of the area you wish to treat.  If after 24 hours there is no adverse reaction, begin waxing.
  • Apply the wax in the direction of hair growth (from knee to ankle for legs). Check that the slanted side of the roller head is in contact with the skin during application to allow the roller to glide over the skin. Roll in one smooth motion; do not roll back and forth.
  • Cut the hair removal strip to a suitable size and place over the area to be waxed, ensuring that there is at least a 1 cm (3/8 inch) section of fabric wax-free. Rub the strip repeatedly over the wax in the direction of hair growth.  Gently lift up the wax-free fabric edge to get a firm grip.  Never lift the strip upwards because this will only break the hair. Always keep it parallel and as close to the skin as possible.
  • Hold your skin taut to avoid discomfort with one hand and use the other hand to very quickly remove the strip in one swift motion, keeping the strip as close to the skin as possible. Immediately afterwards, apply firm pressure to the area with your fingertips or palm of your hand.
  • When you have finished waxing, remove any wax residues form your skin with a Veet® Perfect Finish Wipe (or the back of a used strip). Keep in mind that this wax can’t be rinsed off with water. 
  • Unplug the device. After use the refill will be hot, so allow it to cool down before removing it from the device. And there you have it, salon smoothness for the next 28 days!
