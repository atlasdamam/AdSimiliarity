A Military Discount That Works as Hard as You Do.

Combining preferred pricing and most current incentives; Chevrolet offers the best military discount from any car company. Active Duty members, Reserves, Veterans within one year of discharge date and Retirees of the U.S. Military5 – including their spouses – have an exclusive way to save on an eligible new Chevrolet6.

May is Military Appreciation Month and Chevrolet thanks you for your courage and commitment to our country. We are honoring the occasion by making the best Military Discount from any car company even better.

During the month of May when you purchase an eligible new Chevrolet6 through the Military Discount Program a $1007 donation will be made to the Achilles Freedom Team of Wounded Veterans. There’s also a special Honor and Valor badge to display on your new Chevrolet as a token of our gratitude for your service.

Help our troops call home…free!

To a military family, a phone call home is priceless. But these phone calls are expensive. A call from overseas can cost $7 to $8 per minute.

By donating your used cell phone through Cell Phones for Soldiers, you can help U.S. servicemembers get a free 60-minute calling card.

Download a packing slip and donate your used cell phone now.

Helping soldiers feel closer to home.

Cell Phones for Soldiers addresses an everyday emotional need that everyone has experienced: the imperative to phone home…to hear a familiar voice while far away.

Cell Phones for Soldiers makes a difference.

Cell Phones for Soldiers was founded by teenagers Robbie and Brittany Bergquist from Norwell, MA, with $21 of their own money. Their goal is to help our soldiers serving overseas call home by providing prepaid phone cards.

Please help our troops by donating your used cell phones today!
