Product Description

Product Description

Panasonic E6 Series Smart VIERA LED TV - Fully connected, network enabled TV in a super slim design (Energy Efficiency Class A+)

The E6 Series of super-slim LED TVs, available in 32" (81cm), 39" (99), 42" (107cm) and 50" (127cm) screen sizes, offer Freeview HD, Internet-ready Smart VIERA functionality and a wide range of advanced yet easy to use features. For superior high definition image quality, 100Hz BLB (Back Light Blinking) picture technology ensures sports and action movies look crisp, while built-in Wireless LAN simplifies home networking.

Panasonic E6 Series LED TVs use a highly efficient LED-backlit panel, which delivers vibrant image quality combined with a super-slim design.

For the ultimate in big-screen personalisation, Panasonic's unique My Home Screen customisable launch menu provides quick and easy access to your favourite apps, games and streaming Internet TV and Catch-up services.

Super-slim design

Panasonic E6 Series TVs sport a striking super-slim design with a Super Narrow Bezel, so they're easy to accommodate within any dcor.

Easy operation

Panasonic E6 Series LED TVs couldn't be simpler to set up and use. Panasonic's Voice Guidance technology literally talks you through the set up options. If you need to consult the operating manual at any time there's also an onscreen eHelp guide available at the touch of a button (no more trying to remember where you put the booklet). Internet and network content can be accessed via a wired Ethernet cable or the TV's own Wireless LAN.

Panasonic E6 Series LED TVs offer internet browsing & a range of other online services, easily accessible via My Home Screen. Visit your favourite websites without powering up your PC.
Energy efficient

Cutting-edge product design makes the E6 Series extremely energy efficient, achieving an A+ energy rating. New materials in the LCD panel transmit light more effectively, while the LED backlight is more efficient than ever. An Eco Navigation mode allows you to adjust a variety of energy saving functions, including automatic picture brightness adjustment (Eco mode) and Auto Standby, with a single operation.

Super-smart TV

My Home Screen

With My Home Screen you can personalise your viewing in a way that's never been possible before. Out of the box, Panasonic E6 Series LED TVs offer three My Home Screen presets, themed TV, Lifestyle and Information, each offering relevant content and apps. In addition, you can create your very own My Home Screen based around your own favourite content. This may include BBC iPlayer, YouTube or any of the many other services available from the VIERA Connect portal. A Web Content Optimizer ensures you always get a smooth, clear image from Internet-based video sources.

With Swipe & Share 2.0, you can easily display images from your smartphone or tablet device on an E6 Series Panasonic TV. It's a great way to show off your latest photographs.
Smart VIERA

All E6 Series TVs offer VIERA Connect functionality, with full access to your favourite social media websites. You can also browse the Internet and Skype video chat. Skype calls require a Skype account and optional Skype camera.

Take control of your Panasonic E6 Series TV using the latest Panasonic remote app, available for iOS and Android smartphones.
Remote App 2 and Swipe & Share 2.0

E6 Series screens can be conveniently controlled from a smartphone or tablet device, using the latest Panasonic remote app (available for iOS and Android). This app mirrors the functionality of the TVs main remote control, allowing you to navigate menus without interrupting the viewing of other family members. And by using Panasonic's Swipe & Share DLNA technology, you can move content to/from your mobile device to a Panasonic TV with a simple gesture it's a great way to share photos and video.

Outstanding image & audio quality

IPS LCD panel technology

Thanks to IPS LCD panel technology, Panasonic E6 Series TVs deliver brilliant 1080p images with vibrant colour and outstanding contrast. The viewing angle is extremely wide at 178 degrees, so you'll enjoy a great picture regardless of your seating angle to the screen.

Panasonic E6 Series LED TVs use high-quality IPS LCD panels & Freeview HD tuners to deliver brilliant 1080P images.
Freeview HD tuner

An integrated Freeview HD tuner provides over 100 TV and radio services, including four high definition channels, without the need for any additional subscriptions or service contracts.

Superior audio performance

For clean, dynamic sound, E6 Series TVs feature 2 X 10W audio amplification and full range speakers. For the widest stereo separation, V-Audio processing is also provided.

Connectivity

HDMI inputs

Offering three HDMI inputs, E6 Series TVs can easily connect to other HD home entertainment equipment, including set top boxes, Blu-ray players and games consoles. One HDMI connection is ARC (Audio Return Channel) ready, making it simple to connect to a soundbar or surround sound system.

Additional connectivity

Panasonic E6 Series LED TVs feature an optical digital audio output, which can be used with compatible surround sound audio systems, and have SCART and component video inputs for connecting other, older components. If you have music, video or JPEG files you want to enjoy there's a USB media reader too, compatible with all popular file formats.
