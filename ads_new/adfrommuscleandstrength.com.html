Old School pre-workout formula: Loaded with Arginine - Most of the old school formulas, are largely formulated using the amino-acid arginine to increase nitric oxide (NO). The problem is, arginine is not the rate limiting factor for NO synthesis in humans, meaning by taking extra, it does not drive your body to make more NO as a result. Even when large oral doses of arginine were taken, no increase in NO levels. Simply stated: The scientific studies suggest DOES NOT support that taking extra arginine orally will increase NO levels. Additionally, high doses of arginine, can have you running to the bathroom. Certainly not desirable during your workout.

PreSurge Unleashed solution- We take a three angled approach at boosting NO

  1. GlycoCarn(gycine propionyl l-carnitine) At the core of our nitric oxide/vascularity blend is the patented form of L-carnitine, GlycoCarn(gycine propionyl l-carnitine), which scientific studies suggest have been shown to increase NO. GlycoCarn is the FIRST ingredient shown to actually help increase nitric oxide synthesis.
  2. Epimedium- (standardized for 40% Icariin) PreSurge utilizes an extract of icariin from the herb epimedium. Icariin is the constituent of the epimedim that is largely believed to bring about the vascular effects. Icariin has been shown to help stimulate both short term production of nitric oxide(NO), as well as having longer term effects on endothelia nitric oxide synthase(eNOS), the enzyme needed to synthesize NO. The end result of these effects, is smooth muscle and blood vessels are relaxed, allowing them to increase their diameter, opening up the flood gates for greater blood flow, greater amount of nutrient delivery, feeding, PRIMING and encouraging your muscles for muscular growth.
  3. Antioxidant Blend While exercise in itself is a great way to increase NO output, it also increases free radicals. Free radicals actually reduce NO levels, decreasing vasodialation, which of course is not what we want to happen during exercise. To counteract these free-radicals, we have included a specific array of antioxidants, which have actually been shown in research to protect NO from being broken down and deactivated by free-radicals. These anti-oxidants include, Grape seed extract(95% OPC), alpha-lipoic-acid(ALA) and folic-acid. Interesting, both ALA and folic-acid have been shown in research to additionally enhance eNOS(the enzyme that synthesizes NO)

The perfect supplement to combat exercise induced neurological burnout that occurs to so many people when training hard and heavy. Neuravena is a wild green oat extract, that utilizes specific constituents of the plant that have a high degree of biological activity in relation to the two central nervous system enzymes, monoamine oxidase B(MAO-B) and phosphodiesterase 4(PDE 4). Research in humans has shown that Neuravena inhibits these two enzymes, which in turn will boost the neurotransmitter dopamine and the chemical messenger cyclic adenosine monophosphate(cAMP). A: Because creatine monohydrate, has over 500 hundred peer reviewed, published studies to support it as an effective ergogenic aid.
