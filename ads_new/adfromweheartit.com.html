We are pleased to announce the world premiere of an incredible new film, Girl Rising, to tip-off festivities surrounding the Phillips 66 Big 12 Women's Basketball Championship at the American Airlines Center. This free showing will be in AT&T Plaza outside of the AAC on Thursday, March 7 at 7 p.m. The film is by an Academy Award nominated director (Richard Robbins) and features performances from Meryl Streep, Anne Hathaway, Selena Gomez, Salma Hayek, Alicia Keys and others. It's part of a global movement for girls' education and we are excited to bring it to Dallas.
