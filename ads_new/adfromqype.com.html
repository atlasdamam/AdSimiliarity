If you have problems with your heating or cooling systems, or if you are having issues with too much dust or dirt, call American Weathermakers right away! We are an experienced heating and air conditioning contractor serving all the North Shore, North and Northwest Suburbs of Chicago, IL. Our service area was even recently expanded to include Southeastern Wisconsin. You can count on our highly trained technicians to keep your home comfortable around the clock because we provide 24/7 emergency service.

For more than 60 years, we’ve been well known for our fast replacement parts for furnaces, boilers, A/C units, humidifiers and water heaters. We even offer a duct cleaning service with Super Vac to remove the allergens that can make you sick or uncomfortable.

You can turn to American Weathermakers for all the major and minor repairs you need:

• Preventive maintenance
• Home comfort checkups
• Air filter replacements
• Air duct cleaning
• Air duct replacement and improvement
• Humidity control
• Sump pump maintenance
• Chimney checks
• Dryer vent cleaning
• Home zoning systems

All our products are installed and serviced by our highly trained and experienced technicians, and you can trust them to get the job done right the first time. American Weathermakers offers same-day service with no overtime or travel charges. Plus, we’re dedicated to providing friendly customer service that you’ll tell all your friends about.
