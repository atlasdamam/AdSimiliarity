To earn cash back for college when shopping online, you must make an eligible purchase with a partner through Upromise.com, a Upromise email, or with RewardU(sm) or TurboSaver®.

Product and partner exclusions and limitations to the 5% cash back apply. Go to http://shop.upromise.com/mall/view-all-companies for a list of cash back amounts by partner. You can also click on the 'Store Info' popup when you hover over a partner to see coupons & deals and product exclusions for that partner, if any.

Due to certain mobile device configurations and/or merchant website tracking limitations, online purchases made with a Upromise partner through a mobile device (phones, tablets, etc.) may not be properly tracked to earn cash back.
