The Barnes & Noble MasterCard

Your Rewards Begin with a Free $25 Gift Card1
After your first purchase we'll send you a $25 Barnes & Noble Gift Card. It's our way of saying 'thank you' for becoming a cardmember.

Get an Extra 5% Back at Barnes & Noble2
That's in addition to any other discounts you may receive. Just pay with your Barnes & Noble MasterCard at any Barnes & Noble store or online at www.bn.com and you'll save an extra 5% delivered as a credit on your statement including NOOK and NOOK downloads.

Earn Free Barnes & Noble Gift Cards3
Earn one Reward Point for every dollar you charge anywhere else, even for everyday purchases like gas and groceries. Every time you reach 2,500 points, you'll automatically receive a $25 Barnes & Noble Gift Card.

No Annual Fee Plus a Great Introductory Rate on Balance Transfers5
There is no annual fee for the Barnes & Noble MasterCard and you can take advantage of a special low rate on balance transfers.
