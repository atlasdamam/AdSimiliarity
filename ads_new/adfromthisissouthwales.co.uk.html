Why choose motors.co.uk to find your next car?

Looking for new or second hand car? Motors.co.uk is the UK's second biggest used car classifieds network. Whether you are searching for BMW cars, Mercedes cars or any other car manufacturer, our database of over 150,000 cars for sale will ensure you find the perfect car

Motors.co.uk is a trusted destination for finding used cars for sale by being the only website running a basic history check on 9 out of 10 of its cars. You can browse in confidence, being able to quickly see if a car has ever been listed as stolen or written off following a serious crash.

Using our new car search

Motors.co.uk is proud to present our revolutionary car search, allowing users to create extremely specific searches including colours, trims, safety rating, and performance and running costs to name just a few. You are now able to find estate cars, convertible cars, automatic cars, or even diesel cars at the click of the mouse.

Bargain hunters will love being able to search for a cheap car by sorting search results to display cheap cars first. Don't forget to keep an eye out for the car sales offers.

Find second hand cars near you

Search for local used cars by using our regional car search. Simply select your location to find all the cars being sold in your area.

If you are looking for used vans, then please use our used vans search instead.

Looking for local used car dealers?

If you prefer to find car dealers in your area, be sure to use our car dealer locator containing a selection of 1000's of dealers nationwide.
