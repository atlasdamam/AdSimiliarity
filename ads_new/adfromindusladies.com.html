Who are we?
Started in 2005, Indusladies.com is the largest online community for Indian Women. Indian Women from around the world visit Indusladies every month to ask questions, get help, read and write blogs, engage in a dialogue and along the way build authentic and enduring friendships.

What do we offer?

Community Forum
It is a place to ask questions, discuss and have a conversation. We have forums around every aspect of an Indian Women - Pregnancy & Parenting, Health & Wellness, Arts & Crafts, Religion & Spirituality, Relationships, Work & Money to name a few. Ask your question or provide an answer and you will instantly be swept into the community.
350+ Bloggers
Over 350 bloggers call Indusladies their blogging home. They blog on a variety of topics, all with the intent of expressing themselves to engage the readers. For readers, it is a great place to find some great writing that you will surely enjoy. For bloggers, its an opportunity to get wide readership by accessing the 1.5 Million+ monthly visitors of Indusladies.com. It is like opening shop in a busy shopping center.
100+ Groups
Members who share a similar passion or life stage get together in groups. For example, we have groups for people who love embroidery, for fans of AR Rahman, for curvy ladies etc. We have over 100 groups on various topics. In groups, conversations take on a whole new level of intimacy as the other person instantly 'gets it'.
Deals
We bring you some exclusive deals, discounts & coupons from some of India's large and trusted online retailers. Over 30 Online retailers including India Shopping, Shopper Stop, Naaptol, Toyworld, Hushbabies, Flipkart are partnering with us. Deals cover wide categories including Books, Toys, Jewelry, Apparel, Travel Tickets, Cosmetics to name a few.

What's special about Indusladies.com

Clean & Safe
This is an emotionally safe place to discuss anything that is in your mind. No one would judge you or keep scores. Also, we have several human and automatic means to ensure that SPAM in any form doesn't exist, providing you a clean environment. Detailed privacy settings ensures that you control what others see.
Friendly & Helpful
Start participating and you will quickly realize how friendly and helpful the members are. Over 50 moderators watch over the site 24x7 and ensure that conversations are friendly and civil. Contributions that are rude or personal are immediately removed. The community itself encourages members to be helpful.
Large. 2 000 000+ Posts
With over 1.5 Million Monthly Visitors and 2 Million posts, Indusladies is a very large community. There will always be someone to answer your question or respond to your opinion. Also, much of the information you are searching for might already be there.
Micro Communities
While Indusladies covers a wide breadth of areas, we have several micro communities within us that are centered around a passion. There are communities around Parenting, Crafts, Relationship, Writing, Reading, Work. Just plug into anyone and you will feel the instant connection.
