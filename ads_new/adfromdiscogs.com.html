Voice-activated technology that helps you keep your eyes on the road and stay connected to your world.12
 
Hands-free calling means you never have to miss a call. If your phone rings, you can answer with the push of a button, and you can make a call with the sound of your voice.
Just plug in your compatible digital music player, USB drive or SD card slot, if equipped, and browse your music collection using voice commands.
Say Bluetooth audio25 and you can stream and listen wirelessly to whatever's on your phone, even Pandora47 internet radio.
Stay connected and you could have your incoming texts read to you with audible text messaging.
SYNC Services is complimentary for three years for new vehicle owners and provides turn-by-turn directions, 411 business search, personalized sports scores, news, stock quotes, horoscopes and more. 34
Voice/touch climate control means no knobs or dials to fiddle with. Just tell SYNC with MyLincoln Touch your preferred temperature and it can adjust to suit you.
SYNC with MyLincoln Touch includes two screens in the instrument cluster, allowing you to easily see key features.
911 Assist uses your connected mobile phone to help call 911 when you cant.18
 
And thats just for starters.
