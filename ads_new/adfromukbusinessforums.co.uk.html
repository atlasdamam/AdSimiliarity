Business loans options

If you have plans to grow your business or you're thinking about making a large equipment purchase, then one of our business loans may be just what you need. They are flexible and adaptable and can be tailored to suit short or long-term financial needs. And the interest rate is reduced by 1% as part of the Lloyds TSB Funding for Lending Scheme.

Base Rate Loan

As this loan is linked to the Bank of England Base Rate, the interest you pay changes as the base rate does.

Features & benefits

  • Interest rate reduced by 1% as part of the Lloyds TSB Funding for Lending Scheme
  • Borrow from as little as £1,000
  • Interest rate varies with Bank of England bank rate
  • Capital repayment holidays available
  • Security may be required
  • An arrangement fee will apply, up to 1.5%* and dependent on the amount you borrow.

Five-Year Commercial Fixed Rate Loan

The 5-year fixed rate loan makes it easier to plan your finances, as the interest rate is fixed for the first 5 years of the term, so you'll know exactly what the repayments will be each month. After that, the loan switches to a variable rate.

Features & benefits

  • Interest rate reduced by 1% as part of the Lloyds TSB Funding for Lending Scheme
  • Borrowing starts from £25,001
  • Terms from 5 to 25 years
  • Fixed rate for 5 years, with a variable rate thereafter on longer terms
  • Available on a secured basis only
  • An arrangement fee will apply, up to 1.5%* and dependent on the amount you borrow.

Commercial Mortgage

For many businesses, property can be one of the most significant costs. It can also become an important asset. A commercial mortgage could be one way for you to maximise your business finance.

Features & benefits

  • Interest rate reduced by 1% as part of the Lloyds TSB Funding for Lending Scheme
  • Borrow up to 70% of the value of the property
  • Capital repayment holidays available
  • Choice of bank rate linked or fixed rate*
  • For a bank rate linked mortgage you can apply for flexible capital repayment holidays of up to two years (to be taken in blocks of three months)
  • If you choose a fixed rate*, your repayments will remain the same even if bank rate rises or falls
  • If you repay part or all of your fixed rate* mortgage before the end of the agreed term there may be an early repayment charge**. There is no early repayment charge** for bank rate linked loans
  • If you wish to borrow on other terms we may still be able to help you.

Call us on 0808 1688 987.

Residential buy to let Mortgage

If you're looking for a way to grow your rental income and capital, by extending your property portfolio, our residential buy to let mortgage could be the answer.

Features & benefits

  • Interest rate reduced by 1% as part of the Lloyds TSB Funding for Lending Scheme
  • Borrow up to 70% of the value of the property
  • Capital repayment holidays available
  • Bank rate linked or fixed interest rate*
  • For a bank rate linked mortgage you can apply for a capital repayment holiday of up to 12 months (to be taken at the start of the loan)
  • If you choose a fixed rate*, your repayments will remain the same even if bank rate rises or falls
  • If you repay part or all of your fixed rate mortgage before the end of the agreed term there may be an early repayment charge**. There is no early repayment charge** for bank rate linked loans
  • If you wish to borrow on other terms we may still be able to help you.

Call us on 0808 1688 987.

*There is always a possibility that interest rates may go down leaving a fixed rate loan at a higher level compared to a variable rate loan. However, if interest rates rise, a fixed rate loan will remain at the same rate.

**A break cost may be applied on fixed rate loans, on any early or partial/full repayment of the loan. This could be substantial depending on the interest rate at the time.
