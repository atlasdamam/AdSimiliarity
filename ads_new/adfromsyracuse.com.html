GRADUATE DEGREES AT DePAUL



At DePaul University we prepare our students to see beyond what's obvious, into the world of what's possible. DePaul offers you more than 100 graduate and doctoral degrees in business, technology and computing, communications, education, law, liberal arts, health and science and more. You'll learn and thrive in a high caliber, challenging learning environment that is respected by employers, community leaders and other universities. DePaul's hands-on, practical learning philosophy gives you access to the brightest academic minds that challenge conventional thinking and bring real-world experience to the classroom.
  • Alumni network is more than 150,000 strong, with over 100,000 in Chicago.
  • Our MBA program at Kellstadt Graduate School of Business has received numerous top-10 rankings, and the College of Computing and Digital Media is one of the largest colleges of its kind in the nation.
  • Campuses in Lincoln Park, Loop, Naperville, Oak Forest and O’Hare.
