  • Training Champions for Christ since 1971

    Liberty University is the world’s largest Christian university and has over a 40-year heritage of providing academically excellent education. As an extension of our 6,800-acre campus in Lynchburg, Va., Liberty University Online equips you with the knowledge and skills to be a leader in your field and community. Our courses are taught in a flexible eight-week format with support services that are focused on making your experience successful. Liberty University is fully accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS) to award associate, bachelor’s, master’s, post-graduate, and doctoral degrees.
