At Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital, we understand the busy and fast-paced lifestyle of today’s corporate executive. We also know that the health of today’s top executives is an issue that cannot be overlooked, as they are a valuable asset to the company.

The Executive Health Program at Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital offers male and female executives a first-class health care experience tailored to your busy lifestyle and schedule including:

  • Comprehensive on-site services including the Executive Examination
  • Extended, 90-minute one-on-one physician contact
  • Same-day “Stat” results on testing services
  • Concierge service for scheduling

We encourage you, as one of today’s business leaders, to make executive health a part of your corporate planning for the future. Healthy employees are more productive, absent from work less, and typically more satisfied with their jobs. Healthy executives are the lifeblood of your company.

Our luxurious new suite on the Robert Wood Johnson campus includes a business center, private showers and lockers, refreshments, meals and other amenities. In addition, our location on campus allows you access to world-class physicians and state-of-the-art technology available only at a nationally recognized academic medical center.
