Safety & Security.

Protection from any angle

Compass helps protect you even in the event of side collisions with six airbags+ including new standard supplemental front seat-mounted side airbags+, engineered crush zones, door guard beams, an ultra-high strength steel crossbeam and more.

FEATURE AVAILABILITY EXPLORE SAFETY & SECURITY

  • Full protection

    Compass has more than 30 available safety and security features designed to help protect you and your travelling companions from harm.

  • Rear Back Up Camera+

    The new ParkView® Back Up Camera+ displays a rearview video image with distance grid lines when in reverse.

  • Hill Start Assist

    This system works to prevent Compass from rolling away when starting the vehicle on an incline, either uphill or downhill.
