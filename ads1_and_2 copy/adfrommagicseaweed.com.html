Roberts White Diamond 2

Posted on May 6, 2013 by Trip Forman

The new White Diamond 2 from Roberts is a high performance hybrid design with a strong focus on smaller waves up to head high. This board has quite a bit of horsepower under the hood with fast acceleration thanks to the wider diamond tail. The original White Diamond is the board the won Roberts Surfing Magazine Shaper of the Year Award, so following in those footsteps isnt an easy thing to do. The new White Diamond 2 (WD2) improves on the OGWD with faster/quicker turns and better release off the top.

White Diamond 2 Tech Specs:
Single to Double Concave Bottom
Signature Roberts flipped nose design with rolled entry
Rounded Diamond Tail with 5-fin set up. Works both as a tri/quad.
*Available in PU/Poly or Hydroflex Natural constructions
*All Roberts boards now come with volume specs

What we like:
Compared to original White Diamond, the new White Diamond 2 has a slightly narrower/thinner nose and also a double concave bottom out the back. This results is less swing weight up front during turns and a noticeably quicker turn in snaps off the top. The double concave does two things : Its way faster off the top if your foot isnt all the way on the back of the pad, and it also lengthens the sweet spot where your back foot can be and still have the board turn well. Even if your foot is all the way back against the ramp of your tailpad, you will still find the WD2 more reactive and snappier when compared to the original White Diamond.

This board gets compared a lot to other hybrids but it actually has a much higher performance feel with faster acceleration and better turning. The best way to think of it, is as a high performance shortboard that is VERY easy to surf with a 700hp engine in it. Mega power on demand, super fast and very lively when you want to throw down some turns and airs. This is why you see a lot of people getting one of these boards to position as a step down board to their high performance shortboard. Its a great grovel board too if you have the skills to get on the tail and make it happen. TONS of acceleration and speed!

Last but not least, Roberts is now listing the volumes of all their boards. This makes it incredibly easy to see which size WD2 is right for you based on your height, weight and volume preference.

What we dont like:
Whenever you release a new board, especially a sequel, most expect the original to fade away. The original White Diamond still has its place as its a bit easier to paddle and more forgiving to having your front foot too far forward thanks to the extra foam up there. Because the WD2 isnt made by CI or Lost, it flies under the radar a bit. Once someone tries one theyre usually a convert for life, since the WD2 is so unique compared to most other hybrids.

Why you want the White Diamond 2:
This board makes an incredible step down board to anyone riding a high performance shortboard. Its also one of the highest performance hybrids on the market when it comes to waves under head high.

*If you are a bigger guy. Check out the Roberts Hope Diamond. Available in sizes 6'6" and larger.
