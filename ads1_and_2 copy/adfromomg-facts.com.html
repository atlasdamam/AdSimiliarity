Legendary 4x4 capability

3.6L Pentastar® V6 engine ready to do your bidding

The 2013 Wrangler boats a powerful and efficient 3.6L Pentastar® V6 engine rated at 17 city / 21 hwy mpg+. Voted as one of Ward's 10 Best Engines for 2012+, this 285 horsepower engine is ready to provide you with power, satisfying fuel efficiency with up to 391 miles on a tank of gas+, and will deliver up to 260 pound-feet of torque.

Command-Trac® & Rock-Trac® 4WD Systems

For more four-wheel, off-roading power, the Command Trac® NV241 4WD system comes standard on Wrangler Sport, Wrangler Sport S and Wrangler Sahara, and delivers equal power to all four corners to get you through the rough stuff.

For more control and more torque, the Rock-Trac® 4WD system is a go-anywhere system with a 4:1 low-gear ratio that lets you to crawl at a lower speed with a greater increase in torque at the wheels.

Differentials, axles, sway bars & shocks

Wrangler Rubicon offers tuneable monotube shock absorbers which feature low speed tunable valve technology that's firm on the rocks and relaxed at higher speeds. Standard Dana 44 front and rear axles are equipped with Tru-Lok® locking differentials, and offer the ultimate in traction. Front axle sway bar-disconnect allows for better articulation off-road.

Reassuringly safe & secure.

Airbags and
Antilock brakes

Wrangler comes with a wide range of Safety & Security features including: standard advanced multistage front airbags+ that provide nearly instantaneous occupant protection by matching airbag output to impact severity, available side-supplemental airbags+ with sensor detection, body-on-frame construction, On-Road / Off-Road Four-Wheel Disc Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Brake Assist and more.

Hill Start Assist and Hill Descent Control

Wrangler's Hill Descent Control helps you monitor throttle, speed and braking when you’re traveling downhill and need it most. Plus, Hill Start Assist keeps your brakes applied for a short time after you’ve removed your foot from the brake to give you time to accelerate without rollback.

  • Tire Pressure Monitoring

    A Tire Pressure Monitoring Display System comes standard on all Wrangler models, so you’ll always drive with the utmost confidence.

  • Sentry Key® Engine System

    This system makes it virtually impossible for anyone without a valid key to drive your vehicle. The remote keyless entry system also lets you lock or unlock your doors.

  • Electronic Stability Control+

    Electronic Stability Control (ESC), with Trailer Sway Control (TSC) helps keep you (and whatever you’re towing) safely on course and under control.
