Email Marketing at VerticalResponse

Thousands of small businesses and non-profit companies depend on VerticalResponse to send their email marketing campaigns. Whether you're sending an email newsletter to a dozen loyal fans or an email marketing promotion to 10,000, we simplify your marketing so you can conquer the ten million other things on your to-do list. That's why we're one of the largest, fastest growing and trusted email marketing providers around.

Hundreds of Email Templates

Whether you're a rookie just starting out, or a seasoned email marketing pro, VerticalResponse provides all of the tools you'll need. With over 700 email templates and multiple email creation workflows to choose from, you can be confident we are the solution that will get you results. Absolutely no technical expertise required.

Exceptional Email Delivery

VerticalResponse is committed to ensuring your email marketing gets delivered. We use the most advanced methods in the industry and partner with ReturnPath to authenticate your email and ensure consistently high delivery rates. This allows you to focus on what counts: your relationship with your customers.

Email Campaign Reporting & Statistics

Track the results of your email marketing campaigns in real-time, from any web browser! With easy-to-view graphs and charts you can get a snapshot view or dive deep into the data to analyze your opens, clicks, bounces and unsubscribes, compare campaign performance and more – it's all there waiting for you!

Social Media Marketing Dashboard

Save time on your social media campaigns with VerticalResponse Social. Our social marketing tools allow you to schedule posts for Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn for the next month in just 20 minutes. Not sure what to post? We also provide content suggestions for maximum impact.
