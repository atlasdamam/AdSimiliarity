PACKET PICK-UP

Packet pickup will be hosted at Road Runner Sports on Thursday, June 6 and Friday, June 7 between the hours of 11am and 7pm. The address for Road Runner Sports is:

1435 N Kingsbury St
Chicago, IL 60642

What you need at Packet Pick-Up

  • Your photo ID (required at packet pick-up).
  • An initialed, signed and dated copy of the participant waiver.

Picking up a Friend's Swag

You may pick up the participant t-shirt, race bib, and chip.  Medals will not be released. You will need the following in order to pick up the items:

  • Copy of your friend's photo ID.
  • Signed letter from your friend stating that you are allowed to pick up their packet.

*Race packets and materials must be picked up on-site. They will not be shipped to participants.

When to Arrive on Race Day

We recommend that you arrive at least one hour before your wave time.

WHAT TO BRING

What you need at the race

  • Your photo ID
  • Appropriate footwear is highly recommended

What to leave at home

  • No outside alcoholic beverages will be allowed.
  • No glass containers will be allowed.

Gear Check

Gear check will be available for racers to check their items during the race (like a coat check). Feel free to leave your keys, t-shirt and all those items you don't want to carry with you during the race.

*Urban Warrior Dash is not responsible for any lost or stolen items.
