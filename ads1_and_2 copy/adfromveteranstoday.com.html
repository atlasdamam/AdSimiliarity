Why National University for Military Students

We Are Where You Are
National University has campuses and on-base learning centers throughout California, plus one in Nevada and Washington. Find one near you.
Courses to Fit Your Schedule
With your choice of more than 70 online degree programs, you can take your classes anywhere you have a desktop computer and Internet connection. See complete list of online degree programs.
High Graduation Rates
National University students graduate faster than most students. On average, graduate students complete their master’s degrees in 1.9 years and undergraduate students complete their bachelor’s degrees in 2.7 years.
Strong Alumni Community
Our growing community of 130,000+ alumni enjoy distinguished careers in education, business, engineering, health, the military, social and public services, and the arts.
Your Own Military Specialist
As a military student, you are assigned your own military admissions specialist to help you make the most of your studies at National University.

Top 10 Reasons Military Servicemembers Choose Us

National University's commitment to educational excellence and accessibility makes us the perfect choice for active and reserve servicemembers and their families.

  1. Flexible scheduling: start at the beginning of any month that's convenient for you and still fulfill your military and family commitments
  2. Year-round registration: enroll at any time without waiting for a new quarter or semester
  3. Accelerated course format — just one per month
  4. Focused learning so you can dive deep into each of your classes
  5. High-quality instructors with real-world experience
  6. Your choice of on-campus, online, and on-base learning centers
  7. More than 70 online degree programs
  8. Full accreditation by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC)
  9. Military tuition discounts
  10. Exclusive military support services, including a Veterans Affairs Office, a Veterans Success Center with dedicated faculty and staff, plus benefits as a member of the Servicemembers Opportunity Colleges (SOC) Consortium

Awards and Recognitions

National University, winner of the prestigious Ray Ehrensberger Award, is proud to report that 70% of our undergraduate and 75% of our graduate students complete their degree.

To ensure we are helping you achieve your goals, we have participated in Military Installation Voluntary Education Review (MIVER) since 1994, and as a member of the following organizations, we give military servicemembers extra support:

  • Servicemembers Opportunity College (SOC)
  • SOC Coast Guard (SOCCOAST)
  • SOC Navy (SOCNAV)
  • SOC Marine Corps (SOCMAR)
  • SOCGuard/ARNG/Education Support Center Partnership
