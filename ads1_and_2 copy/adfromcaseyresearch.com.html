Dear Reader,

Very possibly the most important Casey Research Summit ever has been scheduled at the stunning Loews Ventana Canyon Resort in Tucson, Arizona, this October 4, 5 and 6.

 

Over three information-packed days, a limited number of Casey Research readers and guests will participate side by side with a blue-ribbon faculty led by Dr. Ron Paul as they explore the seismic shifts ahead for the large Western economies and investment markets.

The world we now live in is manipulated, managed, and controlled… by the very same people who have manipulated, managed, and controlled the global economy to the precipice.

Separating fact from fiction is essential to surviving what's coming with your assets intact.

  • Will Obamacare destroy the US health care system and cripple the economy?
  • Can the world's central banks really print their economies back to prosperity?
  • What's driving the US stock market? How big is the risk as it reaches new highs?
  • Is the bond bubble approaching a pin, wiping out bond investors while simultaneously opening a decades-long profit opportunity for early movers?
  • Is the bull market in hard assets over or just getting started? What's holding gold and silver stocks back?
  • Has the US now crossed the line in becoming a police state?
  • After decades of false promises, is America finally on the path to energy independence? Or is it a myth opening the door for clear-eyed energy investors to profit?
  • Just how far is your government willing to go to defend the status quo of deficit spending and to keep the social fabric from tearing?
  • Is a currency war now both inevitable and imminent… what are the consequences for investors?
  • Is the Eurozone doomed?
  • And that's just for starters…

As you don't need us to tell you, the world has left the proverbial tracks, with overreaching governments now pursuing "whatever it takes" policies in the attempt to force fundamentally damaged economies into recovery, thereby creating massive misallocations of capital and investment pitfalls around every corner.

The government-led bank heist in Cyprus was just part of a continuum that, as it unfolds, will increasingly penalize the successful, destroy the middle class with inflation, and trap your wealth inside national borders in order to tax it at higher and higher levels.

To help you fully understand the perilous new world we live in and how to prepare for what’s next, Casey Research has pulled out all the stops for its only Summit to be held in 2013. Starting with an exceptional faculty, most of whom will not only present their latest research and answer your questions but participate as audience members throughout the entire three-day event.
