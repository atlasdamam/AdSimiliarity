About Us

 

The Institute of Business Management in Mangalayatan University is one of the top MBA colleges in India. It is an institute with excellent infrastructure offering graduate and post graduate courses in management education. It offers MBA and BBA courses. The institute is committed to a role in creating such leaders through its dynamic academic environment, well structured programmes, extensive specialist resources and a strong network of support during the curriculum.

Facilities

 

Infrastructure Overview: The institute has well equipped with all the modern facilities.

Hostel Details: The university has a modern Wi-Fi equipped six-floor hostel that can accommodate 476 students. It is divided into boys and girls sections, each with its own wardens available round-the-clock.

Laboratory Details:Computer labs at the institute are well equipped with modern software to keep the students abreast of the fast changing business and management needs of the corporate world

Classrooms Details: All the classrooms are well furnished.

Library Details:The institute has rich collection of books and journals in library.
