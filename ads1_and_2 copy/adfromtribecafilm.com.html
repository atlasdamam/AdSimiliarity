Mana Contemporary's qualified personnel and brand new 400,000 sq ft facility provides exceptional fine art storage. Our full array of services ensures safety for art work of all mediums.

We offer private storage units within our climate-controlled facility. Rooms are available in a wide array of sizes, from 50 to 500 square feet.

Our staff are fully trained in museum standards for art handling and packing. Our climate controlled trucks can transport art delicately and professionally from coast to coast.

Our in-house art crating shop features custom-poured molds and fully customized crates. Our crating specialists are trained in various packing techniques, from constructing a travel frame to customizing a crate for a marble sculpture.

Our in-house art framing craftsmen use the highest quality, museum grade materials, ensuring protection and highlighting composition.
