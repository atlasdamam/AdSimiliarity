REVIEW CARD BENEFITS

CARD FEATURES

Earn up to 5 Starpoints

Earn up to 5 Starpoints for each dollar of eligible purchases charged at hotels and resorts participating in the SPG® program — that's 2 Starpoints for using the Card in addition to the Starpoints you earn as an SPG member. Earn 1 Starpoint for all other purchases.3

Upgrade Your Status

Receive credit for 5 nights and 2 stays annually toward the next level of SPG Elite Status.4

REWARDS

Free Nights or Flights

Redeem Starpoints at over 1,000 hotels and resorts5 in nearly 100 countries and for flights on more than 350 airlines through SPG Flights, all with no blackout dates.6

Enjoy our worldwide destination portfolio

Earn free nights at any of our nine distinct brands including St. Regis, The Luxury Collection, W Hotels, Le Méridien, Westin, Sheraton, Aloft, Element and Four Points by Sheraton.

TRAVEL & BUYER PROTECTION

Global Assist® Hotline

You can rely on 24/7 emergency assistance when traveling more than 100 miles from home.7

Purchase Protection8

As a Cardmember, your eligible purchase can be covered for up to 90 days from the date of purchase when you charge any portion of the price of the eligible purchased item with your Card account. You will only be reimbursed for the amount charged to your Card.

FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

Account Management

Track your spending with customizable online tools.

Fraud Protection Guarantee

You won't be held responsible for any fraudulent charges.
