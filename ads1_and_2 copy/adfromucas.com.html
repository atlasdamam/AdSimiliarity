Student Accommodation from UNITE

Welcome to UNITE. We are the proud home to over 40,000 students from the UK and international students  from over 160 countries worldwide. With over 120 student properties in great locations, across 24 top university towns and cities, we are the largest student housing provider in the UK. 
 
You can choose to live with your friends in a shared flat, or decide to have your own self-contained studio flat. We offer lots of different accommodation choices, of different sizes and with all budgets catered for, and all within secure purpose-built buildings.
 
We know that finding the right student accommodation is an important part of student life, and we are committed to making your university experience enjoyable and hassle-free. All of our rooms have a private study area, Wi-Fi internet* and the majority have their own private bathroom. 
 
Plus, our all-inclusive pricing means that all of your electricity and water bills are taken care of, as well as your contents insurance. 
 
Whats more, with security access keys, 24-hr security response teams and on-site customer service and maintenance teams you can be assured that youll be living in a safe environment.
 
Each of our student accommodation properties has something different to offer, so check out UNITE accommodation in your city by using the property finder at the top of the page . If you have any questions, feel free to give us a call on 0800 121 7387 or +(44)117 302 7497 from overseas.
