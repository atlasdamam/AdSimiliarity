Expert tips

CLEANSING

Almost half of men don’t wash their face (48%), even though face care can help guys go the extra mile as appearance and grooming have become increasingly more important in society. The first step to putting your best face forward is using a daily hydrating cleanser like the Dove® Men+CareTM Hydrate+ Face Wash, which helps to replenish lost moisture and fight skin dryness to ensure skin feels smooth and cared for.
Dr. Allan Peterkin

Get More tips

SHAVING

For a closer, more even shave, use a shave gel (rather than soap) and allow it rest on your face for a minute or two to soften the facial hair before shaving. A moisturizing shave gel like Dove® Men+CareTM Hydrate+ Shave Gel will protect from irritation and offer a smooth skin feel.
Dr. Jeff Benabio

Get More tips

POST SHAVE

Many aftershaves contain alcohol, which your father may have used, and will actually dehydrate your skin by stripping it of water and natural oils. Try using a post shave balm instead, like Dove® Men+CareTM Post Shave Balm, which contains vitamin B5 and instantly helps relieve irritation caused by shaving, and soothes skin.
Dr. Jeff Benabio

Get More tips

MOISTURIZING

Even when bearded, your face is susceptible to the sun’s harmful rays, so be sure to use SPF daily. The Dove® Men+CareTM Hydrate+ Face Lotion with SPF 15 sunscreen is a great option as the fast absorbing, non-greasy solution will spread easily over your beard and will provide all day hydration while also protecting skin from the sun.
Dr. Allan Peterkin
