Get more out of your Free Checking relationship with these services

Eastern Bank Debit MasterCard
Make purchasing more convenient with our Eastern Bank Debit MasterCard. You can use it to pay for purchases at millions of locations worldwide, anywhere MasterCard is accepted.

Eastern HomeConnect online banking with bill pay
Bring your account information to your fingertips 24/7. View account activity, transfer funds, pay bills, see check images, and more from any computer with Internet access.

Eastern Bank Mobile Banking
With mobile banking, you can use your wireless device to view account balances and activity, pay bills, transfer funds between your Eastern Bank accounts, and find Eastern Bank offices and ATMs.

Overdraft protection
Protect yourself against expensive overdrafts by signing up for automatic balance protection from a linked savings account or Cash Reserve overdraft protection available for qualified applicants.

Direct deposit
Eliminate trips to the bank by having your pay automatically deposited to your account each payday. Direct deposit is safer and more convenient than receiving paper checking and your funds will be available immediately on your payday.

Combined statement
Simplify your finances by combining all of your Eastern Bank account information into one statement. Youll receive all your balance and transaction information at the same time each month.

Statement savings
Take advantage of a great and affordable way to start building savings. We make it convenient and easy to have funds from your checking account automatically transferred to your savings account - and it's free.
