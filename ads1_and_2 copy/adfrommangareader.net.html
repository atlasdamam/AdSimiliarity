WHAT MAKES AN ULTRABOOK AN ULTRABOOK?

Slim. Light. Fast. Lenovo Ultrabooks fulfill all of your needs, with style and speed to spare.

With extended battery life and super-fast USB 3.0 ports, you can stay connected anywhere, anytime.
