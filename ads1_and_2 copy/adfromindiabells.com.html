How Carbonite Works

  • Back upWith Carbonite, there's no hardware to install or maintain, no wires to connect, no tapes to deal with. Simply install the software – and ask your employees to do the same - and Carbonite takes care of the rest.
  • RelaxYour files are protected automatically and continually in the background. Backups are transmitted using secure socket layer (SSL) technology to one of Carbonite’s state-of-the-art data centers, so you can rest assured knowing your files are safe.
  • ManageA simple browser-based dashboard gives you insight to the backup status of everyone in your company – no matter where your employees are located.
  • AccessForgot the client contract at the office? Left your laptop in a cab? With Carbonite, employees can restore or access files anytime, anywhere, from any Internet-connected computer, or using the free Carbonite Mobile app.
