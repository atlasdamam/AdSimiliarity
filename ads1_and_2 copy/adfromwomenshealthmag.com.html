Mirinda Carfrae

What is your After?
my After is celebrating with my friends and family when I cross the finish; and grabbing a low fat chocolate milk to begin replenishing my body instantly. When I get home I like to ice down and wear my compression boots to help repair my muscles.

The biggest obstacle you’ve had to face as an athlete:
The biggest obstacle I face as an athlete is the discipline it takes to be the best.

Accomplishment you’re most proud of:
I'm most proud of my Ironman World Championships title I claimed in 2010.

How do you get pumped up for the competition?
I get pumped up by visualizing my race, and the race course. Knowing that today could be anyone's race out there brings a certain thrill that gets me pumped!

Athletic goals for 2013:
Not only do I want to make a repeat to Kona, but I want to win the Ironman World Championships.
