    GameTrailers Launches App on Xbox LIVE, Debuts New Original Series

    Written by: Daniel Kayser | March 19, 2013 at 9:09AM

    We here at GameTrailers are proud to announce the launch of our very own App on Xbox LIVE, which is available for download via the Xbox Live Marketplace beginning today. As the first Xbox LIVE app released from Viacom Media Networks, GameTrailers on Xbox LIVE delivers on demand access to the latest trailers from the world of gaming, up to date game reviews from the GT editorial staff, and all of our original in house produced shows such as GT.TV, Bonus Round, Pop Fiction, and Invisible Walls in addition to shows from our roster of third party partners such as Mega 64, ScrewAttack, Hey Ash and Angry Video Game Nerd.

    For those less inclined to search for shows, the app also features a linear channel of curated content from the GT staff which will allow users to watch a TV like “lean back” experience. This experience is available as soon as the app boots up, making it easy to jump right in to the latest and greatest GT content.

    In addition, on the content programming front, GameTrailers is debuting a new scripted series that users can see first on Xbox Live. The 12-part series is called Seedlings and is set in the world of Minecraft – which is the most popular game on Xbox Live. All episodes will debut on the GameTrailers app on Thursdays and then appear worldwide on GameTrailers.com and our iOS mobile app the following Monday. New episodes of Seedlings will be released every other week.

    GameTrailers on Xbox LIVE is available in the following countries:

    • USA
    • Mexico
    • Canada
    • UK
    • France
    • Germany
    • Spain
    • Italy
    • Japan
    • Australia

    So, boot up that Xbox 360 and download the all-new GameTrailers App! We're looking forward to delivering our most popular content on the Xbox LIVE platform, so be sure to check it out and let us know what you think!
