    Venue Details

    Directions

    ONLY 20 MINUTES FROM DOWNTOWN.

    Complimentary transportation to The Venue available from downtown Chicago! Please call (219) 473-6358 for more information.

    From O'Hare Airport - Chicago, IL
    I-190 East to I-90/94 East to Chicago Skyway - exit Indianapolis Blvd.

    From Midway Airport - Chicago, IL
    I-55 North to I-90/94 East to Chicago Skyway - exit Indianapolis Blvd.

    From downtown Chicago, IL
    I-90/94 East to Chicago Skyway - exit Indianapolis Blvd.

    From Illinois
    I-80/94 East to Calumet Ave North
    Or I-57 North to I-94 West to I-90 East (Chicago Skyway) - exit Indianapolis Blvd.

    From Indiana
    I-80/94 West to Calumet Ave North
    Or I-65 North to I-90 West to Calumet Ave North

    Parking

    Free self park. Valet and preferred parking available on site.

    Box Office Numbers

    For questions regarding a show, please call (219) 473-6351

    Box Office Hours

    The Venue at Horseshoe Casino's Box Office Hours of Operation:
    We will open at 2pm on show days and close 1 hour after the event starts.

    In addition the Gift Shop located on the first floor of the Pavilion next to the Pavilion Valet is available to purchase tickets from Sunday thru Thursday 11am  10pm and Friday to Saturday 11am  1am.

    The Box Office is located on the second floor of the casino across from the Village Buffet.

    Tickets can also be purchased at the Ticketmaster Kiosk located on the second floor of the casino across from the Village Buffet.

    Types of Payment Accepted

    Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, Cash

    Will Call

    Will Call tickets can be picked up after 2pm on the day of show at the Box Office. Customers must present the actual credit card, a picture ID and the confirmation number.

    Accessible Seating

    Our facilities are 100% ADA compliant. The Venue has accessible seating in the last row of the Floor Section, first row of the Orchestra and the last rows of the Mezzanine. It is important to indicate when buying tickets if you require wheelchair accessible seating. For more information or to purchase wheelchair accessible seating, please call the Gift Shop at 219-473-7000 ext 6351. Hours of operation are Sunday through Thursday 11am  10pm and Friday and Saturday 11am  1am.
    Service Animals are allowed but they must have their working papers with them. Please call 219.473.5928 to set this up in advance.

    Elevators: The Venue elevator has access to all three levels.

    Parking: Horseshoe Casino Marina and Pavilion parking garages both have accessible parking.

    Entrance: The entrances are ADA compliant.

    Hearing Devices: The Venue has hearing devices available at no charge. Please ask the box office for a device when you arrive. You must leave a Photo ID as a deposit for the device being returned at the end of the show.



    General Rules

    The Venue at Horseshoe Casino is a 21 years old and older facility and is located on the second floor of the Horseshoe Casino only 20 minutes from downtown Chicago. Photo ID's are required to enter the facilities. Cameras and recording devices are never allowed at concerts unless otherwise notified. Outside food or beverages of any kind are not permitted inside the building. Guests wearing obscene or indecent clothing are not allowed in The Venue. Guests using foul or abusive language or gestures will be escorted from the venue. Laser pens and pointers are not permitted. Policies subject to change depending on the nature of the event.

    Children Rules

    No children, The Venue at Horseshoe Casino is strictly a 21 and over facility, ID required.

    Miscellaneous

    MUST BE 21+ TO ATTEND ALL SHOWS AT THE VENUE
