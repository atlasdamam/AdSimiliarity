Welcome to Staybridge Suites Austin Arboretum

Plan an Extended Stay at Staybridge Suites in Austin, Texas

This hotel's Arboretum area accommodations offer convenient access to Mopac and downtown, as well as an appealing environment set apart from the city bustle. Extended stay hotels promise Austin, Texas travelers the comforts of home, and the Staybridge Suites® Hotel Austin Arboretum is no exception, offering all the amenities necessary for work and play.

If business is your pleasure, this hotel near IBM, Hewlett-Packard, Cisco and National Instruments is a perfect one. Guests can stay connected with complimentary wired and wireless Internet access. A 24-hour Business Center is available, too.

Are you visiting Austin, Texas for leisure? The hotel's location near the Arboretum and Domain shopping malls affords fashion-forward opportunities. Nearby Lake Travis offers a scenic getaway, as well, and downtown attractions include terrific entertainment options.

You're invited to "Get Comfortable" the moment you step through the doors of this Austin hotel's property. With the Arboretum for a backyard, a free daily breakfast and free laundry facilities available 24/7, you have an incredibly inviting home away from home. Stay active with the sports court, outdoor pool and fitness center, and then wind down at the Social Receptions with a free beverage and a light meal. Before turning in, stop by the Pantry for snacks and sundries. You can rest assured that your every need is covered.
