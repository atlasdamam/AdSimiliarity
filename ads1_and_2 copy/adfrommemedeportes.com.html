Your Kayako Fusion trial

To provide you with the best experience, your trial will be of our flagship, multichannel helpdesk - Kayako Fusion.

What do I get with the trial?

You have access to your own fully functioning helpdesk for a full (and free) 30 days, as well as unlimited access to our usual customer support channels.

What are staff users?

Staff users (also known as agents or operators) are your team members who login to your helpdesk to deliver customer support. All of our solutions allow you to support unlimited customers.

What happens after the trial?

You can upgrade your trial to a paid account at any point. Upgrading takes minutes. Near the end of the trial we will be in touch to see how things are going. If you upgrade, all your data and customizations will be saved.

Is my data safe?

Yes - we take the protection of your data and privacy very seriously. Our privacy policy is eTrust certified - a voluntary data privacy certification scheme.
