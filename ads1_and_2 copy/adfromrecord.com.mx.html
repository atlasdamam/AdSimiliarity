Features

  • Android™ 4.0

    Enjoy the most-loved features of Android – easy multitasking, customizable home screens and deep interactivity.

  • Get Android Apps on Google Play™

    Choose from hundreds of thousands of apps in Google Play to customize your phone and expand its potential.

  • 4.3" Durable Touch Screen

    Everything's better on the big screen — and this big, scratch-resistant screen delivers. Experience videos, apps, and pictures like never before.

  • QuickMemo™ to Capture, Create & Share Notes

    Snap a shot of any screen and use QuickMemo to take notes, doodle and more.

  • 720p HD Video Recording

    Capture the moment in high-def 720p to share with your social network.

  • 5MP Camera with LED Flash & VGA Front Facing

    Double your fun: front-facing camera lets you video chat, while 5MP rear-facing camera shoots crisp photos and videos.

  • Super Thin & Light (only 0.34" & 4.41oz)

    At only .34 inches thick and 4.41 oz., you'll wonder how they packed the power of Android™ 4.0 into this bad boy.

  • Wi-Fi & GPS Enabled

    Surf the web and get satellite-guided navigation from the palm of your hand. Hop on your nearest available Wi-Fi network to do it all, even faster, with the superior speed of Wi-Fi.

  • Stereo Bluetooth 3.0 Capable

    Listen to music and answer calls wirelessly, in stereo, when you pair with a Bluetooth® headset (sold separately).

  • Services: Boost Zone, Mobile ID

    Make your phone a reflection of who you are. Choose ID packs featuring apps, ringtones, wallpaper and more.

  • Certified

    A green machine: Certified by the independent UL Environment, this phone contains environmentally preferable materials with a minimal environmental impact.
