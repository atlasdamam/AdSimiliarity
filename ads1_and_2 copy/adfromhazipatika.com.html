College of Health Professions Nursing Degrees

Gain academic credentials and proficiency with an online nursing degree

To succeed in today’s competitive nursing industry, you need a degree from a credible, nationally respected university. Western Governors University is an accredited online university offering rigorous online nursing programs that allow you to achieve your educational goals without altering your life.

Online Nursing Degrees

Explore the nursing degree options offered at WGU:

BSN Programs:

  • B.S. Nursing (RN to BSN online)
  • B.S. Nursing (Prelicensure)

MSN Programs:

  • M.S. Nursing—Education (for RNs with BSNs)
  • M.S. Nursing—Leadership and Management (for RNs with BSNs)
  • M.S. Nursing Education (RN to MSN Option)
  • M.S. Nursing Leadership and Management (RN to MSN Option)

Under the guidance of a dedicated, knowledgeable mentor, you can earn your degree on your schedule and within your budget. WGU gives you the flexibility to balance work and family while finishing your degree.

CCNE Accredited Nursing Programs

The bachelor’s and master’s programs at WGU are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (One Dupont Circle, NW, Suite 530, Washington DC 20036, 202-887-6791).

Learn more about CCNE accreditation.
