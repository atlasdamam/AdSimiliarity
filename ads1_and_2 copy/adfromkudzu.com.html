What We Do
Since 1978, Reliable has been serving all of Metro Atlanta's heating & cooling needs and can now handle all plumbing, electrical and installation all types of thermal insulation in attics and crawlspaces. We're a local, family owned and operated company - big enough to provide a wide range of services and the highest quality products, but small enough to offer first-class service with a personal touch. We are owned and operated by the father and son team of Dan and Daniel Jape and you can speak to either anytime you are in need of service. Dan's cell number is 404 626-2344 and his home number is 770 924-5811. His son, Daniels cell number is 404 790-8082. Reliable is a Trane Factory Authorized Comfort Specialist Dealer that specializes in energy upgrades and comfort enhancements. We are proud to recommend and install Rheem tanked water heaters along with the super high efficient tankless models. We offer authorized service and repair and all brands of plumbing, HVAC and electrical systems.

When you need service on your plumbing, electrical and heating and cooling systems, or need to replace your current equipment, give us a call, and you will see why thousands of your neighbors have chosen us to be their contractor of choice.

Our professional, experienced and highly trained service department offers 24 hour emergency service, routine service and yearly maintenance contracts. We can repair and service all brands of faucets and plumbing fixtures, as well as handle any type of electrical issue you may have. Reliable' s HVAC installation department, plumbing department and electricians are known for quality work installing replacement systems, as well as new construction on custom homes, and remodels. We offer emergency, fast replacement of water heaters 24 hours a day with same day service available when needed.

All service technicians and installers at Reliable are EPA certified to handle refrigerant and are trained both in house and by factory personnel. Our Reliable plumbers hold either a Master's license or a Journeyman's license from the State of Georgia as do our electricians. The average Reliable technician has over 15 years' experience in their respective industry and is either NATE certified or currently enrolled in NATE certification classes. Reliable is proud to be certified a NATE Company, which assures you are getting the best of the best in the industry.

Our technicians use state-of-the art tools and testing equipment including refrigerant recovery equipment to protect the environment. Our plumbers have inspection cameras to find and repair plumbing leaks and stoppages with minimal ground or driveway disturbance. We use both small portable sewer line machines that can be brought into an attics as well as extremely powerful machines to chew through tough clogs and tree routes when needed.
With a fleet of 85 vehicles and over 100 employees, Reliable is able to provide you with timely, professional service. Reliable employees are fully bonded, insured and covered by Workers Compensation. The knowledgeable office staff is friendly, efficient, and standing by to assist you when you call.

Reliable provides service and repair for residential and light commercial clients. We specialize in remodel, service, repair and basement finishing. Reliable is actually open 7 days a week and when you call on weekends and evenings, you will speak to a live Reliable employee and not an answering service or a machine. Call 770 594-9969 today for more information!

    Credentials
    Licensed for commercial and residential HVAC, plumbing & electrical services. Nate Certified Company. Dan Jape past president of the Conditioned Air Contractors. Served on the Lennox Dealer Advisory Council. Member of Conditioned Air Association of Georgia. Atlanta Gas Light Natural Gas Advantage Dealer. Trane Comfort Specialist and Titanium Club dealer. Reliable is member in good standing with the National Comfort Institute and employees BPI certified auditors


    Going Green
    Reliable Heating & Air sells Energy Star rated equipment and recycles several tons of metal, steel, aluminum, copper, and cardboard each week. All r-22 (Freon) is recovered and turned into an EPA reclamation facility. All new equipment we sell and install uses the environmentally friendly refrigerant R410a. Our technicians are EPA certified and trained in the proper handling and disposal of the HCFC R-22 (Freon).
