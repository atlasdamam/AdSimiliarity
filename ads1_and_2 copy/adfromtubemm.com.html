TV Your Way

XFINITY gives you TV your way so you get the best entertainment experience. With XFINITY On Demand, you get thousands of TV shows, movies and more  on TV and streaming online  and 90% of them are free.

XFINITY is also your home for the most live sports. You get access to your favorite sports all year long anytime, anywhere. Watch live in HD on your big screen TV or stream ESPN live on your PC, tablet or smartphone.

The Fastest Internet

XFINITY delivers the fastest Internet and now the fastest in-home Wi-Fi so you'll have the speed you need to connect all of your devices at the same time. Stream, download and share in a flash.

Your XFINITY Internet subscription includes Constant Guard®  the most comprehensive online protection of any major Internet provider. It's a $360 value included with XFINITY Internet service for no additional cost. Constant Guard includes top-rated Norton Security Suite, identity theft protection and more so you can enjoy greater peace of mind while surfing, shopping and banking online.

More Ways to Connect and Save

XFINITY Voice gives you more ways to connect and more ways to save. Not only do you get reliable phone service, but you also get unlimited nationwide talk and text  so you can save on your wireless bill too. Simply use the free XFINITY Connect app to text from your smartphone, iPad® or iPod touch® and skip using your wireless text plan

It's easy to switch  you can even keep your current home phone number. And, you'll get the popular features you expect like Call Waiting, 3-Way Calling and even voicemail, plus features you might not expect like Caller ID on your TV, laptop, and smartphone and voicemail you can read as an email.
