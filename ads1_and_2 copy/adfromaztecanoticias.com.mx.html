FEATURED PRODUCTS
WESCAM 474HD™
The WESCAM 474HD™ is a high altitude version of the (now renamed) Sonoma 474™ turret. With increased optical performance, environmental management for stratospheric operations, world-class stabilization, and geo-location, the WESCAM 474™ is an essential tool for long-range covert operations.  With Visible, SWIR, and MWIR sensors, dismounts cannot hide through a variety of atmospheric conditions.  Active sensors are available as options.		 WESCAM Eagle™
The, WESCAM Eagle™ has been significantly upgraded, with new Visible, SWIR and MWIR sensors, providing a dramatic boost to capabilities in a variety of atmospheric conditions.  The system is optimized for long duration operations, whether positioned on towers or rooftops.  The WESCAM Eagle™ features the excellent stabilization of the X-MAST™ optical bench, with large shared aperture optics, providing jitter – free high resolution images of airborne objects of interest.
