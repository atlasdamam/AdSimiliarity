Disney Deluxe Resorts

 

Conveniently Close to the Magic, Far Beyond Compare

Discover the comfort and convenience of staying at a Disney Deluxe Resort. Located close to all the magic of the theme parks, Disney Deluxe Resorts are designed to deliver an unforgettable experience at Walt Disney World Resort. Each Disney Deluxe Resort is uniquely themed with distinctive style and architecture—and offers a variety of benefits, amenities and services that will make your Disney vacation extra special.

Enjoy near-perfect proximity to Epcot at Disney's BoardWalk Inn. Savor an elegant escape at Disney's Grand Floridian Resort & Spa. Or delight in the ultra-modern design of Disney's Contemporary Resort. Whichever Disney Deluxe Resort you choose, you can look forward to a soothing escape from the energy of the theme parks—and from the everyday—even though you're mere moments away.

During your stay, you may enjoy outstanding amenities and signature Disney services, including:

  • Transportation options throughout Walt Disney World Resort and access to the theme parks via motorcoach and, at some locations, monorail and water launch services
  • Spacious rooms that are typically larger than those at other Disney Resort hotels
  • Concierge services to assist you with your vacation planning
  • A variety of dining experiences—including Signature Dining, Character Dining and room service
  • On-site recreation choices, like bicycle rentals, watercraft rentals, pools, arcades and health clubs
  • Valet parking, available for a fee

Enjoy these great benefits when you stay at a Disney Deluxe Resort. Plus, take advantage of the perks available to Guests staying at all Disney Resort hotels, including:

  • Complimentary parking and transportation throughout Walt Disney World Resort—giving you the flexibility to come and go from the parks as you please (valid park admission required)—and complimentary transportation to and from Orlando International Airport via Disney's Magical Express.
  • Extra Magic Hours—each day, one of the parks offers extended hours, so you can enjoy select attractions (valid park admission and Resort ID required).
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi Internet in your room and most public spaces at Disney Resort Hotels

With so many benefits for Guests at Disney Resort Hotels, it's easy to enjoy an unforgettable stay at Walt Disney World Resort.

View the list of Disney Deluxe Resorts to explore all they have to offer. Be sure to bookmark this page so you can return to it when you're ready to take advantage of this great deal.
