Believe in a beaded bracelet

A RHINO FORCE bracelet is made up of white, red and black beads separated by a coconut bead. The black and white beads represent the black and white rhinos, and the red beads symbolize their plight. The coconut bead represents the rhinos colour and tough exterior.

Many years ago beads were used as currency to trade slaves and many years later beads became expressions of beautiful African art and jewellery. Now beads are saving lives. A RHINO FORCE bracelet delivers a message and donates funds to save our rhino. ARE YOU WEARING YOURS?
