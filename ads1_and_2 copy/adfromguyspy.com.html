Description

PEARL – proudly presented by GuySpy.com, is the premiere mobile app for all global LGBT Pride events and Circuit Parties, as well as associated parades and activities. Keep up to date on what’s happening when, build and maintain your schedule, share with your social networks, and upload, view and share user photos.


Features include:
•Event and location listings including full schedules, maps, branding and sponsorship info.
•Abilility to build personal schedule and set personal reminders
•Tweet from within the app , check-in and post right to Facebook
•Push messaging capabilities for changes, updates or time sensitive information
•Xomo mobile live experience- geo tagged photos and real-time text from locales
