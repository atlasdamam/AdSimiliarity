Portada´s Latin Content Marketing Forum

Portada’s Latin Content Marketing Forum will take place on June 4th, 2013 in the just renovated Intercontinental Hotel in Miami. The Latin Content Marketing Forum will analyze the enormous role content marketing can play in the Latin (Latin America and U.S. Hispanic) marketing space, which belongs to the fastest growing markets in the world. The Forum can be sponsored and attended in combination with Portada’s 2013 Latam Advertising and Media Summit which takes place at the Intercontinental Hotel, located on 100 Chopin Plaza, Miami, FL, 33131, in the afternoon of June 4th and the full day of June of 5th.
