Blackberry® Mobile Trader Download Instructions

To download to your BlackBerry:

  1. Download the application for your Blackberry: Blackberry (touchscreen) or
    Blackberry (without touchscreen).
  2. Launch the BlackBerry Desktop Manager.
  3. Click "Application Loader."
  4. Connect your BlackBerry to your computer with a USB cable.
  5. Follow the installation wizard to find and select the "AMERITRADE.alx" file. This is the file you unzipped in Step 1.
  6. If installed correctly, you will now have a TD Ameritrade icon in your applications folder.
  7. Click the icon to launch TD Ameritrade Mobile Trader.
