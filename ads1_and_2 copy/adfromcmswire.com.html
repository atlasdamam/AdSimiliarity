Responsive Web Design

Can responsive design help you engage your customers?

In today’s world, your customers can be anywhere, and on any device when they are trying to connect with you. They expect that you will know who they are, their preferences and respond to them immediately. That’s why it’s never been more important for your organization to have a responsive web experience that provides a consistent, contextualized, and content-centric customer experience. But is your organization up to the challenge of delivering it?

Join Part I of The Art of Customer Experience webinar series, Optimize Your Customer Experience Through Responsive Web Design, on June 4, 2013, featuring a special introduction from OpenText Chief Marketing Officer Kevin Cochrane discussing the importance of Customer Experience Management (CEM) in Enterprise Information Management. You will also hear from CEM experts from OpenText and guest speaker Senior Analyst David Aponovich from Forrester Research Inc., as they look at the changing trends in Digital Experience, and how to use responsive design to make your content contribution and consumption simpler than ever before.

Register today to hear them discuss the importance of:

  • Integrated, real time digital marketing strategy
  • A flexible and rapid response web experience management platform
  • Delivering tailored, content-centric experiences that cater to each unique consumer
