Customer Reviews

AWESOME!
     
by Salon Rituals

I was lucky enough to obtain a copy of this album in its promotional phase and I can honestly say its my favorite! In fact it's the only one I've listened to for the last couple months. I love the blend of country, rock and hip-hop. Every song is alive with energy and when I'm feeling dumpy it just lifts my spirits! I love the entire album from start to finish. My personal favorite is "Time To Get Right", as it holds a special place in my heart for a special person. Close second is the first track just downright catchy. Trust me when I say you won't regret purchasing this album. Great addition to any collection.
