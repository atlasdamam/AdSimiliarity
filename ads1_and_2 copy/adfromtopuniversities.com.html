Chemical Engineering
Home » Technion-Israel Institute of Technology » Chemical Engineering

The 15 month Systems Engineering Master program is designed for engineers from various disciplines interested in expanding upon their existing analytical abilities.

The program teaches engineers to lead complex projects utilizing many components and incorporating a wide range of specification and utilizes interdisciplinary skills and tools as needed in today’s working environment.

Some of the practical skills students will earn are:
  • Ability to analyze systems
  • Ability to asses a technological systems reliability through analysis
  • Ability to plan and engage in strategic decision
  • Understanding product lifetime testing
  • Familiarity with planning and designing control systems
  • Ability to analyze and define requirements of software intensive systems
  • Ability to combine operator/user needs
  • Understanding of quantitative models
  • Knowledge of the various administrative aspects of complex projects
  • Understanding of economic models
