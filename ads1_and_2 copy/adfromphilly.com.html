2013 LIVESTRONG Challenge Philly

Ride, Run, or Walk with Team LIVESTRONG on August 17-18 in Philadelphia, PA and join us in the fight against cancer. Whether you ride, run or walk, your participation helps raise awareness and valuable funds that improve the lives of those affected by cancer. Join us in Philly as we stand up for everyone who has ever had to say or hear the words "I have cancer", and let's fight so they never have to again.

August 17, 2013- 10K/5K Run or Walk
August 18, 2013- Beginner, Intermediate I, Intermediate II, Experienced Ride
