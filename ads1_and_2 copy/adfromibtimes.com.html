    IBM Flex System

    Learn why Clabby Analytics believes its
    the best blade offering available





    IBM Flex System is a revolutionary step forward in blade server-based IT infrastructure  a clean-slate design for todays increasingly virtualized, heterogeneous data centers. Offering the ability to custom build and tune a configuration to meet your specific requirements, its one of the most advanced blade servers available today. Featuring Intel® Xeon® processors, IBM Flex System is the right fit for your IT infrastructure.1

    • Simplicity. IBM Flex System is designed from the ground-up for fast, simple integration with a single management tool for all physical and virtual resources. Easily integrate compute, storage, and networking to support your most demanding IT solutions.

    • Flexibility. Take advantage of a flexible and open choice of architectures, hypervisors, operating systems, storage, and networking technologies  all within the same chassis.

    • Control. IBM Flex System Manager provides single-pane-of-glass management over the entire system via management integration of physical and virtual compute, storage and networking resources, from a single vantage point, enabling you to automate administration functions across multiple, heterogeneous resources.2

    With IBM Flex System, the elements that make up IBM PureFlex System, you can build your own system to meet your unique IT requirements with a set of no-compromise compute, storage, networking and systems management components.

    Custom-build infrastructure to your requirements

    You need an infrastructure solution that goes beyond blade servers with a flexible architecture to support your business needs today, but also designed to support multiple generations of future technologies. You need simple, integrated management to keep operational costs down, but you also want a no-compromise design with long-term investment protection.

    Starting with an innovative chassis designed for new levels of simplicity, reliability, and upgradability, IBM Flex System can help you go beyond blade servers. With a broad range of x86 and IBM POWER compute nodes, the Flex System V7000 storage node, enhanced networking capabilities and sophisticated system management capabilities, you can upgrade your existing blade server infrastructure and make your IT simpler, more flexible, more open, and more efficient.

    Go beyond blade servers with IBM Flex System

    • Compute nodes
    • Networking capabilities
    • Storage capabilities
    • Management integration

    Compute nodes that go beyond blade servers

    • POWER and x86 options from price-optimized entry nodes to nodes providing maximum performance for your most demanding applications
    • Available with the latest Intel processors and industry-leading memory capability for x86 based systems - up to 50 percent more memory than HP1

    Networking capabilities designed for virtualization and cloud

    • Flex System Fabric, designed to help simplify connectivity of your LAN and SAN infrastructure
    • Ethernet options to fit into your current infrastructure while supporting next generation capabilities - up to 200 percent more networking bandwidth than HP3
    • Fibre Channel switches ranging from low-cost 8Gb solutions to highly scalable 16Gb switches for maximum performance
    • InfiniBand switches and adapters to support the bandwidth and latency requirements for high performance computing and financial trading environments

    Storage capabilities optimized for efficiency and performance

    • Up to 300 percent more dedicated storage for compute nodes compared to Dell2
    • Real-time Compression on the Flex System v7000 reduces capacity needs and IBM Easy Tier automatically optimizes data on the tiered storage in the node

    Management integration with IBM Flex System Manager

    • Simple user interface and automation capabilities helps you bring new components online faster
    • Cross-resource integration to manage applications and workloads instead of components
