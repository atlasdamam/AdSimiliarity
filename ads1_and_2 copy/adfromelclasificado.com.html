Published on May 22, 2013

Under the band name Duncan Dhu, the band presented hits like En Algún Lugar, which is now considered an 80s classic and a must-have for playlists of that follow that genre.

Exit Son Locuaz. Enter Vim Furor.

Written by Angel Aguilar
Published on May 22, 2013

I was introduced to Vim Furor during a show at the Hollywood Park Casino last April. Slammed between two ska acts, the band blew me away with its in-your-face approach to rock & roll. Come to find out, the band is made up of bassist Rei Hernandez, drummer Roman Lopez, and guitarist/lead vocalist German Briseño, all of whom previously performed together under the band name of Son Locuaz. But a marketing move of sorts would force the Latin rock band to change its name to one more fitting.

About Al Borde

Al Borde is the leading cutting edge Latin Alternative media company in the US. We are dedicated to reporting and brining to light the unique and creative lifestyle of the young Hispanic population. Since the major television networks, radio stations and websites rarely cover the lifestyle of truly "alternative" trend-setters, we figured we would speak on their behalf! :) read more
