Lineup and Details for Loose Cannon Presents Olly Murs (Live) on Fri 7th June 2013

Loose Cannon Events Management

proudly presents //

Olly Murs (Full Live Show)

+ Special Guest AMELIA LILY & Diana Vickers

--------------------------------------

It is with great pleasure to announce Loose Cannon's first major concert in association with Middlesbrough Council & Love Middlesbrough. Our first guest comes in the form of Olly Murs, when we first sat down to discuss potential artists to launch we could only dream that we could land somebody of Olly's calibre. Olly is a house hold name who first shot to fame after making the final of X-Factor 2009. The cheeky chap then went away and focused on his own sound and came back with a no1 debut album. Olly now has a string of number ones under his belt and is set to support Robbie Williams as well as sell out solo tours. That and now famed for presenting the Xtra Factor on ITV2. Olly is one of a kind in that he appeals to everyone from mums to cool kids.

Loose Cannon Events management are very excited to be working alongside Middlesbrough council to deliver high quality music events that are sure to attract people from all over the North East. We are also pleased to team up with Mela with production and site share which will take place 8th & 9th june on the same site.
