Getting Started
Simple, Easy Interface and No Platform Fees*

Trade Architect puts all the data and research you need right there in front of you. Its intuitive interface lets you focus on mastering your trades, not mastering your trading platform. Plus, you don’t have to worry about platform fees—because there aren’t any.

Hear Everything. Except the Noise.

With tools like Trade Architect’s Integrated Research, you can get a consensus of what third-party analysts are saying about a stock, all in one simplified chart. Get all the info you need to trade, then, get right to trading.

Make it Your Own

Not only can you customize Trade Architect’s look, you can customize the information to fit your specific needs. Add tools like Earnings Analysis, which shows you how a company’s past estimates and actual earnings differed, in one simple chart.
