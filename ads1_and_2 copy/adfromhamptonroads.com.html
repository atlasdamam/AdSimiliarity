SoakItUp

Page Content

Every visit to the Virginia Aquarium is an amazing opportunity to uncover new worlds of imagination and learning for you and your child. Meet Shelby the Loggerhead Turtle, Navy the Harbor Seal, Finn the shark, Ray the Cownose Ray, Spike the Pufferfish and 30,000 of their friends as they take you on magical aquatic adventures you won’t soon forget.

Whether it’s a unique interaction with creatures from the deep, a special moment shared between you and your little critters, or a once-in-a-lifetime encounter beyond the glass, Soak it Up!

The Virginia Aquarium ‘Soak It Up’ app is now available for free download on the iTunes app store and at the Google Play app store.
