Rules & Regulations

Sign up for a local tournament in your area

The National Family Tennis Championships (NFTC) could take you across the country, but it all starts in your neighborhood with your local tournament. There are six (6) divisions to choose from:  Mother/Daughter; Mother/Son; Father/Daughter; Father/Son; Brother/Sister; Husband/Wife

There are no age limitations in the Brother/Sister or the Husband/Wife divisions.  See Player Rules and Regulations for more information.

Search for local tournaments by facility name, division, city, state, or zip code. To sign up your team, just click "Register" next to a search result, fill out the player details, and add the tournament to your cart.  No local tournament in your area, contact your club to host. There may still be time.
