TiVo® Mini

Small footprint.
Big entertainment.

The new TiVo Mini delivers live TV, recorded shows and web entertainment to another TV in your house.

The TiVo Mini conveniently allows you to enjoy all the entertainment from your TiVo Premiere 4 or Premiere XL4/Elite DVR in another room through your home network (Ethernet or MoCA setup only. Wireless setup not supported).

  • Watch live TV and recorded shows from your 4-tuner TiVo DVR
  • Access movies and music from Hulu Plus, YouTube™, Pandora®, Spotify and more*
  • Schedule recordings, WishList® searches and Season Pass® recordings

The TiVo Mini requires the use of a dedicated tuner on your 4-tuner DVR to stream live TV. Mini does not work with TiVo Premiere or Premiere XL.

Setting up your TiVo Mini

To stream live or recorded television to your TiVo Mini, connect your TiVo Premiere 4 or XL4/Elite DVR to the Internet using one of the following:

  • Wired Ethernet
  • Ethernet over coax (MoCA)

If your Premiere 4/XL4/Elite uses a wireless adapter, it will not be able to stream to TiVo Mini. Consider connecting using an Ethernet over coax (MoCA) network. Learn more

If your TiVo Premiere 4 or XL4/Elite DVR is connected via wired Ethernet

If your house is wired for Ethernet, you're all set! Just connect your TiVo Mini to your TV and enjoy.

If your TiVo Premiere 4 or XL4/Elite DVR is connected to your router

You're all set! Just connect your TiVo Mini to your other TV and enjoy.

If your TiVo Premiere 4 or XL4/Elite DVR cannot reach your router

If your TiVo Premiere 4 or XL4/Elite DVR cannot reach your router or it currently uses a wireless adapter, consider using a MoCA Network Adapter to create a simple Ethernet over Coax (MoCA) network.

A MoCA Network Adapter "bridges" the connection between an Ethernet network and a MoCA network so that you can access high-speed internet in any room in your house that has a cable outlet.
