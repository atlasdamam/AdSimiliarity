ESQ One™

Meet the One. 1 great watch – 12 cool colors.

An eye-catching watch of unisex appeal that’s cool, colorful, fashionable and fun, the ESQ One is also Swiss quartz precise and water resistant to 10 ATM. Available in a wide array of tempting colors, it features a clean Arabic/stick dial, 42.5 mm silicone-wrapped steel case with screw-down polycarbonate crown, and soft silicone strap with unique logo-button keeper.
