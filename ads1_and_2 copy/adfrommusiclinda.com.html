What's Pep Boys Glovebox?

It's the electronic equivalent of that pile of oil stained receipts and disorganized mechanics' quotes in your real glovebox. It allows you to:
  • Keep track of all your car's information in one place
  • Access your vehicle's service history records in a click
  • Easily find the parts you need for your car's specific make and model
  • Get online access to your manufacturer's suggested maintenance schedule
  • Stay on top of recall notices for your vehicle
  • Receive exclusive offers sent straight to your inbox
Knowing exactly where to find all your vehicle's information makes it easy to keep your car in tip-top shape. Close [x]

Do I need new tires?
Tires are an investment in your security and peace of mind. Pep Boys gives you the facts you need to make that investment confidently.
