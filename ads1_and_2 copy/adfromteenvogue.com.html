Learn the facts, pass it on!

Texting and driving takes your eyes off the road for an average of five seconds. At 55 miles per hour, that's like driving the length of a football field - completely blind!

*Source: Virginia Tech Transportation Institute.

Tip to Drive by

Texts and calls may seem urgent, but your friends and family will be glad you put safety first and waited until you were off the road to answer.

Eyes up- checking social media, e-mail, apps, using your phone's music features, and even glancing at your caller ID takes your mind off driving and eyes off the road.
