TELL SACRAMENTO TO SAY NO TO NEW FOOD AND BEVERAGE TAXES!

Right now, Sacramento politicians are considering Senate Bill 622—a proposal to tax the distribution of beverages like soda, juice drinks and sports drinks, and enhanced waters containing any sugar.

If passed, this bill would raise the prices of more than 350 of your favorite products - click HERE to see the list.

Sacramento should be focused on protecting and creating jobs, not putting them in jeopardy with another tax. This is the worst time to ask working families to pay more at the grocery store. That’s why we’ve come together to form Californians for Food and Beverage Choice. Join us today by filling out the form on the right.
