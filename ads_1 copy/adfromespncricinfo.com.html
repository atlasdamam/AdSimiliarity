Players to Watch Out For

Sunil Narine $11.5m Bowler

With his continuous variations, Sunil Narine has been a mystery still to be cracked. His guile has saved some face for the otherwise struggling Kolkata side.

Dwayne Bravo $9m All Rounder

One of the best all-rounders, Bravo has held the Chennai middle order with flamboyant batting, provided crucial breakthroughs with his change of pace and displayed some outstanding athletic fielding.

Rohit Sharma $10.5m Batsman

In the flopping star-studded Mumbai line-up, Rohit Sharma has held his own, with scintillating performances. Exhibits lazy elegance with the bat and has also shown that captaincy is not an added burden.
