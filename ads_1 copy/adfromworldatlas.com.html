BankAmericard Travel Rewards credit card

BankAmericard Travel Rewards® credit card
gives you the flexibility to use points to pay for
more than just flights. Use them for any hotel or
any car rental with no restrictions.

  • No annual fee
  • Earn 1.5 points for every dollar spent on purchases
  • No limit to the number of points you can earn and points
    don’t expire
  • No Foreign Transaction Fees
  • Receive 10,000 bonus points after you make at least $500 in purchases in the first 90 days

 

For information about rates, fees, other costs and benefits associated with the use of this card, see Terms and Conditions accompanying application. This credit card program is issued and administered by FIA Card Services, N.A.

*If the primary applicant has a checking, savings or retirement account with Bank of America® or any account with Merrill Lynch or U.S. Trust, the card account will receive an annual bonus points award of 10% of the amount of net purchases (or 1 bonus point for every $10) made with the card during the year.
