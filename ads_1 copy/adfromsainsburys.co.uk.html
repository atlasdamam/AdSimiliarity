Cashback Low Rate Credit Card

 

To keep your promotional rate you must pay at least the minimum payment by the due date and stay within your credit limit.

These products are available to new Sainsbury's Bank credit card customers only. If you are an existing customer please call us on 0500 40 50 60 and we will let you know the options available to you.

Refunded purchases and cash advance transactions (as defined in the Terms & Conditions of your account), including foreign currency purchases, are not eligible for cashback or Nectar points.

†As we treat all our customers and their applications on an individual basis, the APR and the interest rate we offer are determined by the details you supply, along with an independent verification of your credit and repayment history. Find out more about personal pricing.

#When you pay with your Sainsbury's Nectar Credit Card and swipe your Nectar card. Introductory 10 point offer capped at £1,000 spend per month. Points differ on Sainsbury's fuel - Find out more.

*For each of the first three months from account opening, we will give you a rebate of 5% of the value of all eligible purchases at Sainsbury's (once any purchases refunded in the period have been deducted) on your account. The rebates will be shown on the statement in the following month, up to a maximum of £50 per month. Cashback payment is free from income tax.

**For any statement period in which you: a) make at least £250 of purchases at Sainsbury's (once any purchases refunded in that period have been deducted); and b) make at least £250 of purchases elsewhere (once any purchases refunded in that period have been deducted), you will qualify for £5 credit to your account in the following statement period.

Credit available subject to status to UK residents aged 18 or over from Sainsbury's Bank Plc, Pitreavie Business Park, Dunfermline, Fife, KY99 4BS.
