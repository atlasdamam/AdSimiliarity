Dealer Profile: Barloworld Volkswagen Used

Two convenient locations in Melbourne Excellent range of new, demo and preowned vehicles Finance and insurance advice and on-site approval Modern facilities built to Volkswagen's international design standards Extended warranty available on approved preowned vehicles Barloworld Volkswagen is committed to assisting you find and look after the vehicle that best suits your lifestyle. Conveniently situated with locations in Melbourne - Glen Waverley and Moorabbin - our showrooms offer a relaxed environment to view Volkswagen's great new models and accessories. Our modern comfortable Service lounge facilities enable you to use your laptop or get up to-date with the day's news and enjoy a great coffee should you choose to wait while our highly trained technicians service your car. We also have a large range of quality preowned and low kilometer demonstrator cars on site with dealer warranties.
