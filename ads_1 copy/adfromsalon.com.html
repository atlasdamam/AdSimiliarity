Your Choice Auto® Insurance options

Quote Online, Find an Agent or Call 1.866.621.6900

Want even better protection against the unexpected? Want to be rewarded for your safe driving habits? Make the Hands On ® choice. With Your Choice Auto®, you can enjoy Allstate's high-quality auto insurance coverage with exclusive rewards when you avoid accidents, and more protection when you can't.

When you choose Your Choice Auto® from Allstate:

  • Your rates won't go up just because of an accident*
  • You'll get $100 off your collision deductible the day you sign up
  • You'll earn a check** of up to 5% for every six months of accident-free driving
  • You get a check for a new car (not just the depreciated value) if you total a brand new car within the first 3 model years. (New car expanded protection is an optional coverage available if you buy a Your Choice Auto® (YCA) package. It is not included in the core YCA coverage. Talk to your agent about further details.)*

Only Your Choice Auto® offers these car insurance additional options starting the day you sign up. Get an easy online quote or speak to an Allstate agent to see how you can make the most of your auto insurance coverage.
