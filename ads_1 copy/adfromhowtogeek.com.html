The best way for you to determine if you are a candidate for LASIK is with a personal, one-on-one consultation with an experienced, licensed TLC doctor.

LASIK can treat people who:

  • Are at least 18 years old
  • Are not pregnant or nursing
  • Are in good general health
  • Have a stable prescription
  • Have no eye diseases

At TLC, we realize that it's confusing to hear a variety of answers for a medical procedure, so keep these points in mind:

  • Prices vary based on your doctor, your prescription and the technology.
  • Find out if the price quoted includes all of the pre- and post-surgery follow up exams such as lifetime commitment.
  • The best way to determine your cost of LASIK is to schedule a FREE Consultation. Your cost is based on the procedure that is best suited for your eyes.
