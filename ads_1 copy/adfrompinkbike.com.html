Park City, Utah, is the premier
mountain biking destination
in the world.

In fact, it’s the world’s first, and so far only, Gold Level Ride Center Destination, according to the leading authority for the sport, the International Mountain Biking Association – IMBA.

With more than 400 miles of Gold Level trails, 100 bars and restaurants, world class accommodations, and a mini amusement park with mountain coasters, alpine slides, and more for kids of all ages - Park City has something for the whole family.

All the information you need to plan your Park City mountain bike trip is here at your fingertips. Arrange your stay, rent a bike, book a guide, and then hit the trails. Experiencing the world’s only Gold Level Ride Center has never been easier. And there’s never been a better time than now.
