Biz Press Offices - Premier Publishing Platform (P3)


This premier reputation management and online content generation publishing tool allows you to:
  • Easily and tactically manage your company information on the web
  • Manage a professional company or brand presence online
  • Increase search engine ranking in regional and global media via a regular publishing platform
  • Be seen among key blue-chip companies in your industry
  • Position your company and clients as industry opinion leaders
  • Steer issues in your industry
  • Publish brand content and brand stories
  • Make use of our bizContent and advisory services
  • Have your content seen in the relevant Bizcommunity industry newsletters and portals of your choice
View all our Biz Press Office holders.

A Biz Press Office includes:
  • Twelve (12) press office credits for use during the year, valid for either press releases or job ads.
  • Press releases distributed through the daily and weekly email newsletter system and on the portal homepage, within 1-3 sections/categories most relevant to the topic (such as Branding, Advertising or Research), and are also archived permanently in the Biz Press Office area containing all your company's news.
  • Your company logo appearing in the left-hand margin of the site each time a press release is published, linked to your Biz Press Office.
  • Design and set-up of the Biz Press Office, including the news, profile and contact pages, with a link to your website.
  • Five (5) historic press releases in the Biz Press Office news area before launch.
  • Monthly Biz Press Office stats via email each month, which includes the number of readers opening each release.
  • Being listed upfront in Bizcommunity's business directory
  • Being listed upfront in search results when readers search companies or press releases by keyword (such as ad agency, branding, etc).
  • Upon renewal of a Press Office term, unused credits are carried over in addition to the newly allocated 12 credits, but an account may only carry up to a maximum of 24 credits. If more credits are needed, they may either be purchased online through your press office management screen, or ordered through our Sales Department.
  • If you run out of credits before the end of the year, you have the option to order more.
  • One release published in one newsletter uses up one credit. If your release is relevant to more than one of our industry sections, you may also cross-post it into those industries, bearing in mind the one-credit-per-industry cost.

Press office credits

  • Additional press release/credit costs R1 015 (excl VAT) per press release/credit

Once-off press releases

  • Non-press office holders may purchase a once-off press release.
  • A once-off press release costs R1 815 (excl VAT) per press release

Additional industry listing

  • Ideal for companies that do business in more than one industry.
  • An additional charge of R2,250 ex. Vat per year to list yourself in one additional industry.
  • There is no limit to how many industries you would like to list your business in.

Press office recruitment benefits

  • This works on a credit system only.
  • For every new annual Press Office Package contract, you receive 12 press releases.
  • One credit can also act as a recruitment credit if not used for a press release.

Multi-brand press office

We have a multiple-brand press-office option which offers exactly the same benefits as our Biz Press Office, except created to bridge the gap between a company's different brands and incorporate the brands under the main company's Press Office.

This means the Main Press Office is for the Mothership Company; for the each different brand, we create additional Brand Profile pages, and also include three (3) press releases per brand.

The cost of each additional brand is as follows:

Mothership Press Office is R15 200 per annum with 12 press releases.

1 x additional brand - R4 266 (plus 3 press releases)
2 x additional brands - R7 236 (plus 3 press releases per brand)
3 x additional brands - R10 935 (plus 3 press releases per brand)
4 x additional brands - R12 776 (plus 3 press releases per brand)
Thereafter, it becomes R1 826 per brand

All prices exclude VAT
All costs include agency commission
