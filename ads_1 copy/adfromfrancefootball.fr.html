Deductible Saving BenefitSM for Good Drivers

Automatically earn a $50 credit toward your deductible each year your policy is claim-free. That credit can build to $250, which you can use toward your deductible for a covered Collision or Comprehensive claim.1

Replacement Cost for Total Loss on New Cars

If your new car is totaled within the first year of purchase or first 15,000 miles, whichever comes first, you’re covered with no deduction for depreciation.2, 3, 5

Replacement Cost for Special Parts

Receive the non-depreciated replacement cost of certain damaged parts, such as tires, brakes, battery and shocks, with no deduction for wear and tear present at the time of a covered accident.2, 3, 4, 5

Enhanced Rental Car Damage Coverage

If you rent a car and are involved in an accident, MetLife Auto & Home will protect you from incurring additional fees and expenses charged by the rental company.5
