Capable & even more efficient.

Best-in-Class
fuel economy+

The new 3.0L EcoDiesel V6 engine (late availability) delivers clean-diesel technology with low CO2 emissions. The clean and refined powertrain delivers optimum efficiency with awe-inspiring performance. Combined with the advanced eight-speed automatic transmission, the 2014 Jeep® Grand Cherokee can attain up to 30 hwy mpg+, drive up to 730 miles on one tank+ and tow up to 7,400 pounds+ to fulfill whatever your nomadic heart desires.

V6 engine: better by far

The 3.6L Pentastar® V6 engine features an extraordinary, new 8-speed automatic transmission, with paddle-shift steering wheel controls. The new Eco mode improves fuel efficiency by 9% and allows the Grand Cherokee to tow up to 6,200 pounds+ when properly equipped with the Tow Group.

Best-in-class V8 towing+

The legendary 5.7L V8 engine Fuel Saving Technology boasts 360 hp and 390 lbs-ft of torque and provides a Best-in-Class 7,400 lb towing capacity+. The enhanced, new 8-speed automatic transmission, and Eco Mode help improve fuel efficiency by up to 10% vs. last year's vehicle.

Smooth and efficient eight-speed transmission.

The exceptional, new eight-speed automatic transmission provides better fuel economy+, quicker acceleration and smoother shifting. It also allows for a 45% improved crawl ratio of 44.1:1 to aid in off-roading (when equipped with a two-speed transfer case).

FEATURE AVAILABILITY

Experience more - confidently

Legendary Jeep® vehicle capability improves with the Selec-Terrain® traction control system, giving you five modes to tackle any condition, including snow. Traction is also enhanced through the available Quadra-Trac II® or Quadra-Drive® II 4WD systems. As well, the Jeep Quadra-Lift® air suspension features five height settings for optimum ride performance.

Safety & Security

Blind spot monitoring+

Blind Spot monitoring+ (BSM) uses dual radar sensors to monitor driver blind spots. Drivers will hear a chime and see an illuminated icon on the mirrors when the BSM detects a vehicle that is
too close.

FEATURE AVAILABILITY EXPLORE SAFETY & SECURITY

Help is at hand

The new emergency 911 Call button summons help immediately – while another button offers immediate access to roadside assistance, Uconnect® or Customer Service.

FEATURE AVAILABILITY

  • Forward Collision Warning+

    The Forward Collision Warning+ with crash mitigation uses radar sensors that detect when your vehicle may be approaching another too rapidly, alerting you with an audible chime.

  • Full protection

    The 2014 Jeep® Grand Cherokee features over 70 available Safety & Security features including side curtain airbags+.

  • ParkView® Back Up Camera+

    The new ParkView® Back Up Camera+ with ParkSense® Rear Park Assist System displays a rearview video image with dynamic distance grid lines when in reverse.
