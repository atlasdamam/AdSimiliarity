Product Details

When the flu, colds, and other illnesses are on the rise, its important to have Clorox® Disinfecting Wipes on hand to keep that office as sanitized as possible. These premoistened wipes kill 99.9% of germs including viruses that cause colds and flu. They clean and disinfect a variety of surfaces, including countertops, wood, mirrors and glass, tile, appliance exteriors and faucets. Each wipe is pretreated with powerful Clorox® disinfectant for easy, fast cleaning. These wipes are great for hard to clean places, and are a simple way to clean high use, shared equipment, like doorknobs, desktops, keyboards and phones. Wipes tear smoothly for easy use, and the convenient dispenser is well-placed in the kitchen, bathroom or reception areas, as well as on office desks. Without any bleach or phosphorous in the ingredients list, they go easy on hands. From cleaning up after a spill to guarding against illness, Clorox® Disinfecting Wipes are an essential tool for any office to stock, all at a reasonable price.
