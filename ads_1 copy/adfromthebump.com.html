Description



Born Free's Classic Bottle's are the ideal bottle system for both breast feeding and formula feeding. It lets baby control milk flow for an experience closer to breastfeeding. The ActiveFlow Venting Technology is clinically designed to reduce colic and gas. It is hassle free, with fewer parts to clean and maintain.

Features

  • Natural Milk Flow, controlled by baby
  • Naturally Fuss free, ActiveFlow venting technology clinically designed to reduce colic symptoms
  • Naturally Safe, free from BPA and PVC
  • Premium, durable material that is ideal for sterilization. Dishwasher safe.
  • Includes Level 2 nipples

I had 3 bottles in my cabinet that had never been used and have been having a lot less bubbles so it does seem that the ripping vents cause the bottles to bubble. I do not want a bottle that has parts that only last for 4 months...if your smart you will go with Avent or Dr. Brown's. I will be investing in new bottles.

              Comments about Summer Infant Born Free Classic Bottle 9 oz.- 3 pack:

              We first bought these bottles back in 2008, because they were some of the first available that were BPA-free. With another baby, we rebought more bottles again, because their website suggests buying new nipples, collars, and vents if you've stored them away in a place where these things could crack or mess up (which we did--in the garage in our humid summers). Well, seeing how many of these parts are no longer available at the stores or online, and with Babies R'Us running a 3 pack for [$] sale, we just decided to rebuy these bottles and some newborn nipples. The quality was excellent back in 2008 and remains excellent now. Our bottles do not leak (which the website suggests that the collar could be screwed on too tightly if the bottle does leak). The only complaint I have is that, again, if you need replacement parts, you will be currently hard-pressed to find them. Also we were lucky to still have our sterilizer from 2008, whereas they are not going to release a new sterilizer until fall of this year. But if you are not worried about that and have other methods of sterilization, I would still recommend these bottles.

              Bottom Line Yes, I would recommend this to a friend
