lust for life x blaque market

Posted on April 18, 2013 by Olivia

Photos by Jesse Fiorino. Hair by Anna Lee. Make Up by Claire Marshall.

 My design collaboration with Blaque Market is finally here! I cant believe its been a year since I began conceptualizing what my first capsule collection would be like. We were on set for the Blaque Label job I was styling at Milk Studios when John from Blaque Market asked if I would ever be interested in designing my own collection. Fast forward to the mood boards, sketching, fabric and swatch selecting, pattern-making and sample productionand the very edited 6 piece collection has finally come into fruition.

I wanted my first design collaboration to be a mixture of the rock and roll roots of this blog, which I began as a 14 year old obsessed with Iggy Pop and muscle cars, intertwined with the evolution of growing up online and stumbling into the world of apparel, while still drawn to wearability. Im also so grateful to have my nearest and dearest work with me on this shoot. Not only is my glam squad so insanely talented and successful in their individual right, but Im so lucky to have them as great friends. This collection is not exactly for the faint of heart, but my journey through Lust for Life has been much more about living on the edge anyway. I hope you guys enjoy the collection. All the pieces will soon be exclusively available on Miss KL!
