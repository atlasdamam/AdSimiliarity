    Flowers and Plants

    As cold weather becomes a memory and a season of growth and new life is upon us, FTD is ready to help you celebrate in any way you like. Spring is the perfect time of year to enjoy colorful blooms, and to share your joy by sending spring flowers to friends and loved ones. Nothing says spring like gorgeous flower arrangements from FTD, so explore our variety of floral gifts and know that you'll be sending a message of true happiness.

    Sending fresh flowers is a perfect way to express feelings of renewal and positivity. Featuring only the freshest blooms, FTD's flower bouquets are filled with beautiful tulips, elegant lilies, and vibrant roses. Trust our same-day flower delivery experts to ensure your flowers arrive exactly when you need them, no matter what the occasion.

    Of course, spring celebrations come in many forms, and we're here to assist you with all of them. Let FTD help you celebrate birthdays, anniversaries and weddings this season. And although not every occasion is a happy one, we always believe in the power of flowers our funeral flowers help express your sympathy and show how much you care.

    FTD is more than flowers, as well - we also offer a wide variety of spring gifts and gift baskets, filled with fresh fruits, delicious cookies and candy, gourmet coffee, and fine wine. Explore our Sweet Shop, featuring our delectable chocolate covered strawberries. And if you need a gift for a truly special occasion, the FTD Jewelry Collection has stunning earrings, necklaces, diamonds, and much more.

    Let FTD's friendly and knowledgeable staff help give you and your recipients a fun and stress-free spring. Your order will be hand-delivered by one of our professional FTD florists, so shop with us today we're your one-stop shop for fresh spring flowers and gifts!
