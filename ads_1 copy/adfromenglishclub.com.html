I felt sorry for myself because I had no shoes, until I met a man who had no feet.

Possible interpretation: Before complaining, we should think of the context.

Origin: Jewish proverb

Quick Quiz:

According to the saying, I felt sorry for myself
  1. before I met a man who had no feet
  2. since I met a man who had no feet
  3. after I met a man who had no feet
