Discover Berlin from 499 kr* - fly with Lufthansa non-stop
 

Discover Berlin - the German capital city and one of the most exciting metropoles in Europe. Enjoy the variety and visit one of the numerous markets, sights or explore your favourite "Kiez".

As a result of the delay in opening Berlin Brandenburg Airport, your flights to Berlin will land at Berlin Tegel (TXL) on an interim basis. Should there be changes to the flight times we will let you know in good time.

Now there are even more reasons to visit Berlin: the Berlin 'be local' benefits that Lufthansa and its local partners are offering you only by showing your boarding pass.
