The Saturday MBA at the Brennan School of Business

Our Saturday MBA program is a fully accredited degree in a convenient, accelerated format. Gain the business, management and leadership skills to advance your career.
  • 18-courses over 21 months—each offered in intensive, 8 week sessions
  • Classes meet alternating Saturdays with online coursework in between
  • Inclusive pricing covers all tuition, fees, and textbooks, plus a laptop computer and on-campus meals

The Brennan School's Saturday MBA allows you to balance your current career, personal life and graduate studies.
