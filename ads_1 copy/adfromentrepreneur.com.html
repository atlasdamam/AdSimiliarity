    Share More, Save More

    The Share Everything Plan for Small Business empowers you to make the most of your companys devices. And with shared data, unlimited talk and unlimited messaging on the Share Everything Plan, youll be able to stay in communication around the clock.

    Share Everything Plan for Small Business

    Creating your plan is simple. Add the line access for each device on your account to the account access for your desired data pool.

    For example:
    An account with four smartphones, two basic phones, two tablets and two notebooks sharing a monthly allowance of unlimited talk, unlimited text and 20 GB of data would only cost $430 per month, not including taxes and surcharges.

    The Share Everything Plan for Small Business includes:

    • Unlimited voice minutes, with no domestic roaming or long-distance charges. (Voice and Data Options only.)
    • Unlimited text, picture and video messaging. (Voice and Data Options only.)
    • A shareable pool of data for up to 10 or
      25 devices.
    • Mobile Hotspot and Mobile Broadband Connect on all capable devices.1 Connect your device and share your data allowance with multiple Wi-Fienabled devices.
    • Access to personal email2 and corporate email (using Microsoft Exchange ActiveSync or IBM Lotus Notes Traveler), if supported by your device.3 Plus get access to server-based Corporate Email that connects using BlackBerry Enterprise Server, Good for Enterprise or a similar secure connection for an additional $15 per month per line.
     

    The Canada and Mexico add-on plans4 give you a pool of shared voice minutes for:

    • Long distance from the U.S. to Canada/Mexico.5
    • Calls while in Canada/Mexico to numbers in
      the U.S., Mexico or Canada (coverage not
      available everywhere).
    • Data usage in Canada is $0.002/KB ($2.05/MB) and in Mexico is $0.005/KB ($5.12/MB).
    • Text messages while in Mexico are $0.50 per message sent (per recipient) and $0.05 per message received.
    • Multimedia messages while in Mexico are billed the same as when youre in the U.S., plus global data charges. See our global data packages.
