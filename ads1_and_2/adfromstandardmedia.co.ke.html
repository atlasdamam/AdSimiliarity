About AIBUMA

AFRICAN INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS AND MANAGEMENT (AIBUMA) CONFERENCE

The AIBUMA conference aims at providing a forum for disciplinary and interdisciplinary communications, where researchers (in Social and Natural Sciences, as well as in Engineering), intellectuals, policy/decision makers and Consultants would share the results of their research, studies and thoughts, with regards to societies and private/public organizations.

This is an annual event that began in 2010 and is organized and hosted by the School of Business, University of Nairobi.

The conference seeks to;

•  Showcase research as well as provide a forum for collaboration and fusion with business leadership practice.

•  Plug into the global business thinking by engaging in activities that elevate her visibility in the global arena.

The overriding theme for this year's conference is:

"Business and Management Outlook : Optimism or Pessimism?"

In line with the above desired outcomes, the School of Business , University of Nairobi intends to hold the fourth African International Business and Management [AIBUMA] Conference between 11th and 12th July 2013 at the The University of Nairobi, School of Business, Lower Kabete Campus.
