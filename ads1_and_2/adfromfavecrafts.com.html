2013 MDX Features
LEARN MORE
Life goes with you
The TSX Sport Wagon wasn’t just inspired by a sports car, but it was also created from one. Sleek lines, a 2.4-liter VTEC® engine and a sport-tuned suspension contribute to its already impressive performance credentials. Impressive fuel efficiency and 66.2 cubic feet of cargo space highlight its more practical side.
30-HWY MPG Highway Rating1, 201-HP Engine
The TSX Sport Wagon’s impressive performance is a direct result of its 2.4-liter i-VTEC® engine-the same engine you’ll find in its sports sedan counterpart. Performance also comes in the form of the available 10-speaker Elliot Scheiner-tuned Acura/ELS Surround® Premium Audio System with Song By Voice® technology.
Safety
The TSX Sport Wagon’s Advanced Compatibility Engineering™ (ACE™) body structure doesn’t simply absorb crash energy. Using a connected network of structural elements, it also helps redirect frontal-crash energy around the passenger compartment. In addition, active safety technology like Vehicle Stability Assist™ (VSA®), Brake Assist and Electronic Brake Distribution (EBD) are designed to help keep you out of harm’s way.
Cargo Capacity
With its generous cargo space, the TSX Sport Wagon is the ideal vehicle for everything from a weekend adventure to a cross-country road trip. Open the tailgate and you’ll discover a spacious cargo area with a flat floor, carpeted finish, and tie-down hooks. Behind the rear seats you’ll find 31.5 cu ft of cargo space. Fold them down flat and the area expands to a more than ample 66.2 cu ft of cargo space.
Available Power Tailgate
Power isn’t simply something you’ll find under the TSX Sport Wagon’s hood. Ideal for cargo-filled hands, the available remote-linked power-operated tailgate can be opened and closed from a single button on your remote control or from another button near the driver’s side armrest.
A passionate performer
The TSX offers exhilaration at every turn, thanks to its tenacious grip and engine options that include a lightweight 201-hp in-line 4 or a more potent 280-hp V-6. Combine that with Bluetooth®5 HandsFreeLink® and the available Acura Navigation System3 and you’ll be extremely well-connected to the road, and the world beyond it.
Acura/ELS Surround® Premium Audio System
Grammy® award-winning recording engineer and producer Elliot Scheiner helped design the available Acura/ELS Surround® 415-watt Premium Audio System 10-speaker audio system8 to reproduce music the way it is heard in the recording studio.
Active Sound Control
Active sound control on TSX V-6 is an inventive way to use the audio system to reduce engine noise inside the cabin. Since some engine noise is welcome in a performance vehicle, the system is linked to the throttle position and engine’s rpm to tune the V-6’s sound, based on how it’s being used. The result is remarkably quiet cruising with a satisfying growl upon acceleration.
Song By Voice®
Acura’s available Song By Voice® feature uses the Voice Recognition system to help the TSX driver find music from the hard disk drive or a USB4-connected iPod®5 simply by voicing a preference.
Aggression in its Most Elegant Form
The TL exudes speed from every angle. But look beyond its aggressive styling and you'll find a sport sedan with serious performance attributes. Like a 6-speed automatic with Sequential SportShift paddle shifters transmission mated to one of two available V-6 engines. Not to mention the available Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™system.
Acura/ELS Surround® Premium Audio System
Grammy® award-winning recording engineer and producer Elliot Scheiner helped design the TL's available Acura/ELS Surround® Premium Audio System8 to reproduce music as it's heard in the recording studio. Most functions of the 440-watt system can be accessed with a word or two, thanks to Acura's innovative voice-recognition technology.
29-mpg Hwy Rating
Thanks in part to the quick-shifting, fuel-efficient 6-speed automatic transmission, the 2013 TL is rated at an impressive 29-mpg hwy7. A rating made even more impressive when you take into account the TL's high-performance 280-hp V-6 VTEC® engine.
Refined exhilaration
The RL pairs an uncompromising 300-hp V-6 engine with breakthrough technologies, like the world’s first noise-reducing wheels and a quick-accelerating 6-speed automatic transmission. Meanwhile, its lavishly appointed cabin surrounds you with all the comforts you’d expect from a world-class luxury automobile.
Noise-Reducing Wheels
The RL now features exclusive Noise-Reducing Wheels - something no other vehicle has. Utilizing specially designed chambers that absorb tire and road noise, this revolutionary technology contributes significantly to the RL’s supremely quiet cabin environment.
Active Headlights
To help you see farther into a turn at night, the available Active Front Lighting System can rotate the headlights up to 20 degrees in the direction you’re turning, and light areas normally left in the dark.
Acura Navigation System
The RL’s available Acura Navigation System4 features continuously updated traffic and weather forecast information via AcuraLink as well as a complete database of Zagat® restaurant reviews. With the help of voice-recognition technology, a simple word or two can control these and many other of the RL’s sophisticated features.
The driver’s SUV
The MDX developed its commanding on-road presence at Germany’s famed Nürburgring test track. It is perfectly complemented by a luxurious 7-seat interior, which features technologies like an available Acura/ELS Surround® Premium Audio System8 and an available Acura DVD Entertainment System for rear-seat passengers3.
Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™ (SH-AWD®)
Though it has the same all-weather attributes of conventional all-wheel drive, Acura developed its revolutionary Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™ (SH-AWD®) system to enhance high-performance dry-road handling. By continuously directing engine power to match weight distribution, available traction, and other conditions, SH-AWD provides seamless power transitions and supreme agility.
3.7-liter VTEC V-6 Engine
The MDX features Acura’s largest engine, which offers a remarkable combination of power, efficiency and refinement. The 3.7-liter VTEC® V-6 delivers 300 horsepower and 270 lbs.-ft. of torque. EPA estimated mileage is 16 city / 21 hwy / 18 combined15.
Keyless Access System
The Keyless Access System with smart entry and pushbutton ignition allows you to walk up to the RDX, unlock the doors, have the car set your preferences and start the engine without ever having to take the key out of your pocket.
Available Voice Recognition
The voice recognition feature that accompanies the available Acura Navigation System allows you to perform the most frequently used functions of the audio, climate control, and navigation systems without ever taking your hands off the wheel.
Available Navigation Functionality & Controls3
The centerpiece of the Technology Package, the Acura Navigation System, in conjunction with the AcuraLink® Satellite Communication System, is perhaps the best driving companion since the invention of the car radio.
Body Structure Design
Acura’s Advanced Compatibility EngineeringTM (ACETM) body structure helps enhance occupant protection and crash compatibility in frontal collisions.16
Acura ELS Surround® Premium Audio System
Designed in part by GRAMMY® Award-winning producer Elliot Scheiner, the available Acura ELS Surround® Premium Audio System8 gives music the kind of clarity you would hear if you were in the studio as it was being recorded. Most functions of the 435-watt, 10-speaker system can be accessed through Acura’s innovative voice-recognition technology.
Lane Departure Warning (LDW)
Safety is a big part of luxury, which is precisely why the ZDX comes with Lane Departure Warning (LDW). Sensing your vehicle is leaving its lane without indicating with a turn signal, the system warns you as you begin to drift. The ZDX also comes with Forward Collision Warning (FCW) to alert the driver if a potential collision risk exists. Peace of mind, standard.
Blind Spot Information System
For added convenience, the ZDX is available with blind spot information system that uses sensors to scan adjacent lanes. If it detects a vehicle that might be out of the driver’s field of vision, indicators located next to the side-view mirrors light up to alert the driver14.
ILX | The luxury of efficiency
The 2.0-liter, 5-speed automatic is tailored for the city, delivering an incomparably smooth ride. The Sequential SportShift paddle shifters provide race-inspired control over shift-points. With luxuries like a Keyless Access System with smart entry, pushbutton ignition, SMS text message function and available voice recognition for climate, audio and navigation controls and you have a luxury vehicle that’s helping forge an entirely new class of luxury vehicles.
ILX 6MT with Premium Package | The luxury of power
The feeling behind the wheel of the 201-hp ILX with its close-ratio 6-speed manual transmission is among the most unique of any luxury vehicle. The 2.4-liter, DOHC i-VTEC® 4-cylinder engine is not to be taken lightly, but thanks to Acura’s distinctively smooth gearbox, it can be. The DOHC i-VTEC® engine is built to deliver superior top-end performance while optimizing efficiency at lower RPMs. Those features, combined with our unique Amplitude-Reactive Dampers, make the 6-speed ILX a luxury vehicle unlike any luxury vehicle you’ve ever known.
ILX Hybrid | The luxury of technology
Featuring Acura’s first engine combined with a powerful electric motor, the ILX Hybrid offers an impressive 39 city/38 highway/38 combined mpg.2 As the only vehicle in its class to offer a Continuously Variable Transmission with paddle shifters, available HDD media storage with Song By Voice® and AcuraLink Real-Time Traffic and WeatherTM4, the ILX Hybrid is a true hybrid of economy and luxury.
Jewel Eye™ LED Headlights
More than just a design statement, the RLX's stunning new headlights are brighter than traditional halogen or HID lamps. Designed to reproduce the spectrum of sunlight, they show colors more vividly, helping to make potential obstacles easier to spot.
AcuraLink®
From Pandora®17 compatibility to your own customizable Aha™18 cloud-based content, available AcuraLink19 lets you personalize your information and media options like never before. New Connect Services include Automatic Collision Notification, Enhanced Roadside Assistance and a new local search feature. AcuraLink also offers an available 24/7 live concierge service.
Precision All-Wheel Steer™ (P-AWS)
An industry-first technology that can adjust the steering angle of each rear wheel independently, giving the driver unprecedented control during high-speed turns and hard braking, and more precise maneuverability at lower speeds.
Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS)20
Building on the standard Lane Departure Warning system, this available system tracks lane markings and can actively help keep the vehicle centered in the lane it’s currently traveling in.
