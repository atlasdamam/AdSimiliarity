Handmade interior

Practically every inch of the Flying Spur interior is handmade at the factory in Crewe, England. From the mirror-matched veneers to the organ-stop style chrome air vents, almost every detail requires the attention of an experienced Bentley craftsman. Learn more about the work they do.

MULLINER SPECIFICATION

As the coachbuilder of choice for Britain’s discerning motorists in the beginning of the 20th century, Mulliner craftsmanship soon became legendary. Today, the Flying Spur W12 Mulliner continues this proud tradition of handcrafted excellence. Drive digital

A front touchscreen system controls the audio, telephone, ride and navigation systems, while rear seat passengers can access many of these settings with the integrated Touch Screen Remote. Options include the Naim for Bentley audio system with 11 separate Balance Mode Radiator speakers, which together with surround sound logic, delivers audiophile sound, wherever you sit in the car.

The best seats in the house

Rear seat options include ventilation and massage to complement the seat heating function. With the Multimedia Specification, the rear also has two 10” LCD screens and two DVD players with USB ports, HDMI connections and SD card slots. An integrated Wi-Fi hotspot allows connection to the internet, for web browsing and email.
