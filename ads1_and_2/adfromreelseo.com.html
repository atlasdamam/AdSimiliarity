YOUR IMPRESSIONS SHOULD
BE QUALITY IMPRESSIONS

Unfortunately, they are not. That’s because most ad placements by video ad networks boil down to site lists. But at YuMe, we believe that what really matters is the quality of video ad placements on those sites and apps. To address this, we’ve developed our Placement Quality Index, or PQI. For brand advertisers and agencies, this index ensures that brand video ads get placed with the most relevant, brand-safe and highest-quality content to achieve strong audience engagement. For publishers, a high PQI rank means they can attract the best-fit premium brand advertisers and therefore maximize their CPMs. It’s smarter thinking resulting in video advertising that performs better for everyone.

How Does PQI Work?

At its core, YuMe's PQI uses a proprietary ad campaign placement algorithm to optimize video advertising performance across our Connected Audience Network. In real time, the algorithm continually analyzes metrics such as interaction rate, video completion rate, player size, player location, content relevancy, brand-safety measures, campaign objectives and much more. Based on the results, ads are placed on the right screen, site, or app in an optimal publisher content context, at the perfect time, to be seen by the most brand-receptive audience—thereby greatly improving the brand performance of each campaign.

 

 

 

Smarter Video Advertising For Smarter Advertisers.

Smart advertisers want to get the highest quality impressions for the highest brand impact. YuMe’s PQI delivers just that, and it does so automatically. With PQI technology, video ads are placed across our Connected Audience Network and across an array of screens with optimal brand engagement always in mind—and it works. In a recent YuMe study, a PQI-enabled campaign realized a 90-percent lift in completion rate and an incredible 215-percent rise in interaction rate. That’s real brand impact.

 

 

 

With PQI, Publishers Win, Too.

What does YuMe’s PQI do for publishers? Quite a bit. With a higher PQI rating, publishers automatically attract more fill from name-brand advertisers that fit best into publishers’ individual video properties. And since PQI is a premium feature for advertisers, publishers earn higher CPMs! Everybody wins with PQI. Learn more about the publisher benefits of higher quality ad placements in our PQI for publishers whitepaper.
