Convenience Around Every Corner

Traverse was redesigned from the rubber to the roof. With a beautiful exterior and a spacious interior, the 2013 Traverse offers you the ability to set yourself apart with style, while allowing you to carry everything you need thanks to class-leading maximum cargo space. A standard rear vision back-up camera and available Side Blind Zone Alert help you stay aware of your surroundings. Step into this reimagined masterpiece, and enjoy getting to your destination.

Passenger Comfort

We made sure to think of the small details, like adding up to 12 cup holders and multiple storage units throughout the cabin to make juggling keys, coffee, and kids a little easier. But we didnt forget the big details, like large door handles that are easy for every passenger to grab, child and adult alike. Big or little, youre sure to enjoy your Traverse in comfort.

Redesigned with You and Yours in Mind

Traverse was redesigned to elevate interior styling and offer new levels of comfort for drivers and passengers alike. Refined materials and additional seating space give your Traverse a comfortable interior, letting you feel more at home when on the road. Available ambient lighting casts a cozy glow throughout the cabin for nighttime driving. A new standard 6.5" diagonal color touch-screen radio with a rear vision camera helps you spot obstacles you might otherwise miss. Available heated and cooled seats for driver and front passenger keep you comfortable while youre getting there.

Cargo Space

Traverse has the most cargo space in its class: an incredible 116.3 cubic feet. Thats about 335 footballs. Available split-folding rear seats give you even more versatile cargo options, and are perfect for adapting on the fly. Traverse also boasts best-in-class space behind the third row, so youll never struggle finding room for any trip.

Full-Size Third-Row Seating

Traverse has room for all aspects of your life: from carpooling and longer trips, to a night out with friends. Designed with comfort for every passenger in mind, Traverse features a third row thats even comfortable for adults. Smart Slide second-row seating makes this space simple to access just slide the seat out of the way with one hand, and enjoy the convenience of a full-size third row.

    Go Far With Every Fill-Up

    Enjoy fewer trips to the pump with an EPA-estimated 17 MPG city/24 highway on FWD models, giving you a range of up to 520 highway miles per tank. Whats more, the standard 3.6L V6 engine with Direct Injection in LS and LT delivers 281 horsepower, while LTZ delivers 288 horsepower. That's enough get-up-and-go to merge with confidence in highway traffic.

    Power and Grace

    Traverse gives you the power to carry up to 8 people or haul the maximum amount of cargo in its class, and do so efficiently. The Direct Injection 3.6L V6 engine with Direct Injection and standard 6-speed automatic transmission provides smooth shifting and effortless, quiet acceleration while contributing to impressive highway fuel economyan EPA-estimated 24 MPG.

    Surround Yourself With Safety

    The 2013 Chevrolet Traverse is designed to help you avoid crashes and protect you in the event a collision happens. From the StabiliTrak Electronic Stability Control System, to the six standard air bags, to OnStar Automatic Crash Response, were with you every step of the way. So whether its before, during or after, we have your back.

    Before

    Whether youre cruising along the highway or navigating bad weather, Traverse has technology like StabiliTrak Electronic Stability Control System with Traction Control thats designed to improve vehicle stability on most road surfaces, especially during emergency maneuvers. The standard rear vision back-up camera helps alert you to stationary objects behind your Traverse, while the available integrated blind spot mirrors enhance visibility on both sides of the vehicle. Avoiding a crash is key, and Traverse helps you keep an eye out for things, and keep in control.

    During

    To help protect you and your passengers, every Traverse comes standard with six air bags. Traverse helps protect you with dual-stage frontal air bags, driver and front-seat passenger seat-mounted side-impact air bags, and head-curtain side-impact air bags for outboard occupants all three rows. Safety meets innovation in the newly available class-exclusive driver seat-mounted front center air bag, which helps protect both the driver and front passenger from colliding with each other in side-impact crashes. This coupled with an optional 7th airbag, sets Traverse ahead of the competition. When it comes to safety, Traverse comes prepared.

    After

    Traverse never stops working to protect you, even after an accident, with the OnStar Directions & Connections Plan (standard for the first six months) and Automatic Crash Response. In a collision, an OnStar Advisor is immediately connected to your vehicle and can request that emergency help be sent to your exact GPS location, even if you cant respond.

    How Sustainable is the Traverse?

    Check out its Ecologic Features. Here at Chevrolet, we believe in being a responsible steward of our environment. Recently, weve developed a new way to communicate some of the environmentally significant features of each vehicle we make. We call it Ecologic.

    Every aspect of Ecologic is audited by Two Tomorrows, an independent third party sustainability auditor. To learn more about Ecologic and the Two Tomorrows process, click here.

    Fuel-Saving Technologies

    With Direct Injection, fuel is injected directly into the combustion chamber in one of two ways; either before air flows into the cylinder or at the same time, resulting in efficient power generation and fuel consumption.

    Low-mass materials, such as an aluminum hood, plastic intake manifold, aluminum control arms and a magnesium instrument panel support beam, reduce engine workload and contribute to better fuel economy. Low rolling resistance tires reduce friction without compromising traction. Reduced friction lessens the energy needed to keep the vehicle moving, which improves fuel economy.

    The engine has been factory-filled with engine oil meeting GM's new dexos specification, which has improved viscosity characteristics as compared to the previous GF-4 oil, contributing to better fuel economy.

    Responsible Manufacturing

    The assembly facility is LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) Gold certified. This facility has met performance metrics in energy and water efficiency, CO2 emissions reduction, improved indoor environmental quality and stewardship of resources. The Lansing Delta Township, MI, assembly facility and Warren, MI, transmission facility feature designated habitat areas certified by the Wildlife Habitat Council, which provide ecological benefits.

    Both the engine and transmission facilities are landfill-free. These facilities use a process to reuse, recycle or convert to energy all waste created in their daily operations, which conserves resources.

    End-of-Life Recyclability

    After the vehicles useful life, 85% of it is recyclable. The majority of its components can be broken down and recycled, which conserves resources and reduces landfill deposits.
