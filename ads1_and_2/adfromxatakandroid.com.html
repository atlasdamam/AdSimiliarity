Mobile Development Bachelor's Degree - Online

Build and create for Android devices.

If you have a passion for software development and the possibilities within the world of Android devices, Full Sail University’s online Mobile Development bachelor’s degree program can teach you the skills you’ll need to bring your apps, games, and utilities to life. From usability and design principles, to software development and programming languages, you’ll learn the fundamental tools needed to begin a path in the growing Android market.
Specialized courses include:
  • Android Application Deployment
  • iOS Application Deployment
  • Integrated Product Development
  • Mobile Business and Marketing
  • Mobile Game Design
  • Mobile Interfaces and Usability
