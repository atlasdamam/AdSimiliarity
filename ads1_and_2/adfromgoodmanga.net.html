    Save the Date Magnets from Wedding Paper Divas


    There is no better way to have your guests keep your save the date front and center than with a save the date magnet. Our custom magnets are made to attach to a surface such as a refrigerator, so every time someone opens the fridge door, they will notice your save the date magnet and remember your special day. With their chic layouts and distinguished design, it's guaranteed that your wedding save the date magnets will stick to everyone's mind.

    Whether your taste is opulent and old fashioned or magnificently modern, there are sure to be wedding save the date magnets that appeal to your personality. If you're looking for something that exudes elegance, we have designs that are decorated with gorgeous floral accents and sophisticated script. Or maybe you're having a destination wedding in paradise. Choose a save the date magnet that is embellished with breathtaking beaches and pretty palm trees. In addition to the various styles of these magnetic reminders, you can also personalize each one with a photo that symbolizes your wedding such as a couples photo. And if you're not sure what photo would work the best on your save the date magnet, or need etiquette and style tips, please do not hesitate to contact one of our designers!

    Many of our customers order save the date magnets about 6 to 12 months before the wedding to give your guests plenty of time to book a hotel and make travel arrangements if necessary. It also allows them to check their calendars to confirm that there is nothing conflicting on that day. And to ensure that your guests don't get mixed up or lose track of your wedding, add a mini calendar or display the date predominantly on the magnet. These small save the date ideas guarantee that nobody will miss your big day.

    At Wedding Paper Divas, we know your wedding is important so we make sure that the save the date magnets you purchase from us are the highest in quality. But their high quality doesn't come at a higher cost. Each of our save the date magnets for weddings are very affordable and will leave you with plenty of extra money for the formal invitations.

    Our save the dates magnets are modern and unique and have a strong magnet that will ensure your reminder sticks on your guest's refrigerator. And with such a practical save the date, it will be impossible for anyone to forget your special day.
