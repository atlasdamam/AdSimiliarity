Vancouver Island Pet Expo

The Vancouver Island Pet Expo is the largest gathering of pet owners on Vancouver Island. No other consumer pet events attract so many affluent pet owners eager to show all types of fantastic pet products that may not traditionally be found in pet stores. The pet expo promotes responsible pet ownership and helps draw attention to abandoned pets available for adoption.

Come and join us at the Vancouver Island Pet Expo on May 25 & 26, 2013 at Pearkes Recreation Centre in Victoria, BC! The Vancouver Island Pet Expo delivers tons of animal education and entertainment under one roof. The fun-filled family Pet Expo will feature pet related vendors along with some highly unique entertainment.

For everyone's safety (animals included) please leave your pets at home.

The Vancouver Island Pet Expo is an invaluable and exciting opportunity to showcase your company directly to the pet owner enthusiasts! The pet expo is an extraordinary opportunity for brand exposure, attracting new customers, and reinvigorating previous relationships. Be part of the best show of the season! Thousands of pet owners will attend the show. To find out more click one of these links: Exhibit at the Pet Expo or Sponsor the Pet Expo.

Visit our Pet Expo Blog to keep up to date with what's happening with the Pet Expo and sign up for our Pet Expo newsletter for exclusive chances to win great prizes from our exhibitors and passes to the pet expo throughout the year.

The Vancouver Island Pet Expo does not permit the display of any exotic animals, unless the organization is an advocate for the sanctuary of endangered species. We have a strict policy in place that does not allow any spiders at the trade show.

Charity of Choice

We are pleased to have Greater Victoria Animal Crusadors (GVAC) as our Charity of Choice at this years Vancouver Island Pet Expo!

GVAC is a registered, non-profit organization whose main mandate is to provide funding to spay and neuter abandoned and stray cats and dogs in the Greater Victoria Area. They also assist low income families with these costs.
