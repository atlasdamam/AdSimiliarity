WTOP Anywhere

In our continuing effort to keep you in touch wherever you are, WTOP has added a few new mobile features. Below you can find links to sign up to receive text message alerts on your cell phone, a url to get the top stories from our website on your PDA, information about WTOP's podcasts, and a link to the streaming audio for devices that are enabled.

WTOP iPhone App

The Glass-Enclosed Nerve-App is now available in the Apple App Store

WTOP Android App

Glass-Enclosed Nerve App is also available for Android

Mobile Website

Access WTOP on any web enabled mobile device - Just go to wtop.com on your mobile phone.

Text Alerts

Sign up to receive breaking news and weather alerts on your mobile phone. Text the word BREAK to 91035 to sign up instantly. Number of messages will depend on the frequency of breaking news. Msg&data rates may apply. Text HELP for help. Text STOP to stop.

Email Alerts

Get breaking news alerts delivered to your email.

Daily Headlines

The news comes to your in-box. Each weekday WTOP sends out one email with the day's top headlines. It's a great way to get a quick read on what's happening.

WTOP on Facebook

Like WTOP on Facebook and receive news, traffic and weather information on your wall.

Follow WTOP on Twitter

WTOP has a number of Twitter feeds you can follow

Listen on Your Mobile Phone

You can now listen to WTOP on any phone. Just dial (202) 380- 9977.
