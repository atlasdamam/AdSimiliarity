        Welcome to Memory Alpha!

        Memory Alpha is a collaborative project to create the most definitive, accurate, and accessible encyclopedia and reference for everything related to Star Trek. The English-language Memory Alpha started in November 2003, and currently consists of 35,798 articles. If this is your first visit, please read an introduction to Memory Alpha.

        Latest news

        21 May 2013
        Star Trek Online, the massive multiplayer online role-playing game, has released its first expansion, Legacy of Romulus, expanding the in-game universe to allow players to be part of the Romulan faction 30 years after the events of Star Trek Nemesis. [1]
        19 May 2013
        Star Trek Into Darkness has grossed approximately $84.1 million during its four-day opening in North America. Meanwhile, the film has already outgrossed its predecessor in international markets such as Russia, Taiwan, and Mexico. [2]
        12 May 2013
        Star Trek Into Darkness has grossed approximately $31.7 million from the seven countries in which it was released, setting records for the series' international profits. [3]
