Your trading platform: help or hindrance?

Todays advanced trading platforms are supposed to help you as a trader. But often, their complexity makes them hard to learn and use (and even harder to master). So they end up getting in the way of your trading.

Designed for traders like you.

When StreetSmart Edge development began, Schwab designers recognized the frustration traders had with their platforms. Visiting traders in their homes and offices and observing their workflow using the platforms, they asked, What do you need to do now? and How is your platform getting in the way? They then created a platform that combines a deep understanding of traders with design innovation.

A platform that thinks like a trader.

StreetSmart Edge has an intuitive, approachable design that aligns with your trading workflow. Its integrated tools make the process of identifying (and acting) on potential opportunities more logical and efficient. Its flexibility helps it adapt to your style and skill level. And its features work together to help you monitor your orders and positions.

Getting started:

What makes StreetSmart Edge different than other trading platforms?

StreetSmart Edge is different because it was created using a deep understanding of what traders need a trading platform to do. Our platform designers looked at the entire trading experience. They visited and observed traders in their homes and offices. They asked them at every stage of their workflow, "What do you need to do now?" and How is your platform getting in the way? Our designers also listened to client feedback, keeping notes about the tools and features they wanted. Then they created a platform that combined these needs with design innovationa platform that thinks like a trader.

StreetSmart Edge is designed to work the way traders thinkand allow them to focus on their strategies instead of dealing with complex trading tool controls. By aligning with a traders work flow, StreetSmart Edge offers an advanced trading experience that blends sophistication with efficiency.

How much does it cost to use StreetSmart Edge?

StreetSmart Edge is available at no cost to Schwab Active Trading clients. To become a Schwab Active Trader, please call 888-245-6864. At Schwab, youll get great service with no account service fees and with low minimum balance requirements. You can trade equities online for a flat rate of $8.95, trade options online for $8.95 + $0.75 per contract, and trade Schwab ETFs online in your Schwab account with $0 commissions2.

I have a Mac®. Can I use StreetSmart Edge?

Yes. Mac users can run StreetSmart Edge by using a Windows® emulator. A Windows emulator is an installed software program that allows computers using the Mac Operating System (OS) to run Windows-based programs. For more details, go to System Requirements tab.

On the platform:

Can I get real-time balance information and streaming quotes on StreetSmart Edge?

Yes. You can keep track of your gains and losses, and you can monitor your balances in real time. Additionally, you will have access to streaming quotes and real-time charting.

What types of orders can I place on StreetSmart Edge?

You can place market, limit, stop-limit, trailing-stop, and trailing-stop-limit equity orders. You can also add a bracket to your buy order or existing position. Additionally, you can place various types of simple and complex options orders (using an options-approved account).

I trade options. How does StreetSmart Edge support options trading?

StreetSmart Edge offers streaming Level II options quotes along with max gain/max loss and breakeven calculations right in the trading window. Additionally, you can view historical pricing data to help assess time impact. You can also customize the quote function to include option Greeks, midpoint, and more. Plus you can trade basic and multi-leg options with automatic electronic access to all primary listed options exchanges.

What type of charting capabilities does StreetSmart Edge offer?

With StreetSmart Edge, you can create and customize up to 20 real-time charts, each with up to 20 tabs, creating a possible 400 charts to help support your research and strategy needs. You can choose from multiple chart styles, time frames, and technical indicators. You can also synchronize charts by linking them to each other or to other tools.

What is "chart pattern recognition"?

Chart pattern recognition helps support your decision making by automating pattern recognition of more than 60 chart patterns, indicators, and standard oscillators. You can also set up custom screens and email alerts, and monitor real-time technical analysis. While built into the StreetSmart Edge platform, the chart pattern recognition application is provided by Recognia®, a third-party provider of investment research products.

Getting help:

What resources are available to me for assistance with StreetSmart Edge?

A wealth of resources are available to help you get the most out of StreetSmart Edge. In addition to an in-platform "Getting Started" guide and an online user manual, a Learning Center provides more information on the platform, plus technical trading, risk management, and investing strategies.

You will also be able to talk to an Active Trading specialist who can answer your questions via either phone or the Live Help instant-message chat built into the platform. You may also call Schwab anytime, 24/7.
