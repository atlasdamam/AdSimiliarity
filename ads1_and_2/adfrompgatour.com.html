BlackBerry® Z10

RE-INVENTED WITH YOU IN MIND

Keep moving

Introducing the reinvented BlackBerry® - an intelligent and intuitive experience designed with you in mind that constantly adapts to your needs. With features and apps working seamlessly together, the BlackBerry Z10 makes all your connections fast, secure, and tailored to your needs. New features of the BlackBerry Z10 include:
  • The BlackBerry® Hub allows you to peek at your inbox from any app without having to stop what you're doing
  • The smart BlackBerry® Keyboard reconfigures itself based on your typing habits
  • BBM Video with Screen Share* shares content and apps right from your device
  • Time Shift mode lets you create a photo even more perfect than the moment it happened
BLACKBERRY® HUB

Stay close to what's important

You're watching a video or browsing the Web when you see it - the blinking red light urging you to take a look at a new message or email. With the new BlackBerry, you can glance at new notifications but get right back to what you were doing. A swipe of your finger lets you peek into the BlackBerry Hub from any app to check your inbox, then choose to reply or slide back to your previous screen.
BLACKBERRY® KEYBOARD

Fast, accurate, and effortless typing

Always typing an "N" when you meant to type an "M"? The BlackBerry Keyboard can help you with that! It's an adaptable keyboard that remembers how you write, learns your most common words and mistakes, and changes itself to fit your typing style. With the BlackBerry Keyboard, you type faster, more accurately, and with less effort as you respond to emails or stay connected with BBM.
BBM VIDEO WITH SCREEN SHARE

Share what you want real-time

Catch up face-to-face or share what's on your screen
with BBM Video with Screen Share.* It's a new
BBM experience for a new generation of
BlackBerry® smartphones.
TIME SHIFT MODE AND BLACKBERRY® STORY MAKER

The perfect shot, every time

Life isn't perfect, but with the BlackBerry Z10, your photos can be. Using the Time Shift mode lets you take a picture using an ultra-short video, so you can choose the exact moment in time when your subjects look best to create the ideal shot. It lets you select different areas in the same photo, choose the best expressions and poses, and then combine them to create one great shot. Once your photos are the best they can be, use Story Maker to weave them together and make a movie with music to share with the world.

Would you recommend this product to a friend? Yes
Pros: expandable memory to 80gb, smooth operation
Cons: only the black model is available from at&t
"My wife and I each bought one of these phones to replace our aging Torch 9800's. We felt that the Torch 9800's were hard to use and basically were not thrilled with them. Our new Z10's are totally opposite. The new OS takes some getting use to but it is smooth and solid and everything just seems to work. The phone accepts the Sandisk 64 gb micro sdxc card and runs it flawlessly after telling the phone to format the card. This addition puts the phone memory at a total of 80gb, 16 gb more than the most expensive iPhone and for much less money. This allowed us to transfer over 350 albums of music to the memory card from our main computer's iTunes library with over 30 gb of memory to spare! You have to use Blackberry's Link software to transfer the music but it works perfectly. The phone's call quality is excellent and we were able to use the opening 20 seconds, or so, of any of the transferred songs as a ring tone with a few simple choices in the phones setup menu and, with the right app, you can choose the exact portion of any song that you want also. All-in-all we have really found a lot to like about this phone including its display which has a higher pixel density than the much touted Retina Display on the iPhone 5. For our aging eyes, the display is much appreciated and sharp as a tack. We highly recommend this phone!"
