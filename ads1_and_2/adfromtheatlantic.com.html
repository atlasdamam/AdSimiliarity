Ushering in a New Era of Computing for Healthcare

Best known for winning Jeopardy, IBM Watson represents a shift forward in the relationship between people and technology, enabling our world to make better decisions. With the unique ability to process natural language how people actually talk to each other Watson analyzes information from medical journals to doctors notes, generates and recommends hypotheses to clinicians, and learns from experience to improve how we research, teach, practice and pay for healthcare.
