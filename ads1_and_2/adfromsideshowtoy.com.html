Sideshow Collectibles and Hot Toys are proud to present the Roadblock Sixth Scale Collectible figure, the newest addition to Hot Toys' MMS series, from the action packed film G.I. Joe Retaliation. The film-accurate collectible is specially crafted based on the image of Dwayne Jonson as Roadblock, featuring a highly detailed head sculpt, finely tailored costume and an array of weapons

The Roadblock Sixth Scale Figure features:

  • Authentic and detailed fully realized likeness of Dwayne Johnson as Roadblock in his military uniform and armor from G.I. Joe Retaliation
  • Approximately 30 cm tall
  • Muscular body with over 30 points of articulation
  • Three (3) pairs of interchangeable gloved palms including:
  • One (1) pair of fists
  • One (1) pair of palms for holding gun
  • One (1) pair of relaxed palms
  • One (1) military camouflage uniform with tactical armor vest
  • One (1) black t-shirt
  • Two (2) holsters
  • One (1) machine gun
  • One (1) pair of brass knuckles
  • One (1) pair of daggers (attaches to brass knuckles)
  • One (1) knife (attaches to brass knuckles)
  • One (1) baton (attaches to brass knuckles)
  • Two (2) pistols (attaches to brass knuckles)
  • One (1) pistol with silencer
