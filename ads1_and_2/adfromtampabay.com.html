Stravinsky's
The Rite of Spring



Fri, May 24, 2013 at 8:00 PM
Tampa - STRAZ Buy Tickets

Sat, May 25, 2013 at 8:00 PM
St. Petersburg - MAH Buy Tickets



 





Tito Munoz, guest conductor

The concert opens with Messiaen’s symphonic meditation Le offrandes oubliées (The Forgotten Offerings)...followed by the raw energy and breath-taking beauty of nature with the surging seas of sound in Debussy’s La Mer.  The evening concludes with the pagan-intoned orchestral power of Stravinsky’s The Rite of Spring.

Click here to listen to the musicians of The Florida Orchestra discuss Stravinsky's The Rite of Spring on its 100th anniversary.

To read the program notes for this concert, click here.

Pre-Concert Conversation: All evening Masterworks programs offer a pre-concert talk about the music and the composers in the concert hall beginning one hour prior to curtain. 

Seating charts and pricing:
STRAZ: Straz Center for the Performing Arts, Carol Morsani Hall, Tampa
MAH: Progress Energy Center for the Arts, Mahaffey Theater, St. Petersburg
