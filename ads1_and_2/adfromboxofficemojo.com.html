SAVE THE KENNEDY

To all of our loyal customers, family, friends and fellow movie lovers,

For the last 5 years that we have owned the Kennedy.  Your support for our business has been incredible.  It has been an awesome experience for our family. When we took it over in 2007 one of the first things we did was lower the ticket prices to $5 to make it more affordable for more people to enjoy a night out with some of the best popcorn around and immerse themselves in the big screen and enjoy Hollywood’s finest.

I’m sad to say; the ever advancing technology of film and rising costs (on everything else) are suddenly catching up to us and forcing us to try to tackle both in order to stay alive in this industry. This is incredibly troubling in this economy in which everybody is struggling.  I get it…

So before I lay out what we are going to try to do, let me explain where the biggest hurdle (and costs) are coming from.
