Checkout some of Bloomfire's advanced features…

Content Creation Tools
Create a text document, record a webcam video, or record your screen with audio—
all from your Bloomfire. It's quick, it's easy.
No other software required.
File Upload Types
Upload documents, presentations, videos, images, and more. We support just about every file type known to man. Just browse and select any piece of content from your computer or the web, and go.
Content Series
Create a series of posts (like a playlist)
to create a linear learning path. Combine videos, documents, and presentations
of a similar topic in a single Series.
Questions & Answers
Figuring out WHO to ask is half the battle
so just ask everyone at once. Get answers
in all forms, from simple text replies to multimedia demonstrations.
Content Management
Browse content by type, featured posts,
most recent posts, and most popular posts.
Add titles, descriptions, keywords, and categories for advanced search capabilities. No technical expertise needed.
Advanced Search
Each content posts offers the ability to
add a title, description, keywords and categories. When you search, Bloomfire searches these tags, as well as plain text posts and questions.
Content Moderation
Turn on content moderation to review content before it is published. Do the same with questions and answers.
Sub-Communities
Create a sub-community for a team, project, or subject. Publish content across selected sub-communities.
Role-Based Management
Manage Owners, Admins, Authors, and Learners across your community and sub-communities. Each role has designated access rights and privileges.
Email Notifications
Receive email notifications when new
posts are made and when questions are asked and answered. Get a weekly digest
of all of your Bloomfire's activity. Don't
want alerts? Turn them off.
Personalization
Add your logo, customize colors and messaging. Go crazy and dig into the CSS for a seamless branding experience. We even offer 100% white labeling upon request.
Invite People
Invite all of your LinkedIn, Facebook,
Twitter, and Google+ contacts, or simply copy and paste a list of email addresses
and send a personal note inviting them
to your Bloomfire.
Dashboard Analytics
See who is contributing and viewing the most content. Learn which content is most helpful. Analyze which days of the week are best for learning.
Mobile Application
Take your Bloomfire with you wherever you go. Get our free mobile app for iPhone® and iPad®. (Android® coming soon.)
Integrate Bloomfire
Use our simple API to integrate with Salesforce® Chatter.com™, LinkedIn® professional network groups, and
Google Apps™ service.
