Multimedia Content Engine ( Learn More )

Create and upload virtually any type of content.

Imagine being able to upload virtually anything you’ve already created alongside the native tools to create rich text documents, web links, and even HD videos. Now imagine being able to keyword tag, title, and categorize for future recall and display everything in a simple, easy-to-use interface. Bloomfire gives you all of this and more. Learn More.

Social Engagement System ( Learn More )

Connect people who know the answers to the people who need them.

Having a place to store everything isn't enough anymore. The context around that content is where the real value lies: the tribal knowledge within your teams and companies. Getting people to engage is one place where Bloomfire excels. Email integrations, recommendations, social sharing, the ability to ask specific users to answer questions and hi-fives are only a few ways Bloomfire reaches out and influences positive engagement from users. Learn More.

Expertise Beacon ( Learn More )

Leading you to the people around you who have the information you need.

Getting answers to your questions is only half of the problem; finding who to ask is the first battle. With Bloomfire's Expertise Beacon, you can discover subject matter experts and identify new talented team members. Following their contributions is as easy as the click of a button, allowing you to be notified of everything they author or answer. Learn More.

Analytics Dashboard ( Learn More )

Arming you with valuable business data to help you understand how
information is shared in your teams and companies.

Big data is great, but only when it actually helps you make educated business decisions. With Bloomfire's Analytics Dashboard, you can see exactly what kind of content is making an impact with your users, how people are interacting, and what contributions people are finding most useful. With this data, you can inform business processes, optimize operations and justify the ROI of your training and learning programs. Learn More.

Productivity Integrations ( Learn More )

Increasing productivity by integrating with the tools you use most so
you can learn where you work.

Learning happens everywhere- not just 9-5 behind your desk. With Bloomfire's productivity integrations, you can take learning with you everywhere you go- online with your PC, in the field with your iPhone or smartphone, or even offline with our Stash feature. Bloomfire also integrates with Google Apps, LinkedIn, and Salesforce CRM- giving you and your team seamless knowledge sharing where they work. Learn More.

Dynamic Q&A Tool ( Learn More )

Forever solving the problem of questions that get asked over and over and over.

There's a reason FAQs exist. There are questions that get asked repeatedly. And with each time you answer that question, you are serving a 1:1 ask:answer relationship. Think of all the time you'd save if you could answer once and never again. And also imagine if you could answer a question with any method of media: a screencast, a video, an uploaded piece of content, or simple text. Bloomfire's Dynamic Q&A Tool does this and much more. Learn More.

Intelligent Search

Providing quick fingertip access to the knowledge you need, when you need it.

Not all search is equal, and Bloomfire's Intelligent Search is proof. Type-ahead results help you quickly identify the most relevant results. To narrow your results, filter by content type, categories, and keyword tags. Plus, search results have never been clearer. See who contributed a post, asked a question, or answered a question, and when the post was published and how many times it's been viewed.
