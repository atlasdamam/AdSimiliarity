Features of Match.com

Match.com stands out from most other dating sites thanks to its innovative perks. After you sign up, Match.com will direct you to a personality test. The results from this test will then be used as a starting point for the site to begin sending the you matches, according to your answers and preferences. Those who prefer to search for themselves will find a host of unique search features to help them find the match of their dreams.

Your Match.com profile will also allow you to:

  • Send and receive IMs and emails
  • Specify your "Deal Breakers" to narrow down matches
  • Join "Stir", Match.com's exclusive dating events and Happy Hours!
  • See who's interested in you
  • Get full access to advice columns, newsletters and magazine articles, for a truly all-inclusive online dating website.

Signing Up

Because Match.com requires users to complete their personality test, signing up for the site may take a bit of time, but more positive results will be garnered by those who fill in the questionnaire completely. Creating an account is free and users are encouraged to take a look around the site before signing up for the monthly subscription.

Safety and Security

Match.com is a huge site but it makes the safety of its users a top priority. There are multiple ways to report unsavory characters. The site dedicates a large amount of real estate to articles about online dating safety. The privacy policy is fairly broad but promises that users can opt out of certain third party marketing from their account settings page.
