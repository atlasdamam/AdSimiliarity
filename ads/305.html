Flowering Trees

Everyone looks forward to the bright, fragrant flowers of spring that signal the end of winter and the arrival of the warm, sunny months ahead. What better way to welcome the spring season into your yard than with an eye-catching flowering tree?

Select trees that bloom in your favorite color. From vibrant red and classic pink, to pure white and soft purple, there is shade to match any taste. Whether you're looking to work with an existing scheme, or find a showpiece to set the tone for a new one, there is no limit to what you can achieve in your landscape with a beautiful flowering tree.

Plus, our flowering trees have some great advantages that make them even better. Many of these beauties are highly adaptable to various growing conditions, require very little maintenance, and will not be bothered by pests and disease. From the look of these trees, no one would guess you spend so little time on them!

Crape Myrtle– Our most popular flowering tree! White, Pink, Purple, Red – there is a flowering Crape Myrtle for every landscape! In addition to being beautiful, the Crape Myrtle is adaptable to various soil types and disease resistant... also relatively drought tolerant. Beautiful flowers last for up to 4 months!

Flowering Cherry Trees – One of the first flowering trees to bloom in spring! These versatile trees come in a variety of colors and forms, and are hardy and adaptable.

Flowering Dogwood – These famous flowering trees are offered in a selection of stunning colors. Their relatively compact size allows them to find a place in even the smallest of landscapes. Disease resistance and adaptability only add to the value of this beautiful tree.

Royal Empress Tree - The fastest growing flowering trees you can find. Fragrant lavender blooms each spring. Grows up to 18 ft. per year for dense shade. Grows almost anywhere in zones 5-11.

And let's face it: you're not going to get this kind of color and beauty from a few border flowers.
