Efficiency Through Expert Systems
Expert Systems can play a powerful role in optimizing Tech Support. Customer satisfaction goes way up when customers get the right answer quickly from a knowledgeable rep or through online self-service tools. Meanwhile, the organization wants to improve rep efficiency, productivity and recognition. Most Support groups don't have automated expert platforms for workflow, guided troubleshooting for complex products, or expert knowledge delivery and evolution. For many, Search is the only tool for reps and customers to navigate complex technical issues.
Pre-Packaged or Do-It-Yourself
The LogicNets Decision Support Platform is a web-based expert system for delivering technical support services that satisfy these needs. LogicNets provides flexible solutions ranging from comprehensive Expert Support platforms to intelligent point apps that you can design and deploy yourself to solve specific problems.
