Hosting Technology:

iPage is more than just accessibility. They are also dedicated to providing powerful web hosting services, meaning you can rest assured that as an iPage customer, your website will be running at optimal performance. They run their entire system on the highest quality Dell servers, to ensure high speed processing and a fast on-site experience. And, by employing dual OC-48 connections on diverse backbones, you can be certain you have more than enough bandwidth to support even high traffic to your site.

Youll find their custom control panel easy to use and manage. There is also a built in FTP account, which allows for the easy uploading of additional files, images, products, video, audio, and more. As a customer, you will also receive an unlimited number of MySQL databases, which is terrific for using catalogs and other query driven sites.

Pricing Concerns:

Knowing what you get for your money is always an important consideration when deciding to invest in a website solution provider. Most other companies have multiple plans to choose from. These can often be confusing and difficult to decipher, not to mention determine which is the best value (or right for your situation). Not here. iPage.com packs a punch with their one, all-inclusive hosting plan, at one low price! And with seasonal offers, sales and specials, we found their prices to offer extraordinary value for money.

Customer Support:

Whether youre a technophobe or a webhosting geek, iPage understands that all customers deserve top-notch support. In our customer response test, they performed well in response time, friendliness and knowledgeability. In addition to online tutorials, an FAQ section, and a comprehensive knowledgebase, iPage also offers e-mail support, phone support, and live-chat for customers.

But the icing on the cake with iPage customer support is their dedicated Account Manager. Sign up with iPage and expect a call within 2-3 days from your personal Account Manager. Theyre there to walk you through your hosting plan, and help you optimize your settings. Reliability is also a big concern, especially with something as important as your online web presence. Every site includes the legendary iPage 99.9% up-time guarantee.
